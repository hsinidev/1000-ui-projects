Essential Questions

Transparent answers to help you navigate your private villa experience with confidence.

Every reservation begins with a direct consultation. Once your desired dates and villa are confirmed, we issue a secure digital contract. A 50% deposit is required to hold your sanctuary, with the balance due 60 days prior to arrival. All transactions are handled with ultra-secure encryption.

Our villas include a dedicated host and daily housekeeping. Private chefs, chauffeurs, and personal concierge services can be curated to your specific needs. Our staff is trained to provide 'invisible service'—anticipating needs while respecting your complete privacy.

Due to the exclusive nature of our properties, cancellations made outside of 90 days are subject to a 15% administrative fee. Within 90 days, deposits are non-refundable unless the property is re-booked. We strongly recommend our bespoke travel insurance for complete peace of mind.
