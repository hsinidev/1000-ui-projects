Frequently Asked Questions

What are the distinct membership tiers? expand_more
Our club offers three tiers of artisanal engagement:

The Vintage Circle: Curated seasonal selections and digital cellar access.
The Grand Cru: Priority booking for tours, exclusive member vintages, and invitations to regional tastings.
The Sommelier’s Reserve: Bespoke cellar management, private vineyard access, and a dedicated concierge.
How are rare vintages transported? expand_more
Rarity requires protection. All rare vintages are shipped via climate-controlled couriers with vibration-dampening packaging. We monitor the temperature and humidity levels in real-time until the bottle reaches your cellar door. Shipping schedules are tailored to seasonal weather windows to prevent thermal shock.
Managing your bespoke tour bookings expand_more
Bookings for vineyard tours and private tastings can be managed through the Member Portal. We recommend booking at least six weeks in advance for Grand Cru locations. Cancellations must be made 72 hours prior to the event to avoid a sommelier preparation fee.
