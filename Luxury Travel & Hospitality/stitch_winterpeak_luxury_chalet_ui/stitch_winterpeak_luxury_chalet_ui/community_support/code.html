Support Categories

Everything you need for a seamless mountain experience, from chalet access to slope safety protocols.

How do I access my private chalet?

expand_more
Upon arrival, your digital key will be activated within the WinterPeak app. Simply hold your mobile device near the smart lock at your entrance. If you prefer a physical keycard, our concierge at the base lodge can provide one 24/7.

What are the spa hours of operation?

expand_more
The Summit Wellness Spa is open daily from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. We recommend booking treatments at least 48 hours in advance through the 'Amenities' section of this app.

Are mountain guides available for off-piste?

expand_more
Yes, professional certified guides are available. Off-piste tours must be scheduled 24 hours in advance and are subject to daily avalanche risk assessments and weather conditions.
