Frequently Asked Questions

Detailed insights into our heritage service, estate protocols, and guest expectations.

RESERVATIONS

What is the cancellation policy for peak season? expand_more
During our heritage peak seasons, we require a 72-hour notice for cancellations to ensure other distinguished guests have the opportunity to experience the estate. Late cancellations may incur a fee equivalent to one night's stay.
Are private chauffeur services included? expand_more
Complimentary chauffeur service is provided for our Royal Suite guests within a 50-mile radius. For other suites, private transfers can be arranged through the concierge for an additional fee.

SPA & WELLNESS

Do I need to book spa treatments in advance? expand_more
To ensure your preferred therapist and time, we recommend booking at least two weeks prior to your arrival. Walk-in appointments are subject to limited availability.

ESTATE POLICIES

What is the formal dress code for dinner? expand_more
In the Grand Hall, we observe a "Heritage Formal" dress code after 6:00 PM. We kindly request gentlemen to wear jackets and ladies to opt for cocktail or evening attire.
