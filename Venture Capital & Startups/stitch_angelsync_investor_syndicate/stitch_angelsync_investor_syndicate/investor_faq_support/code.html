Membership Tiers

The Sovereign tier offers exclusive access to pre-seed rounds and direct secondary market participation. Associate members receive standard syndicate access, whereas Sovereign members are assigned a dedicated capital strategist and gain early-viewing rights (48-hour lead time) on all institutional-grade offerings.

Investment Process

Once an opportunity is posted, the participation window remains open for 14 business days or until the allocation is fully subscribed. Funding must be cleared through our escrow partners within 72 hours of commitment confirmation.
