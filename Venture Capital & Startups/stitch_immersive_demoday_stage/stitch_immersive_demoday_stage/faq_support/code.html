Frequently Asked Questions

Investor Access

How do I access the Private Lounge?

Private Lounges are reserved for Tier 1 Investors. Once your verification is complete, a gold key icon will appear in your top navigation bar allowing instant access to startup founders.

Voting

What determines my Voting Power?

Voting Power is calculated based on your historical investment activity and platform engagement score. Higher engagement throughout the demo day increases your influence on the leaderboards.

Streaming

My stream is lagging, what should I do?

First, check your bandwidth. We recommend at least 15Mbps for 4K. If issues persist, click the gear icon in the player and switch the 'Latency Mode' to 'Low Latency Optimized'.

Security

Are my investment commitments binding?

DemoDay Live facilitating 'Soft Commitments'. These are expressions of interest. Final legal binding contracts are generated via the 'Resources' tab after the live pitch session concludes.

Can't find what you're looking for?

Our dedicated concierge team is online 24/7 during live events to assist you with any technical or investment-related inquiries.
