How does VentureBase calculate 'Dry Powder' metrics? expand_more
Our 'Dry Powder' calculations utilize a multi-modal approach combining publicly available SEC filings, verified LP reporting, and proprietary machine learning models. We estimate callable capital based on historic fund structures and deployment cycles for over 4,500 active VC firms globally. All data is updated on a 24-hour rolling cycle to ensure maximum accuracy for institutional decision-making.
Can we integrate our internal deal flow CRM with VentureBase? expand_more
Yes. VentureBase supports native integrations with Salesforce, Affinity, and HubSpot. Through our Enterprise API, you can also push VentureBase enrichment data directly into custom proprietary stacks. This ensures your deal flow remains synced with the latest market intelligence.
What is the difference between 'Thesis' and 'Directory' views? expand_more
The Directory is a flat list of entities filtered by geography, sector, or stage. Thesis views are qualitative workspaces where users can define investment hypotheses and the platform identifies companies that match those specific parameters using semantic search and competitive landscape mapping.
