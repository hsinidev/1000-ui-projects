How long does a bespoke AquaVista build typically take?

expand_more
A signature AquaVista project typically spans 12 to 18 weeks from the initial architectural consultation to the final site commissioning. This timeline ensures our artisans can meticulously execute the custom masonry and integrated water-tech systems that define our brand.

What technology is used for water purification?

expand_more
We utilize a proprietary Crystal-Clear™ UV-Ozone filtration system. This medical-grade technology eliminates 99.9% of bacteria while reducing chemical reliance by 80%, providing water that is as gentle on the skin as it is clear to the eye.

Do you offer smart home integration for pool controls?

expand_more
Absolutely. Every AquaVista installation includes our VistaConnect suite, allowing you to manage temperature, ambient lighting, and water features directly from your iOS or Android device, or through voice integration with major smart home ecosystems.

What kind of ongoing maintenance is required?

expand_more
While our systems are designed for low intervention, we recommend a monthly professional check-up. We offer the 'Artisan Care' package, where our technicians handle all diagnostics, ensuring your sanctuary remains in pristine condition year-round.
