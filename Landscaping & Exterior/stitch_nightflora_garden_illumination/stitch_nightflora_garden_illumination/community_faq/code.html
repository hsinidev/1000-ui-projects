Frequently Asked Questions

The technical nuances of luxury illumination, decoded for the refined practitioner.

How do I calibrate the color temperature across fixtures?

expand_more

Ensure all fixtures are connected to the Lumina Pro Bridge. Within the fixture control grid, select 'Global Calibration' to synchronize kelvin levels (2700K - 4000K) with a 2% variance threshold for visual consistency.

Implementing automated circadian rhythm schedules

expand_more

Access the 'Sensor Grid' tab in the side console. Enable 'Astronomical Clocking' to allow the system to adjust intensity and warmth based on local longitudinal sunset data.

Weatherproofing architectural exterior arrays

expand_more

All Lumina Archive fixtures are rated IP67. However, for saline coastal environments, we recommend the 'Maritime Coating' upgrade to prevent anodic oxidation of the charcoal chassis.

Integrating third-party architectural consoles

expand_more

Lumina Pro supports DMX512 and Matter protocols. Export your 'Fixture Map' from the dashboard as a .json file to import into external control systems.
