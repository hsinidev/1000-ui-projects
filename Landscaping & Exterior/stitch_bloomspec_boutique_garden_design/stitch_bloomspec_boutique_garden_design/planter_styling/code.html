THE ART OF CURATION

Planter Styling

Elevate your botanical sanctuary with our curated approach to container garden aesthetics. From romantic overflows to architectural precision.

COLLECTION 01

Modern Minimalist

Focus on silhouette and architectural integrity. Use monochromatic vessels and structural foliage to create a focal point of quiet strength.

STYLING TIPS

  • — Repeat silhouettes for visual rhythm.
  • — Limit your palette to three tonal shades.
  • — Use scale to ground larger architectural spaces.

COLLECTION 02

English Cottage

A whimsical celebration of abundance. This styling method favors the "Thriller, Filler, Spiller" rule with a focus on heirloom varieties and soft, romantic textures.

The Florist's
Composition

01

SCALE & VOLUME

Your vessel should never be more than one-third of the total height of the arrangement. Focus on creating volume through varied leaf sizes.

02

TEXTURED CONTRAST

Pair glossy, broad leaves with fine, feathery textures to create visual depth and a sense of movement within the container.

03

SEASONAL LAYERING

Utilize perennials as your foundation and layer in seasonal annuals to maintain a vibrant look throughout the changing year.
