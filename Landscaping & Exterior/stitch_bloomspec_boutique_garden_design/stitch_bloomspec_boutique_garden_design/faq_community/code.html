How do I extend the life of my 'Cutting Garden' arrangement?

Artisanal blooms require a delicate touch. We recommend a clean, glass vessel filled with lukewarm water. Trim the stems at a 45-degree angle every two days to ensure optimal hydration channels remain open.

Keep your arrangement away from direct afternoon sun and ripening fruit, which releases ethylene gas, accelerating the aging process of sensitive petals like Ranunculus and Sweet Peas.

What should I bring to a Garden Workshop?

Our studio provides all necessary floral snips, aprons, and professional-grade vessels. We only ask that you bring an open mind and a passion for texture. If you have a specific heirloom vase you wish to style, feel free to bring it along.

Do you offer seasonal subscriptions?

Indeed. Our 'Seasonal Rhythm' subscription provides a bi-weekly curation of whatever is reaching its peak in the garden. This ensures your space is always inhabited by the most vibrant, ephemeral beauty of the current micro-season.
