Wilderness Safety & Medical Response

expand_more
Every Nomadic Chef expedition is accompanied by a Wilderness First Responder (WFR) certified guide. We carry satellite communication (Garmin inReach), comprehensive medical kits, and maintain detailed evacuation protocols for every location. Our staff is trained to handle local wildlife encounters and extreme weather transitions, ensuring your luxury experience remains secure.

Dietary Customization & Allergies

expand_more
We pride ourselves on culinary inclusivity. Whether you require strict Keto, Vegan, or have severe nut/shellfish allergies, our chefs plan your menu 30 days in advance. We utilize separate "clean-kit" cookware for severe allergies to prevent cross-contamination in remote settings. Please specify all requirements during the booking intake phase.

Booking Logistics & Cancellations

expand_more
Due to the complex logistical nature of transporting a kitchen into the wild, we require a 50% non-refundable deposit. Cancellations made more than 45 days prior to departure may receive credit toward a future expedition. Weather-related cancellations are rare; we are equipped to provide a luxury experience in rain or snow, unless conditions are deemed unsafe by our lead guide.
