PREPARATION

Sanctuary FAQ

We recommend hydrating significantly 24 hours prior to your ascent. Avoid heavy meals and alcohol 4 hours before your session. Due to the high altitude (4,000m+), please consult a physician if you have cardiovascular concerns.
Summit Sauna is a sanctuary of silence. We request that guests maintain a whisper or remain silent in shared spaces. Mobile devices are strictly prohibited within the ritual zones to preserve the meditative atmosphere.
Our private helicopter shuttle departs from the Base Camp every 30 minutes. Please arrive at the helipad 15 minutes prior to your scheduled slot. All required attire—robes, slippers, and towels—are provided upon arrival.
A standard journey lasts 120 minutes, including the heating phase, cold-plunge immersion, and the mountain-view rest period. Extended sunset rituals are available upon request.
