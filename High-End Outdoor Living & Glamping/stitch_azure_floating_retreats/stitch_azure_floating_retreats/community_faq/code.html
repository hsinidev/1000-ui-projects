Navigating the Deep

Everything you need to know about your upcoming aquatic sanctuary. From technical specs to safety protocols.

How secure are the floating domes in stormy weather?

add

Our domes are engineered with dynamic ballast systems that respond in real-time to sea conditions. The structural glass is multi-layered aeronautic grade, capable of withstanding extreme pressure and surface impacts. Each retreat is also anchored by a proprietary submerged stabilization matrix.

What happens to waste management in such remote areas?

add

We utilize a closed-loop "Bio-Evolve" system. 100% of liquid and solid waste is processed on-site through advanced microbial reactors, resulting in pristine, mineral-rich water that is safely returned to the ocean. We are proud to be a zero-outflow facility.

Is it safe to swim directly from the retreat platform?

add

Yes. Each retreat features a "Secure-Swim" perimeter that uses ultra-low frequency acoustic pulses to gently discourage large predators without harming any marine life. On-site AI sensors monitor water quality and currents 24/7 to ensure your safety.

How do guests reach their Aqua-Glamp destination?

add

Transportation is handled via our fleet of electric hydrofoil shuttles. These vessels "fly" above the waves to provide a smooth, silent, and nausea-free arrival experience. Your journey from the mainland terminal typically takes 25 to 40 minutes.
