Conservation Protocols

How do we ensure minimal disturbance?

expand_more

We utilize low-impact electric vehicles and maintain a minimum distance of 50 meters from all keystone species. Our guides are trained in non-intrusive observation techniques that prioritize animal well-being over visibility.

Where does the funding go?

expand_more

85% of all expedition fees are directly channeled into the habitat restoration project. This includes reforestation of native corridors and the support of local anti-poaching units within the Great Basin area.

Can guests participate in fieldwork?

expand_more

Yes, our 'Field Ledger' sessions allow guests to assist biologists with population counts and data entry for our seasonal migration reports, providing a hands-on connection to the ecosystem.
