Common inquiries regarding our clinical standards, organic sourcing, and ethical practices.

What is clinical aromatherapy?

expand_more
Clinical aromatherapy is the use of essential oils for targeted health outcomes. Unlike recreational use, it involves high-potency, therapeutic-grade extracts administered based on physiological research and chemistry-focused blending techniques.

Are your oils organic?

expand_more
Every batch we source is certified organic or wild-harvested. We prioritize ecological sustainability and gas chromatography-mass spectrometry (GC/MS) testing to ensure absolute purity from soil to bottle.

How do I store my oils?

expand_more
To preserve the delicate chemical structure, oils should be kept in amber or cobalt glass bottles, away from direct sunlight, and in a cool, stable environment (ideally below 20°C).

Can I ingest these oils?

expand_more
PureAroma does not recommend internal consumption without the direct supervision of a healthcare professional or qualified clinical aromatherapist, as high concentrations can cause mucosal irritation.
