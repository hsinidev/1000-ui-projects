The Golden Ratios

Essential oils are highly concentrated plant essences. Diluting with a carrier oil is essential for topical application to ensure efficacy while preventing sensitivity.

PRO TIP

Always use a patch test on the inner elbow before a full application, especially for "hot" oils like Cinnamon or Oregano.

Safety Fundamentals

Storage & Preservation

Keep oils in dark glass bottles away from direct sunlight and heat to prevent oxidation and maintain therapeutic potency.

warning

Ingestion Policy

Internal use should only be practiced under the direct supervision of a certified aromatherapist or licensed medical professional.

pets

Pet Safety

Certain oils (like Tea Tree and Citrus) can be toxic to domestic animals. Always ensure proper ventilation and space for pets to leave the room.

sunny

Phototoxicity

Cold-pressed citrus oils contain furanocoumarins which can cause severe skin reactions if exposed to UV light within 12-18 hours of application.
