How do I safely blend citrus oils for topical use?

add

Citrus oils such as Bergamot and Lemon are phototoxic. For safe topical application, always dilute to a maximum of 0.4% (roughly 2 drops per 30ml of carrier oil) if the skin will be exposed to sunlight within 12 hours.
