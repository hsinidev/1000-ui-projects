Configuration FAQ

Can I adjust anatomical specs after ordering?

add

Due to the precision-cast nature of our bisque porcelain, dimensions are finalized once the firing process begins. We provide a 48-hour revision window following your configurator submission.

What is the difference between Bisque and Resin?

add

Bisque offers a timeless, matte finish with a weight that mirrors traditional antiques, while our proprietary resin is lighter and more resilient for complex dynamic posing.
