Inquiries for the Archivist

When do the artifacts ship?

Relics are dispatched on the 15th of every month. Orders placed after the 10th will be included in the following month's shipment.

Can I change my path mid-journey?

Yes, the High Archivist permits tier changes at any time. Your new path will take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

What resides within each box?

Each box contains high-resolution resin miniatures, custom-mixed paints, and narrative scrolls. Legend tier boxes may include large-scale multi-part kits or terrain.

How is the billing handled?

We utilize secure arcane encryption for all transactions. You will be billed monthly on the date you first enrolled in your chosen path.

Are the miniatures pre-painted?

No. Part of the hobby is the ritual of the brush. We provide the artifacts and the materials; you provide the soul and the color.
