Top Operational Queries

How is the grading authenticity verified for high-value cards? expand_more

All assets deposited into the Archive undergo a secondary verification by our senior archivists. We cross-reference certificate IDs with PSA, BGS, and SGC databases while performing physical slab inspections using multi-spectrum ultraviolet scanning to ensure zero tampering.

What security measures protect the physical Vault locations? expand_more

Our physical storage facilities are Grade-5 institutional vaults featuring biometric multi-factor authentication, 24/7 armed surveillance, and seismic-dampened shelving. Every asset is stored in a climate-controlled, oxygen-regulated environment to prevent degradation.

How are assets insured during transit to the vault? expand_more

We partner with Lloyd’s of London to provide door-to-door coverage. For assets exceeding $100,000 in value, Grail-Archive provides armored transport logistics. Each shipment is tracked via GPS-enabled encrypted telemetry.

Can I withdraw an asset for private viewing? expand_more

Physical withdrawals can be scheduled with a 72-hour lead time. Assets are released via secure courier or at one of our regional clearance centers. Please note that withdrawal voids current active auction listings for that asset.
