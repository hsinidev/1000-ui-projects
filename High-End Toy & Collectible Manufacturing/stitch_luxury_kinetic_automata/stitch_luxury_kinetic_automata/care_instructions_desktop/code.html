Frequently Asked Questions

02 — INQUIRIES

Why is the motion slowing down?

Dust accumulation in the ball-bearing pivot is the most common cause. We recommend a compressed air cleaning of the main axle every 90 days to maintain friction-less rotation.

Can I use furniture polish on the wood?

Strictly no. Commercial polishes contain silicones that can penetrate the fiber and affect the weight balance. Use only the provided KINETIC ART botanical oil blend.

What is the expected lifespan of the drive spring?

Our tempered steel springs are engineered for 250,000 cycles. Under normal daily use, this equates to approximately 15 years of continuous motion before a replacement is recommended.

How do I reset the movement after a move?

After relocating the piece, ensure it is perfectly level using a spirit level. Even a 1-degree tilt can disrupt the escapement timing. Refer to Section 4.2 of the physical manual for leveling steps.
