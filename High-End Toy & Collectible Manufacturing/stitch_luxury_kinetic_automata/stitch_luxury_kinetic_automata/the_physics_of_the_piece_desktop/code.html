THEORY OF MOTION

Radial Synergy and Harmonic Balance

At the heart of the Kinetic Art collection lies the principle of Radial Synergy. Unlike traditional mechanisms that fight against friction, our movements leverage the rotational torque generated by precision-milled wooden vanes. This synergy ensures that every joule of energy is recycled through the system.

The interaction between the primary drive gear and the secondary escapement is calculated using non-linear dynamics, allowing the piece to maintain a consistent rhythm regardless of ambient temperature or humidity changes in the wood grain.

Gravity-Driven Power

The silent descent of a lead-filled weight provides the constant force required for movement. By employing a double-compound pulley system, we extend the power reserve to 72 hours, creating a tangible link between the user's weekly ritual of 'winding' and the object's life.
