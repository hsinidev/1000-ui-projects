Acquisition & Logisitics

Common inquiries regarding the purchase and global transport of our kinetic automata.

PURCHASING PROCESS

How do I secure a specific edition number?

Edition numbers are assigned on a first-come, first-served basis upon receipt of a 30% deposit. During your private viewing, you may request a specific available number from the remaining archive.

GLOBAL SHIPPING

What measures are taken for white-glove delivery?

All luxury automata are shipped in custom-milled, shock-absorbent walnut crates. We utilize specialized fine-art couriers who manage climate-controlled transit and provide on-site installation and calibration.

WARRANTY & SERVICE

What is the maintenance requirement for mechanical pieces?

Each artifact comes with a 5-year mechanical warranty. We recommend a professional cleaning and lubrication every 24 months, which can be performed by our traveling technicians or at our central atelier.
