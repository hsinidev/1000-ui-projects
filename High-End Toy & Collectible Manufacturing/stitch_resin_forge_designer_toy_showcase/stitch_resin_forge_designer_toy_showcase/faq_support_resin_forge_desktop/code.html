Product Care

Material preservation guidelines add
Our statues are composed of high-grade resin and cold-cast porcelain. To prevent oxidation or discoloration, maintain a temperature-controlled environment between 18-24°C and avoid direct UV exposure.
Cleaning the architectural finish add
Use only the provided microfiber kit for dusting. Do not use chemical solvents or water on matte finishes, as this may compromise the architectural texture of the piece.
