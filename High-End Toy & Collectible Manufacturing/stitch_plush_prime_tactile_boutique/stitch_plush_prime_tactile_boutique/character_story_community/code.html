Barnaby the Midnight Guardian

Born from the finest indigo-dyed silk and weighted with moonstone-infused pellets, Barnaby is more than a plush companion—he is a silent sentinel for the restless.

Crafted during the peak of the winter solstice, his fur mimics the velvet texture of a clear night sky. He was designed for those who find solace in the quiet hours, offering a tactile anchor when the world feels too loud. Every stitch in his paws represents a promise of protection, a soft-luxe guardian for your most precious dreams.

The Secret of the Silk

Each Barnaby plush undergoes a 48-hour "resting period" in a lavender-scented vault before shipment. This ensures the luxury textile fibers maintain their structural integrity while absorbing a calming botanical essence that lasts for years.
