Adoption & Shipping

How long does the adoption process take? expand_more
Each Plush-Prime companion is meticulously hand-finished upon adoption. Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days for domestic orders, while international adoptions may take 14-21 days depending on customs clearance.
Do you offer international shipping? expand_more
Yes, we ship our luxury textiles worldwide. Please note that adoptions to certain regions may incur additional import duties and taxes which are the responsibility of the adopter.

Material Care

Washing Instructions

To maintain the premium "squishy" feel, we recommend spot cleaning with a damp silk cloth and mild organic detergent. Avoid machine washing to preserve the delicate textile integrity.

What materials are used? expand_more
We use exclusive ultra-soft microfiber blends and premium hypoallergenic fillings that mimic the feel of silk clouds.
Are the plushies hypoallergenic? expand_more
Absolutely. Every Plush-Prime product is crafted with 100% hypoallergenic materials, safe for even the most sensitive tactile enthusiasts.
