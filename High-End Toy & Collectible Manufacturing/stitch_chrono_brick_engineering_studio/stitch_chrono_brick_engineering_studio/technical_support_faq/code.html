Assembly Protocols

SYS_CAT_01
All Chrono-Brick modules are engineered to a 0.05mm tolerance. Ensure surfaces are clean of debris before interlocking. For structural integrity, vertical load-bearing columns should be reinforced with the provided internal tension rods as per manual CB-STR-01.
Adhesives are strictly prohibited as they alter the thermal expansion coefficients of the polymer. Use the mechanical locking pins for permanent assemblies.
