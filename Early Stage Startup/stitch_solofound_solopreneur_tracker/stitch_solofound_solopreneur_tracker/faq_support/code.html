Zen Mode

What exactly is Zen Mode? expand_more
Zen Mode is our proprietary distraction-free interface. It flattens the UI, removes all non-essential shadows, and shifts to a high-contrast monochrome palette to help you enter a deep flow state.
Can I schedule Zen Mode? expand_more
Currently, Zen Mode is toggled manually to ensure you have full control over your focus environment, but automated scheduling is on our roadmap.

Task Management

What is the Backlog strategy? add

SoloFound encourages moving all non-essential items to the Backlog to keep your 'Today's Focus' completely clear of clutter.

Can I import from Notion? add

Yes! We offer a CSV and direct API import for Notion, Trello, and Linear users under the Admin Panel.
