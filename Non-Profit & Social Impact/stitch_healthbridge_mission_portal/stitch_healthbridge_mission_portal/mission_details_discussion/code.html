Pediatric Surgical Initiative 2024

Restoring mobility and hope through specialized surgical interventions for children in underserved coastal regions.

info About the Mission

This four-week intensive mission focuses on orthopedic and soft-tissue surgical procedures for pediatric patients ages 2-16. Located in the coastal hub of Mombasa, the mission serves a catchment area of over 200,000 residents with limited access to specialized surgical care.

Our team will collaborate with local health authorities to not only perform life-altering surgeries but also provide comprehensive post-operative training to local nursing staff, ensuring long-term sustainability and care excellence.
