COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find a shelter tonight? expand_more
Our real-time Resource Map shows available beds in your current vicinity. You can access it via the main navigation or by calling our 24/7 hotline. All shelters listed are vetted for safety and basic amenities.
Is my donation tax-deductible? expand_more
Yes, ShelterSpace Urban Solutions is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. All financial contributions are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive an automated tax receipt via email immediately after donating.
Can I donate physical goods like clothing? expand_more
We accept seasonal clothing, unopened hygiene products, and non-perishable food at our regional hubs. Please check our "Donation Guidelines" page for a specific list of current high-need items and drop-off locations.
How can my company partner with ShelterSpace? expand_more
Corporate partners can engage through financial grants, skills-based volunteering, or by sponsoring specific facility upgrades. Contact our Partnership Hub to receive a tailored impact report proposal.

Still need help?

Our support team is available 24/7 for urgent inquiries.
