Technical Specs

Our site selection involves a multi-stage geological survey using satellite imagery and local hydrogeological mapping. We prioritize regions with the highest scarcity index and ensure the soil structure can support long-term drilling without ecological disruption.
Every PureWater installation includes a 5-year proactive maintenance contract with local technicians. We train community water boards to handle minor repairs, while major mechanical servicing is performed bi-annually by our regional engineering teams.
Our high-grade photovoltaic systems are rated for 20-25 years of operation. The pumps themselves are brushless DC units designed for high-grit environments, typically requiring internal seal replacement every 10 years.

Engineering for Longevity

Every component is tested for extreme environments.

All wells are equipped with real-time IoT sensors that monitor pH levels, turbidity, and mineral content. This data is transmitted daily to our global dashboard, triggering automatic alerts if quality dips below WHO standards.
