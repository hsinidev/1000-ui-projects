Adoption & Donations

How are my donations allocated across different habitats? expand_more
Every contribution is split: 60% directly to habitat restoration and land acquisition, 30% to high-tech anti-poaching telemetry, and 10% to essential field researcher logistics. We provide quarterly transparent impact reports for all donors.
Can I adopt a specific individual animal within the reserve? expand_more
Yes, through our Digital Twin program, you can sponsor specific monitored individuals. You'll receive real-time updates on their health and migration patterns via our encrypted telemetry platform.

Field Research

What is the application process for visiting researchers? expand_more
Research applications must be submitted 6 months prior to the proposed start date. All projects must align with our primary conservation goals. After an initial ethics review, candidates are interviewed by our Chief Science Officer.
How do I access raw telemetry data for peer-reviewed studies? expand_more
Raw data is available through our Science Portal for verified institutional researchers. Due to the sensitive nature of endangered species locations, all data access requires a signed non-disclosure and ethical use agreement.

Technology & Security

How does BioReserve ensure data privacy for our field workers? expand_more
We utilize end-to-end encryption for all field communications. Location data for researchers is anonymized in our public-facing logs to prevent targeted tracking while maintaining absolute safety monitoring for our security team.
