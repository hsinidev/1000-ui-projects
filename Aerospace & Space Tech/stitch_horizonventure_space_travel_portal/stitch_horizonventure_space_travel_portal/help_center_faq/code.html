FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the duration of the pre-flight training program? expand_more
The Horizon Venture training regimen spans 12 weeks of intensive preparation at our private facility in the Atacama Desert. This includes G-force endurance, microgravity simulation, and sophisticated emergency response drills. For elite tier travelers, a compressed 4-week executive program is available upon medical clearance.
How are medical emergencies handled in orbit? expand_more
Every Horizon Venture vessel is equipped with an AI-integrated surgical suite and staffed by a certified Flight Surgeon. We maintain a dedicated telemetry link with the Global Orbital Trauma Center. In extreme cases, a rapid-reentry pod is permanently available for immediate return to terrestrial medical facilities.
What communication services are available for families? expand_more
Our proprietary Quantum-Link ensures zero-latency ultra-high-definition video calls to any location on Earth. Guests also have access to the Private Orbital Social Network to share high-resolution captures of their journey in real-time.
Are there weight restrictions for personal cargo? expand_more
Standard allowance is 15kg of personal items, which must undergo sterilization and safety screening. Custom storage modules can be purchased for additional cargo, including specialized professional photography gear or private scientific instruments.
