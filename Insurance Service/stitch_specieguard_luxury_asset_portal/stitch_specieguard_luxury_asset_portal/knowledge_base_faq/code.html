How do I verify the provenance of a new acquisition?

add

Submit your high-resolution documentation via the Concierge portal. Our team of historians and forensic appraisers will conduct a triple-layer validation against international databases and auction house archives.

What is the standard for international transit insurance?

add

Standard coverage includes door-to-door protection provided the transport is managed by an approved 'Tier 1' courier. Specific 'Riders' are required for transit exceeding $50M USD in value.

Are temporary loans to public galleries covered?

add

Yes, under our 'Nail-to-Nail' endorsement. You must provide the borrowing institution's climate control logs and security certificates prior to release.
