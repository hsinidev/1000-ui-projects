Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my clearance level? expand_more
Clearance level elevation requires a multi-sig approval from the Operational Security Committee. Submit form SO-REQ-01 through the secure terminal at headquarters. Standard processing time is 48 planetary hours.
Encryption protocol for external transfers? expand_more
All external data outbound must utilize AES-256-GCM with rotating ephemeral keys. Please refer to Policy Doc 114.b for specific implementation details regarding zero-knowledge handshakes.
Authorized hardware for MFA? expand_more
Only FIDO2 certified hardware keys issued by SHIELDOPS Logistics are permitted. Third-party mobile authenticators are restricted to Tier 3 personnel and below.
