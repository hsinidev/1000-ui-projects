A comprehensive Stage 3 restoration typically spans 12 to 18 months. This meticulous process involves a complete nut-and-bolt disassembly, chemical stripping of the chassis, and hand-stitched interior trimming. Each phase is documented in your private archival logbook with high-resolution photography.

Non-original engines (period-correct replacements) generally result in a 15-20% adjustment to the Investment Grade versus "Matching Numbers" examples. However, if the replacement is a factory-documented exchange, the premium retention is significantly higher. Our graders utilize the Heritage Garage Proprietary Index for these evaluations.

Consignment within the Heritage Garage Private Showroom carries a flat 8% commission on successful sale. This includes professional studio photography, historical research documentation, and international marketing through our network of vetted private collectors. There are no upfront cataloging fees for qualified assets.

Digital verification is available through our 'Private Office' portal for registered members. By entering a chassis number, you can view the basic verification certificate. Full archival pedigree dossiers, which include chain-of-ownership and factory build sheets, require a physical inspection or notarized request.
