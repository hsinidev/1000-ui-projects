Frequently Asked Questions

Find immediate answers to common inquiries regarding our private aviation services.

How early should I arrive before my charter?

For domestic flights, we recommend arriving at the FBO (Fixed Base Operator) 15–20 minutes prior to your scheduled departure. Our crew will be prepared for immediate boarding upon your arrival. For international charters, please allow 30 minutes for customs document verification.

What is the baggage allowance for the Global 7500?

The Global 7500 features a generous pressurized baggage compartment accessible during flight. Standard allowance is 20 pieces of medium luggage (up to 195 cubic feet). For oversized items like golf clubs or skis, please notify your concierge 24 hours prior to departure.

Can I modify my catering menu after booking?

Yes, menu modifications can be accommodated up to 12 hours before departure. Our culinary team works with world-class chefs to ensure bespoke dining experiences. Specific dietary requirements or luxury preferences (e.g., specific vintages) should be communicated as early as possible.

What is the policy for traveling with pets?

SkySleek is a pet-friendly operator. We require updated vaccination records and proper restraint during takeoff and landing. Our concierge can arrange for specialized pet catering and comfort kits upon request.
