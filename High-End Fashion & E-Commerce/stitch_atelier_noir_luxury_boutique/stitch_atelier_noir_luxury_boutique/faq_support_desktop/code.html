SHIPPING PROTOCOLS

All archival pieces and seasonal ready-to-wear items are shipped via our secure carbon-neutral logistics network. Delivery times range from 48 hours for domestic transit to 5-7 business days for international destinations. Each shipment is fully insured and requires a signature upon receipt to maintain the chain of custody.

Items are encased in climate-controlled, sustainable matte black housing, featuring tamper-evident seals and archival-grade protection. We prioritize the preservation of the garment's structural silhouette during transit.

02

RETURNS & EXCHANGES

To ensure the exclusivity and pristine condition of our collections, returns must be initiated within 14 days of delivery. Pieces must remain in unworn condition with all original security tags and seals intact.

Bespoke commissions, personalized fragrance, and items marked as 'Final Archive' are ineligible for return. We encourage clients to consult with our agents prior to finalizing high-tier acquisitions.
