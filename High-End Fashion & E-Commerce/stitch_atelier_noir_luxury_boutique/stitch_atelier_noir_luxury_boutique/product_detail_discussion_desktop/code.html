A singular exploration of tectonic form. The Obsidian Structure is constructed from a proprietary 18oz technical wool-blend, featuring internal scaffolding to maintain its architectural silhouette throughout motion.

TECHNICAL PHILOSOPHY

The Craft

Anatomy of Form

Each piece is hand-assembled in our Parisian atelier over a 48-hour period. The internal structure utilizes repurposed aerospace carbon rods, ensuring the lapel and shoulder lines remain mathematically perfect for the life of the garment.

Sustainment

Sourced from a multi-generational mill in Biella, the wool is treated with a natural, fluorocarbon-free water-repellent finish. We believe in garments that outlive the wearer.
