FABRICATION & ETHOS

01. ORIGIN

Ethically sourced from a small-batch mill in Biella, Italy. The wool undergoes a proprietary 48-hour boiling process to achieve maximum density and weather resistance without synthetic coatings.

02. ASSEMBLY

Each piece is assembled in our Paris atelier by a single artisan. Laser-cut edges are hand-sealed to prevent fraying while maintaining a raw, avant-garde finish.

03. LONGEVITY

Structured to hold its shape over decades. The internal canvassing uses traditional horsehair to ensure the collar and lapel maintain their geometric precision through wear.
