Chronicles of Innovation

1892

The Foundation

Auguste Elite establishes the workshop in Le Locle, specializing in maritime chronometers that set new standards for naval navigation.

1945

Post-War Precision

The introduction of the 'Caliber 45' movement, the first wristwatch movement to receive the double Geneva Seal for finishing and accuracy.
