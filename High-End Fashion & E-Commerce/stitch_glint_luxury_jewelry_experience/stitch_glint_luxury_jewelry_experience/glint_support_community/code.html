Frequently Asked Questions

Our bespoke journey begins with a private consultation with one of our master gemologists. We curate a selection of ethically sourced diamonds tailored specifically to your preferences and the 4Cs profile established during your intake.

We offer complimentary, fully insured international priority shipping. Every piece is discreetly packaged in our signature vault-style boxes to ensure security and maintain the element of surprise until the moment of unveiling.

Every AURUM & CO. piece is accompanied by a lifetime warranty covering cleaning, stone tightening, and structural integrity. We invite you to visit our ateliers annually for a professional revitalisation service.

Complimentary resizing is available for all engagement rings within the first 6 months of purchase. Simply contact your dedicated concierge representative to arrange a secure collection of your piece.
