Fitting

What should I wear to my first consultation? expand_more

Wear form-fitting undergarments and, if possible, the shoes you intend to wear with your bespoke piece. This ensures the most accurate silhouette measurement.

How many fittings are required for a tuxedo? expand_more

Typically, three fittings are required: the Initial Measurement, the Baste Fitting, and the Final Refinement. Complex commissions may require a fourth session.
