Find Your Silhouette

A curated exploration of form and grace. Discover the silhouette that speaks to your unique elegance.

A-Line

Universal elegance. The A-line silhouette is fitted at the bodice and flows out gently toward the hem, resembling the shape of an uppercase 'A'.

Ball Gown

The classic princess moment. A dramatic, full skirt paired with a fitted bodice, ideal for grand venues and making a definitive entrance.

Mermaid

Contoured sophistication. This silhouette closely follows the curves of the body from the chest to the knee, where it flares out boldly.

Sheath

Modern minimalism. A narrow shape that flows straight down from the neckline to the hem, perfect for an understated yet powerful look.

Empire

Regency charm. Defined by a high waistline just below the bust, falling into a long, loose, and graceful skirt that elongates the frame.

Trumpet

Sculpted beauty. A compromise between the mermaid and A-line, it fits closely to the body and flares out at the mid-thigh.
