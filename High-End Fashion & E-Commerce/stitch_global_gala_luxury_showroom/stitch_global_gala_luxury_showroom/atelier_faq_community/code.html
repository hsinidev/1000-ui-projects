Atelier FAQ

Every silhouette tells a story; every question deserves a moment of quiet reflection.

To preserve the ethereal quality of our bespoke gowns, we recommend professional preservation services exclusively. Avoid standard dry cleaning; silk and hand-sewn crystal embellishments require specialized air-cleaning techniques.

Store your garment in a temperature-controlled environment away from direct light to prevent the "Blue Hour" dyes from shifting over time.

Our silhouettes are meticulously tailored to individual measurements. During your initial consultation, we utilize advanced 3D scanning alongside traditional draping to ensure a flawless fit that respects the natural movement of the form.

Alterations are handled by our master tailors within the GLOBAL-GALA atelier to maintain structural integrity.

Membership in our inner circle provides priority access to international trunk shows. Invitations are dispatched four weeks prior to each event, held in exclusive metropolitan locations ranging from New York to Dubai.

RSVP is mandatory as these moments are curated for a maximum of twelve guests per session.
