Condition Grading

We use a strict editorial grading system to ensure every buyer understands the history and present state of their acquisition.

info

"Our specialists reject over 40% of submitted items to maintain the sanctity of our vault."

01

Pristine

Unused with original tags, protective plastics, and full packaging. Condition is identical to a boutique purchase.

02

Excellent

Worn sparingly. No visible signs of wear to the leather or hardware. May include original dustbag.

03

Very Good

Light visible wear. Minor scuffing at corners or faint hairline scratches on hardware from respectful use.

04

Fair

Significant wear or patina. Well-loved pieces that retain their structural integrity but show clear signs of their journey.
