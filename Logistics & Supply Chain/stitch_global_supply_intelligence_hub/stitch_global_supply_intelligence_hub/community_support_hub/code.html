Frequently Asked Questions

How do I resolve real-time port congestion alerts? expand_more
Port congestion alerts are triggered when dwell times exceed 72 hours. Navigate to 'Predictive Analytics' to view alternative routing options. Our AI engine suggests rerouting through secondary hubs if the throughput differential is >15%.
Integrating third-party IoT sensors into the dashboard expand_more
SupplyChain360 supports standard MQTT and REST API protocols. Go to Admin Panel > Integration Hub to generate your secure API keys and download the integration manifest.
Managing multi-tiered supplier risk assessments expand_more
Risk assessments utilize the ISO 28000 framework. Ensure all Tier 2 and Tier 3 suppliers have completed their digital self-audits to generate a complete compliance score.
