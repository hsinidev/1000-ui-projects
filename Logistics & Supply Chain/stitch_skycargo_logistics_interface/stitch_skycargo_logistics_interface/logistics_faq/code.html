Procedure: Lithium-Ion Battery Screening

Effective Date: OCT 2024 | Revision: 14.2

CRITICAL STEP 01: Visual Verification

Inspect outer packaging for UN3480 / UN3481 labeling. Ensure the Cargo Aircraft Only (CAO) label is present for standalone battery shipments exceeding 30% state of charge.

STEP 02: Weight Validation

Compare gross weight with Shippers Declaration for Dangerous Goods (SDDG). Any discrepancy >0.5kg requires immediate quarantine of the shipment.

STEP 03: Digital Logging

Input the UN ID and quantity into the Flight Loading System (FLS) to ensure automatic center-of-gravity calculation and emergency fire-suppression planning.
