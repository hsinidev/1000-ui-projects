Carbon Accounting

Our platform utilizes the GLEC Framework for greenhouse gas emissions calculation. We integrate real-time data from carrier telematics and fuel consumption reports to provide high-precision Scope 3 estimates, ensuring that all indirect value chain emissions are captured and reported with 98% accuracy.
Yes, the Dashboard includes a "Reporting Toolkit" specifically designed for GRI, SASB, and TCFD standards. You can export data in CSV, PDF, and interactive JSON formats, complete with data visualizations ready for inclusion in your annual reports.

EV Logistics

Our AI-driven routing engine incorporates dynamic variables including battery health, current state of charge, topography, weather conditions, and real-time charger availability. We maintain a "Safety Margin" protocol that ensures trucks always arrive at high-capacity charging nodes with at least 15% charge remaining.
Yes, our specialized cold-chain EV modules use integrated battery management systems to power refrigeration units independently from the traction battery. This ensures consistent temperature control even during traffic congestion or stationary loading periods.
