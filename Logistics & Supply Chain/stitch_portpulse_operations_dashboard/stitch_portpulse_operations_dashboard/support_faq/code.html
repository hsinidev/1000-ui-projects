Frequently Asked Questions

UPDATED: 2023.10.24

All personnel entering Zone B (Quayside) must wear Class 3 high-visibility vests, industrial steel-toe footwear (ASTM F2413), and reinforced head protection. Respiratory masks are required during dry bulk discharge.

Code 403 occurs when the RFID transponder does not match the pre-lodged manifest. Verify the vessel manifest in the Operations Hub or contact Terminal Gate Dispatch on Channel 14.

STS Gantry Cranes undergo automated optical calibration every 72 hours of operation. Manual sensor verification is required following wind gusts exceeding 45 knots.
