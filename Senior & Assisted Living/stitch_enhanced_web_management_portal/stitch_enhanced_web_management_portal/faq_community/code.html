Life at VitalStay

What defines the 'Restorative Suite' experience? expand_more
Our suites are designed with editorial minimalism in mind—prioritizing quietude and restorative textures. Each suite features hospital-grade care discreetly integrated into a five-star hospitality environment, ensuring dignity and comfort are never compromised.
Are daily enrichment programs customizable? expand_more
Precisely. Upon arrival, our lifestyle concierge coordinates with your clinical team to curate a schedule of cognitive therapy, physical movement, and social salons that reflect your personal intellectual interests.

Care Standards

How is clinical oversight maintained? expand_more
VitalStay maintains a 1:4 staff-to-guest ratio, with 24/7 on-site nursing. We partner with leading geriatric specialists to ensure that every respite stay serves as a seamless extension of home-based care plans.
What protocols are in place for specialized nutrition? expand_more
Our culinary team works directly with clinical nutritionists to provide "Restorative Dining"—gourmet, seasonal meals tailored to specific dietary requirements without sacrificing the sensory pleasure of fine dining.
