Frequently Asked Questions

What are the gym hours during holiday weekends? expand_more
The fitness center remains open 24/7 for residents with a valid key fob. On holiday weekends, specialized trainers and classes are cancelled, but all equipment remains accessible for individual use.
How do I submit a maintenance request? expand_more
Maintenance requests can be submitted directly through the "Management" tab in the SideNav or by visiting the Resident Portal homepage. For urgent matters like leaks or electrical issues after 5 PM, please call the emergency hotline at (555) 0199.
Can guests use the VillageSquare Wi-Fi? expand_more
Yes! Guests can connect to the 'VS-Guest' network. The password is refreshed monthly and can be found on the bulletin board in the main lobby or in the monthly physical newsletter delivered to your unit.

Our dedicated community team is here to assist you with anything you need. We pride ourselves on timely, personal support.
