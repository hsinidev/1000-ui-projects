Admissions

What documents are needed?

A full medical history, valid ID, and insurance verification are required for the initial intake process.

How long is the waitlist?

Current wait times vary by suite level. Standard suites currently have a 3-month lead time.

medical_services

Care

What is the staff-to-resident ratio?

We maintain a 1:5 ratio during peak hours and 1:8 during night shifts to ensure immediate response.

How are meds managed?

Our automated dispensing system, overseen by licensed nurses, ensures 100% accuracy in medication delivery.

spa

Amenities

Is the pool heated?

Yes, our therapeutic pool is maintained at a constant 88°F for joint comfort and aquatic therapy sessions.

Are pets allowed?

We are pet-friendly! Small companions are welcome in private suites following a temperament assessment.
