Meet the Master Behind the Menu

Chef Julian Vance joins SilverOasis after a distinguished twenty-year career at Michelin-starred destinations across Europe and North America.

His philosophy centers on "Radical Freshness"—sourcing 90% of all ingredients from local organic farms within a 50-mile radius of the resort. Julian believes that dining is not just about sustenance, but about creating shared memories that define the resident experience.
