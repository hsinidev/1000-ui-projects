How does Sustainability work here?

Our lodges run entirely on passive solar and geothermal energy. We utilize a closed-loop graywater system for our organic gardens, ensuring every drop of water is honored and repurposed for the forest floor.

restaurant

What are the Dining hours?

The Hearth Dining Room serves breakfast from 7:00 AM to 9:30 AM, Lunch from 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM, and a communal fireside dinner starting at 6:30 PM. All ingredients are locally foraged or sourced.

forest

Are activities supervised?

Guided forest walks and woodworking workshops are led by certified naturalists. While we encourage independent exploration, our staff is always present at the trailhead stations for safety and guidance.

medical_services

Where is the support staff?

Discreet support staff are stationed at 'The Root'—our central hub. They are available 24/7 for wellness checks and assistance, maintaining a respectful distance to preserve your privacy and peace.
