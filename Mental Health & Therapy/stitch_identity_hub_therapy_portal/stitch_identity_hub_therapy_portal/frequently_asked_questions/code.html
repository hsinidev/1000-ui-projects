Finding a Provider

How do I verify a provider's affirming credentials?

expand_more

Every provider in our directory undergoes a multi-step verification process. We review their professional licenses, check for specific LGBTQ+ competency training, and require them to sign our Affirming Care Pledge. Look for the "Verified Affirming" badge on provider profiles.

Can I search for providers by specific identities?

expand_more

Absolutely. Our directory allows you to filter by the provider's own lived experience, gender identity, and languages spoken, alongside clinical specialties. We believe representation matters in the therapeutic relationship.

Safety & Privacy

Is my interaction with providers private?

expand_more

The Identity Hub uses bank-level encryption for all messaging and data storage. We do not sell your data to third parties. Your identity and the providers you contact remain strictly confidential within our HIPAA-compliant infrastructure.

What should I do if I encounter a non-affirming provider?

expand_more

We take these reports very seriously. There is a "Report Provider" link on every profile. Our quality assurance team will investigate within 24 hours. If a provider is found to violate our safety guidelines, they are permanently removed from the platform.

Community Events

Who can attend the virtual workshops?

expand_more

Most of our workshops are open to all community members. Some specific support groups may be closed to individuals with specific lived experiences (e.g., Trans-only spaces) to ensure maximum safety and belonging. Group details will always specify the intended audience.

Are there costs associated with community events?

expand_more

The majority of our community-led events are free. Clinician-led therapeutic workshops may have a small sliding-scale fee, but we never turn anyone away for lack of funds. Scholarships are always available upon request.
