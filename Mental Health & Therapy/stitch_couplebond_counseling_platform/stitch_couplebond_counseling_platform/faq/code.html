Frequently Asked Questions

CONFIDENTIALITY

Is our data and conversation private?

add

Every interaction within CoupleBond is protected by medical-grade encryption. We adhere to strict HIPAA-compliant protocols and clinical ethical standards to ensure your journey remains strictly between you, your partner, and your licensed specialist.

WORKSHOP FORMATS

What are the different workshop formats?

add

We offer three distinct formats: Self-Paced digital modules for independent growth, Live Group Intensives for shared community learning, and Private Couple Retreats designed for deep, focused restoration under direct guidance.

PRICING

How does the investment work?

add

We believe in transparent, value-driven pricing. Consultation fees vary based on specialist expertise level. Workshops are priced per couple, and we offer flexible payment plans to ensure healing is accessible without financial strain.

SESSION LENGTH

How long is a standard session?

add

Standard clinical consultations are 50 minutes. However, for specialized intake or crisis resolution, we offer 90-minute extended sessions to ensure ample space for both partners to be fully heard and understood.
