Frequently Asked Questions

Healing is a journey of clarity. We have structured our answers to help you and your partner navigate the logistical and emotional landscape of CoupleBond's clinical approach.

CONFIDENTIALITY

lock

How do you protect our privacy?

All consultations and journal entries are encrypted using industry-standard protocols. Our clinical records are maintained in a secure, HIPAA-compliant vault accessible only by your assigned specialist.

WORKSHOP FORMATS

Are workshops conducted in groups? expand_more
Our workshops are primarily structured as private "Couple-Centric" intensives. While we offer occasional group seminars for theory, the healing work remains strictly between you, your partner, and the moderator.
Can we attend remotely? expand_more
Yes. We utilize a secure, high-definition digital interface that supports the split-screen modality, ensuring both partners feel equally represented in the digital space.

PRICING & SESSION LENGTH

schedule

How long is a standard session?

Initial evaluations are 90 minutes. Subsequent healing sessions are 50 minutes, structured with 20 minutes for each individual perspective and 10 minutes for unified synthesis.

payments

Do you accept insurance?

We operate as an out-of-network provider to maintain the highest level of clinical autonomy and privacy. We provide detailed superbills for reimbursement requests.

Still have questions?

If you need further clarification on our methodology or logistics, our support team is available for a private chat.
