Common Questions

We understand that seeking support can feel overwhelming. Here, we aim to provide clarity and peace of mind as you navigate our quiet sanctuary.

Joining a Group

Finding your community is a gentle process of connection and shared understanding.

How do I find a group that fits my experience?

We categorize our groups based on shared experiences, such as the loss of a partner, parent, or friend. You can browse our "Groups" tab and filter by type of loss, or reach out to our support coordinators who can help guide you toward a group that feels like the right fit for your current journey.

What is expected of me during a group session?

There are no demands on your voice or your story. You are welcome to share as much or as little as you feel comfortable with. Our sessions are facilitated to ensure everyone feels heard, but silence is also a respected and valued part of the healing process.

Are these groups led by professionals?

Yes, every GriefBridge group is facilitated by a trained grief counselor or a certified peer-support leader who has specialized training in creating safe, empathetic digital environments.

How can I create a tribute for my loved one?

The Memorial Wall is a digital space for quiet reflection. You can create a profile for your loved one, share a single photograph, and write a narrative of their life. We prioritize a minimalist aesthetic to ensure the focus remains entirely on the memory and the person being honored.

Can others leave comments on tributes?

To maintain the "Quiet Design" philosophy, we do not have traditional comment sections. Instead, visitors can light a "virtual candle" or leave a brief, pre-written word of support (such as "Remembered" or "Peace"). This prevents the anxiety of managing social interactions on a memorial.

Privacy & Safety

Your vulnerability is precious. We protect it with the utmost care and transparency.

Is my data shared with any third parties?

Never. GriefBridge is a sanctuary, not a marketplace. We do not sell your data, use tracking pixels for advertising, or share your personal stories with anyone outside of your chosen support groups.

How do you ensure group safety?

All members go through a brief verification process to ensure the community remains a space for genuine seekers of healing. Facilitators are present in every live session to maintain a respectful and safe environment. We have zero tolerance for harassment or intrusive behavior.

Can I delete my account and data?

Yes. You have full control over your presence here. At any time, you can choose to archive or permanently delete your profile, memorials, and journal entries with a single request.

Still have questions?

If you haven't found what you're looking for, our support team is here to listen and help in any way we can.
