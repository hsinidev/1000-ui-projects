Why Play Therapy?

For children, toys are like their words and play is their language. Our therapists use specific play techniques to help children express what is troubling them when they don't have the verbal language to express their thoughts and feelings.

  • check_circle Reduces anxiety through natural interaction.
  • check_circle Builds confidence and social skills.
  • check_circle Develops better emotional regulation.
