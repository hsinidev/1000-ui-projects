Dr. Sarah Joy

Child Psychologist Sensory Integration

With over 15 years of experience, Dr. Sarah specializes in helping children navigate neurodiversity through gentle, child-led interventions. She believes that a child's "work" is their play, and her sessions are designed to feel like adventures.

Mr. David Chen

Speech Pathologist Early Intervention

David is passionate about giving every child a voice. He uses interactive storytelling and digital play tools to make speech therapy an engaging and rewarding experience for toddlers and school-aged children alike.
