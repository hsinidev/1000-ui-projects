favorite Getting Started

How do I schedule my first evaluation? expand_more
Starting your journey is simple! You can click the "Booking" link in our navigation menu to see available times, or call our friendly concierge at (555) 123-4567. We'll guide you through the initial paperwork and match your child with the therapist who best fits their unique personality and needs.
What should my child wear to therapy? expand_more
Comfort is key! We recommend comfortable play clothes and socks. Many of our sessions involve movement, climbing, and tactile play, so clothes that allow for a full range of motion (and a little bit of mess!) are perfect.
Do you offer virtual therapy sessions? expand_more
Yes, we do! We provide Telehealth services for Speech and some Occupational therapy sessions. This is a great option for families with busy schedules or those who live a bit further away from our physical clinic locations.

shield Insurance & Payments

Which insurance providers do you work with? expand_more
We are currently in-network with Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, UnitedHealthcare, and Cigna. For other providers, we can offer "superbills" that you can submit for out-of-network reimbursement. Our billing specialist is happy to run a benefits check for you!
What is your cancellation policy? expand_more
We ask for at least 24 hours' notice for any cancellations. This allows us to offer the time slot to another child on our waiting list. Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice may be subject to a small rescheduling fee.
