The Pillars of Resistance

01

Radical Repair

Don't buy new. The most sustainable garment is the one you already own. Every patch is a badge of honor; every visible mend is a SECRET CODE to the system.

02

Textile Sovereignty

Own your production. When you know how to deconstruct, you are no longer a consumer. You are a CREATOR. Reject the factory; embrace the workshop.

03

Anti-Fast Fashion

Rebel against trends. Speed is the enemy. We slow down. We stitch deep. We build things that OUTLAST THE ALGORITHM.

SOLIDARITY
100% RECYCLED IDEAS
P-01

Protocol 01: Scavenging

All materials must be sourced from the 'dead stock' of capital. Charity shops, dumpsters, and forgotten attics are our primary mines. We do not support the extraction of new resources.

P-02

Protocol 02: Open Source

Knowledge is a common good. Our patterns are free, our techniques are shared, and our mistakes are documented. We are building a library of resistance, one stitch at a time.

P-03

Protocol 03: Urban Foraging

The city is our ecosystem. We harvest from the overflow—discarded samples, industrial waste, and remnants. Every scrap is a potential masterpiece.
