Issue No. 01 / Manifesto

OUR RADICAL
MANIFESTO

The fashion industry is a meat grinder. We are the wrench in the gears. Stitch Punk isn't just about sewing; it's about reclaiming the means of personal expression from the corporate entities that want us compliant and disposable.

RE-STITCH OR DIE

The Pillars of Resistance

01

Radical Repair

Don't buy new. The most sustainable garment is the one you already own. Every patch is a badge of honor; every visible mend is a secret message to the system.

02

Textile Sovereignty

Own your production. When you know how to deconstruct and rebuild, you are no longer a consumer. You are a creator. Reject the factory; embrace the workshop.

03

Anti-Fast Fashion

Rebel against disposable trends. Speed is the enemy of craft. We slow down. We stitch deep. We build things that outlast the algorithm.

Deconstruct Rebuild Repeat

Join the Rebellion

Get our weekly survival guide for the textile underground. No ads. No spam. Just pure, unadulterated construction techniques and radical inspiration.

Solidarity

Protocol 01: Scavenging

All materials must be sourced from the 'dead stock' of capital. Charity shops, dumpsters, and forgotten attics are our primary mines. We do not support the extraction of new resources while the old ones rot in landfills.

Protocol 02: Open Source

Knowledge is a common good. Our patterns are free, our techniques are shared, and our mistakes are documented. We are building a library of resistance, one stitch at a time.
