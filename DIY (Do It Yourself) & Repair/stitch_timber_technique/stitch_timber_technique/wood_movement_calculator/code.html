Workshop Notes

Wood is a hygroscopic material. It perpetually exchanges moisture with the surrounding environment to reach an Equilibrium Moisture Content (EMC).

Tangential movement (Flat-sawn) is typically double that of Radial movement (Quarter-sawn). Always design joinery to accommodate these seasonal shifts.
