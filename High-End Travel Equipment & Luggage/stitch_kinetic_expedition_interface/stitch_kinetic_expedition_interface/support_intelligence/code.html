K101-A: OPTIMIZING ROTATIONAL INERTIA IN ARCTIC ENVIRONMENTS? expand_more

In sub-zero conditions, lubricant viscosity increases significantly. To maintain harvesting efficiency, ensure the Thermal Recirculator is set to 'Active-Flow' mode. This diverts 2% of generated energy to heat the bearing assembly, reducing friction by 40%.
