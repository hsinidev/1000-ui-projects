Shipping

What is the standard global delivery window? expand_more
Aero-Globe prioritizes expedited movement. Standard global shipping typically arrives within 3 to 5 business days. For remote destinations, please allow up to 7 business days. All shipments are fully insured and tracked via our private logistics partner.
Do you offer white-glove delivery service? expand_more
Yes, for our Bespoke and Heritage collections, we offer a complimentary white-glove service. Our couriers will deliver to your preferred room, unbox, and remove all packaging materials at your convenience.

Full-Grain Leather

Our leathers develop a natural patina. Clean with a soft, damp cloth and apply a neutral leather balm every six months.

How do I store my luggage between travels? expand_more
Store your Aero-Globe cases in a cool, dry place within the provided dust bags. Avoid stacking heavy objects on top to prevent structural deformation of the lightweight magnesium frames.

Gift Concierge

Can I include a personalized message? expand_more
Every Aero-Globe gift is accompanied by a hand-calligraphed card. You may provide your text during checkout, or our concierge can draft a message based on your specified sentiment.

Orders

How can I modify a bespoke order? expand_more
Modifications to bespoke orders must be made within 24 hours of confirmation. Beyond this window, materials are sourced and hand-crafting begins. Please contact our 24/7 Concierge for urgent adjustments.
