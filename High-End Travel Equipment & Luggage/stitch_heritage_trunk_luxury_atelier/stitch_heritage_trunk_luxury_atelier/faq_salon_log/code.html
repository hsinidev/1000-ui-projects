Shipping & Provenance

Methods of Conveyance expand_more
All heritage artifacts are dispatched via a white-glove courier service. Each trunk is secured in a custom-fitted climate-controlled crate to ensure the integrity of the hand-tanned leathers and brass fittings remains uncompromised during transit.
Bespoke Material Origins expand_more
We source only Grade-A vegetable-tanned hides from select tanneries in Tuscany. Our hardware is solid sand-cast brass, hand-polished in our private atelier. Every material is documented with a certificate of provenance.
International Salon Protocols expand_more
Our atelier serves a global clientele. Please note that customs duties and import levies are handled by our dedicated logistics concierge to provide a seamless door-to-door delivery experience.
