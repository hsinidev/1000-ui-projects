The Corner Reinforcement

Each corner is hand-hammered using solid brass, secured by exactly twelve copper rivets—a technique perfected by our atelier in 1894 to withstand the rigors of transatlantic voyages.

ESTABLISHED 1894

The Alchemy of Wood & Hide

The creation of a Heritage Trunk begins not with a blueprint, but with the selection of the tree. Our master carpenters source only sustainable Poplar, chosen for its lightness and remarkable resilience against the fluctuating pressures of high-altitude travel and deep-sea transit.

Layer by layer, the skeleton is draped in signature canvases or hand-buffed calfskin. Our artisans employ the "saddle-stitch" method, a process so intricate that no machine can replicate its durability. If a single thread breaks, the rest remain secure—the ultimate metaphor for our commitment to your legacy.
