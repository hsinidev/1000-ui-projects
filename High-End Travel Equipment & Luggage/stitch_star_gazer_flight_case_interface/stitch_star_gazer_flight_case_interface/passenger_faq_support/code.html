01.

What is the maximum payload for personal luggage?

expand_more

Passengers are allotted a total of 12kg for personal effects. This weight limit is strictly enforced due to the precise fuel-to-mass ratios required for orbital insertion. All items must be soft-shelled and fit within the designated 30cm x 40cm x 20cm locker located behind your flight seat.

System Note:

Excess weight will be charged at a rate of 50,000 Credits per kilogram, subject to mission commander approval.

02.

How do I manage G-force induced vertigo?

expand_more

Our flight seats are engineered with active dampening systems, but internal stabilization is required. We recommend the Hickam Maneuver during peak ascent. Focus on the internal HUD horizon line rather than looking out the viewing port until stable orbit is achieved. On-board medical drones are available if symptoms persist past T+15 minutes.
