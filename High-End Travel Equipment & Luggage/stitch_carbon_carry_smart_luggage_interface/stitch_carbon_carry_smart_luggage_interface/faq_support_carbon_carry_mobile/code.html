How does the aerospace-grade carbon frame react to high-salinity coastal environments?

All CARBON-CARRY frames are treated with a proprietary anti-corrosive UV-resistant resin. In high-salinity zones, we recommend a freshwater rinse every 48 hours to prevent mineral accumulation on pivot points.

ID: 002-BRAVO add_circle

What is the maximum torque limit for the modular wheel hub assembly?

The structural limit is rated at 45Nm. However, for standard terrain deployment, 32Nm provides the optimal balance between rigidity and vibration dampening.

ID: 003-CHARLIE add_circle

Can the integrated GPS unit be recalibrated for high-altitude trekking?

The GPS module includes an atmospheric pressure sensor. Manual recalibration is available under Admin > Advanced Telemetry for altitudes exceeding 4,000m ASL.

ID: 004-DELTA add_circle

Warranty protocol for structural carbon hairline fractures?

CARBON-CARRY offers a lifetime chassis replacement. If a fracture is detected, upload a high-resolution scan to the Material Lab for stress-test verification.
