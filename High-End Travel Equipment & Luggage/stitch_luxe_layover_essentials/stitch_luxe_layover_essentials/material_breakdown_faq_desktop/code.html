Sourcing & Sustainability FAQ

Transparency is our core material.

Where is the Silk sourced?

We partner with a heritage farm in the Zhejiang province, known for its centuries-old mulberry cultivation. Our partnership ensures fair wages and a pesticide-free environment for the silkworms.

Is your Vegan Leather biodegradable?

While not 100% biodegradable yet, our Sian Leather is 60% bio-based (derived from corn waste) and designed for extreme longevity, reducing the need for replacement.

What about the shipping footprint?

We use carbon-neutral shipping for every order. Our packaging is made from FSC-certified recycled paper and uses soy-based inks, avoiding plastic polybags entirely.

Are the chemicals really 'Clean'?

Yes. We adhere to the ZDHC (Zero Discharge of Hazardous Chemicals) roadmap. This means no harmful substances enter the local water systems during the production of our Kits.
