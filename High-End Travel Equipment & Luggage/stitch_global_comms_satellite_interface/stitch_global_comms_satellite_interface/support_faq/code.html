Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to technical inquiries and common operational hurdles.

How do I align my Zenith Dish for maximum signal strength? expand_more

To achieve optimal signal strength (SNR > 15dB), ensure your terminal has a 110-degree unobstructed view of the sky. Use the Global-Comms mobile app's "Augmented Reality" tool to identify any physical obstructions like foliage or structures.

info Tip: Heavy rain fade can be mitigated by adjusting the dish pitch by +2 degrees during extreme weather events.
What is the difference between Enterprise and Standard tiers? expand_more

Enterprise tiers feature prioritized bandwidth, static IP addressing, and a 99.99% SLA uptime guarantee. Standard tiers operate on a best-effort bandwidth basis with dynamic IP assignments. Both tiers benefit from the same low-latency orbital shell.

How do I reset my network security credentials? expand_more

Navigate to the 'Security' tab in your Zenith Orbit dashboard. Select 'Credential Management' and follow the multi-factor authentication steps. For hard resets, hold the physical pinhole button on the router for 10 seconds.

Can I pause my service during seasonal inactivity? expand_more

Yes, Global-Comms offers a 'Hibernation Mode' for up to 6 months per year. During hibernation, a minimal maintenance fee is applied to preserve your orbital slot and IP reservation. Service can be reactivated instantly via the Account Billing portal.
