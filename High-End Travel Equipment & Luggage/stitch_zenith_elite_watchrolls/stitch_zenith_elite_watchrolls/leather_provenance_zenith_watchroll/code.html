The Soul of the Hide

Full-Grain Italian Leather

"Sourced from the Tuscan foothills, our leather undergoes a century-old vegetable tanning process."

Each hide is hand-selected for its unique character and structural integrity. Unlike mass-produced alternatives, our full-grain leather retains the natural marks of the animal's life, ensuring no two watch rolls are ever identical. Over time, it develops a deep, rich patina that mirrors the heritage of the timepieces it protects.
