The Soul of the Hide

Tracing the lineage of our materials from the rolling hills of Tuscany to the master's bench. Every roll begins with a selection of uncompromised character.

Italian Full-Grain

Our leathers are sourced exclusively from tanneries in the Santa Croce sull'Arno region. Vegetable-tanned using traditional mimosa and chestnut extracts, these hides are not merely treated; they are matured.
