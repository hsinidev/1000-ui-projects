CHAPTER I

Structural Integrity

True preservation begins beneath the surface. We employ master structural engineers who specialize in heritage masonry and timber framing. Every intervention is designed to be reversible, honoring the building's original skeleton while fortifying it for the centuries to come.

REINFORCEMENT

Discrete steel integration that preserves the visual continuity of timber.

FOUNDATION

Advanced stabilization techniques for century-old urban footprints.

CHAPTER II

Artisan Craft

Our network of artisans represents the vanishing guard of traditional trades. From lime-mortar tuckpointing to hand-rubbed oil finishes on reclaimed oak, we celebrate the evidence of the human hand.

Historical Masonry

Restoring the breathability of original clay brick using period-accurate mortar compositions.

Reclaimed Timber

Ethical salvage and restoration of old-growth heart pine and oak for structural and aesthetic use.

Bespoke Ironwork

Custom metal components forged using traditional methods to match original architectural ironmongery.
