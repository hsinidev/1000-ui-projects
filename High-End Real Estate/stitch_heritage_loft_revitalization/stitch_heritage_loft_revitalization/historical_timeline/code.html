HERITAGE LOFT

HISTORICAL CHRONICLE

A Legacy in Stone.

The journey of Heritage Loft began as a textile foundry, evolving through eras of industry to become a sanctuary of modern architectural craft.

1892 THE FOUNDATION

The first stone was laid for the Carrington Foundry, a masterpiece of industrial masonry and ironwork that would define the district's skyline.

1945 THE TRANSITION

Post-war industrial shifts left the halls quiet, echoing with the memories of a bustling past, waiting for its second act.

2024 THE REVITALIZATION

Our current chapter: a meticulous restoration that honors the past while embracing the avant-garde of modern luxury living.
