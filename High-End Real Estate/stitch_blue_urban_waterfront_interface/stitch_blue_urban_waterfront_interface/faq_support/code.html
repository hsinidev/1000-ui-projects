What are the security protocols for the Blue-Urban Marina?

expand_more

Our marina features 24/7 biomechanical perimeter monitoring, encrypted key-fob access for all slips, and a dedicated team of marine safety officers. We utilize advanced sonar-based perimeter alerts to ensure complete environmental security.

How is the residential resilience infrastructure managed?

expand_more

Each residency is integrated into our proprietary Coastal Resilience Grid, featuring automated flood barriers and independent energy desalination systems that activate during extreme environmental events.

Can I schedule a slip maintenance inspection online?

expand_more

Yes, through the Marina Services portal. Residents and slip holders can book dive inspections, hull cleaning, and shore-power diagnostics with 48 hours notice.
