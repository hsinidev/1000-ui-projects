How do I sync my blinds with local astronomical sunrise?

Navigate to Tracking > Environmental Settings and enable 'Astro-Link'. This uses your GPS coordinates to calculate precise lighting shifts throughout the seasons.

Can I create conditional triggers based on room temperature?

Yes. Lumina Elite supports temperature-based automation to optimize HVAC efficiency. Ensure your thermal sensors are calibrated in the Controls tab.
