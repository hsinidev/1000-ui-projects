How do I resolve the 'Sensor Calibration Mismatch' error in Zone 04?

Posted by Senior Engineer, Munich Facility • 2 hours ago

expand_more

The mismatch usually occurs when the ambient humidity exceeds 85% during the initial boot sequence. Manually override the calibration using the console command `sys.recal(z04, true)` or check for debris in the secondary sensor intake.

person

Maintenance Lead, Tokyo

Confirming this worked for our cleanroom units as well. Also recommend checking the grounding pins on the sensor bus.
