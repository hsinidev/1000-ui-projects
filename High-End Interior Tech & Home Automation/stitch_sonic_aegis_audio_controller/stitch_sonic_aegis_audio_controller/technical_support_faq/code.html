Technical FAQ

Deep-dive solutions for common operational queries. Each answer is verified by our senior audio engineers.

Ground loops occur when multiple paths exist between grounding points. To eliminate hum, ensure all devices are connected to a single power conditioner. For Aegis-series hardware, utilize the 'Ground Lift' switch located adjacent to the balanced outputs. If the issue persists, verify the shielding integrity of your interconnects.

For bit-perfect transmission, bypass the Windows Mixer by utilizing the Sonic-Aegis ASIO drivers. In your playback software (Roon, Audirvana, or Foobar2000), select "ASIO: Sonic-Aegis USB Audio" as the output device. Ensure "Exclusive Mode" is checked in Windows Sound Control Panel properties.

Sonic-Aegis Class-A modules are designed to operate at temperatures between 45°C and 60°C. Provide at least 15cm of vertical clearance for passive convection. If the thermal indicator LED turns solid red, the unit has entered protection mode; power down immediately and check ventilation.
