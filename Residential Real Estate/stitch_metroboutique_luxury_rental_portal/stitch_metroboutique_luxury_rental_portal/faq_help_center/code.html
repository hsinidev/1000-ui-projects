Tenants & Guests

How do I request a white-glove airport transfer?

add

All airport logistics can be managed via the 'Concierge' tab in your app. Requests should be submitted at least 24 hours prior to arrival to ensure vehicle availability and security clearance.

What are the protocols for late-night check-ins?

add

Our properties feature smart-lock technology or 24/7 on-site lobby managers. Your unique access code will be activated exactly at your scheduled check-in time.

Can I extend my stay past the initial booking?

add

Extensions are subject to property availability. Please contact your dedicated Residence Manager via the secure messaging portal to inquire about extended occupancy.

Owners & Investors

How is ROI calculated for portfolio properties?

add

We use a proprietary algorithm that accounts for seasonal demand, local market fluctuations, and maintenance overhead to provide a net-yield forecast updated every 24 hours.

What is the vetting process for potential tenants?

add

Each tenant undergoes a multi-stage verification process, including financial solvency checks, identity authentication, and a historical review of previous boutique stays.

How do I access maintenance and repair reports?

add

Real-time maintenance updates and high-resolution photographic documentation are available in your Executive Dashboard under the 'Maintenance' section.
