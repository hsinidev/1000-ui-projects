Frequent Inquiries

How does the Hive-AI predict winter survival?

Our algorithms correlate internal brood temperatures with external meteorological data from the estate's weather station to calculate fat-bee ratios and honey reserve burn rates.

What defines the 'Golden Grade' honey?

Golden Grade is achieved when a single-floral source exceeds 85% of the pollen count and moisture content is naturally capped at exactly 17.2% by the bees.

Can I integrate my estate’s floral calendar?

Yes. The almanac allows you to upload seasonal planting data which our sensors use to anticipate foraging surges and adjust automated harvest schedules.

Why is the hive humming in a D-minor key?

Low-frequency acoustic resonance in minor keys often indicates a 'queenless' state or an impending swarm. Consult the 'Acoustic Diagnosis' section immediately.
