TECHNICAL TROUBLESHOOTING

Pressure fluctuations are typically indicative of thermal expansion or minor air entrapment. Follow these diagnostic steps:

  • Verify manifold pressure matches sensor v4 readout
  • Inspect expansion tank diaphragm integrity
  • Run 'Purge Cycle' from Admin Control panel

To achieve maximum efficiency, the GeoCore algorithm requires precise Delta-T data from your primary loop. If your COP is below 4.2, ensure your ground loops aren't thermally saturated.
