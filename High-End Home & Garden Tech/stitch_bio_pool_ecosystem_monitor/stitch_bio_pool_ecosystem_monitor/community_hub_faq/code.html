quiz Maintenance FAQ

How often should I test the microbial balance? expand_more
For established Bio-Pools, we recommend a deep microbial analysis every 2 weeks during peak summer and once a month during cooler seasons. Our intelligent sensors provide real-time snapshots, but a physical sample ensures the highest precision for bio-film health.
Is it normal to see slight algae growth in the regeneration zone? expand_more
Yes! A small amount of filamentous algae in the regeneration zone is actually a sign of a healthy nutrient cycle. It means your aquatic plants and micro-organisms are actively processing phosphates. However, if growth spreads to the swimming zone, check your phosphate levels immediately.
What aquatic plants are best for high-calcium water? expand_more
For hard water environments, we recommend Phragmites australis or Typha latifolia. These species are highly resilient and thrive in mineral-rich conditions while providing excellent filtration properties.
