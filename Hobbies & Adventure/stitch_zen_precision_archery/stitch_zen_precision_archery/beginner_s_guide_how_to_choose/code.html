The Geometry of Form

Archery is a relationship between your body's geometry and the bow's physics.

Step 01: DRAW LENGTH

Measure your wingspan (tip-to-tip) and divide by 2.5. This ensures the bow's peak performance matches your reach.

Step 02: DRAW WEIGHT

Beginners should start between 15-25 lbs for recurve or 30-40 lbs for compound. Precision comes from control, not force.
