Tying Instructions

01

Foundation

Secure the hook in the vice. Start the thread at the 2/3 point of the hook shank and wrap back to the bend. Ensure even tension to create a smooth surface for dubbing.

02

Wing Placement

Select two Light Dun CDC feathers. Tie them in with the tips pointing forward over the eye. Secure with several firm wraps and then pull them upright to create the silhouette.

03

Abdomen Construction

Apply a thin layer of PMD Superfine Dubbing to the thread. Form a tapered abdomen from the bend of the hook up to the base of the wing.

04

Thorax and Finish

Add a small amount of dubbing for the thorax area. Wrap behind and in front of the CDC wings to support them. Whip finish and apply a tiny drop of head cement.
