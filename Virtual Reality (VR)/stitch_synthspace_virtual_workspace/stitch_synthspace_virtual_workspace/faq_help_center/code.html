How do I invite external collaborators to a VR Room?

expand_less
You can invite external guests by generating a 'Guest Access Link' from the Room Settings panel. Guests can join via browser (2D mode) or via supported VR headsets without needing a full SynthSpace license for up to 24 hours. Ensure you have 'Public Lobby' toggled off for security.
