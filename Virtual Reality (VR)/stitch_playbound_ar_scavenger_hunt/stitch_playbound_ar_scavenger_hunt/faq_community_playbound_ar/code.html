Player Knowledge Base

How do I calibrate my AR reticle? expand_more

Ensure you are in a well-lit environment. Open the Command Center, select 'Map', and tap the crosshair icon in the top-right corner. Follow the on-screen geometric prompts to sync your physical location with the AR grid.

What is the "Neon Protocol" active status? expand_more

Neon Protocol status indicates a global 2x XP event is currently live. During this period, all vault captures and rank points are doubled. This protocol usually triggers during peak community activity cycles.

How do I join a competitive faction? expand_more

Once you reach Rank Level 5, the 'Vault' menu will unlock Faction selection. Choose between Cyber-Dragons, Shadow-Runners, or Neon-Knights to unlock exclusive team-based augmented challenges.
