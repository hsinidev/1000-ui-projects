Frequently Asked Questions

How long should a typical VR session last?

expand_more

For most users, we recommend starting with 15-20 minute sessions twice a day. As your neural stamina increases, these can be extended to 45 minutes. It is vital to listen to your body—if you feel any visual fatigue or dizziness, utilize the "Gentle Exit" button in your headset immediately.

What if I have limited physical space?

expand_more

NeuroVis is designed with a "Stationary Mode" specifically for users with limited mobility or space. You only need a 1.5m x 1.5m clear area to safely perform all guided exercises. Ensure there are no low-hanging light fixtures or sharp furniture corners within arm's reach.

Is the headset safe for my vision?

expand_more

Our software incorporates "Biological Blue-Light Filtration" and maintains a constant 90Hz refresh rate to minimize eye strain and prevent motion sickness. We also include guided eye-relaxation intervals every 10 minutes within the virtual environment.

Can I sync my progress with my doctor?

expand_more

Yes, through the "Care Connect" portal, you can generate a bi-weekly neurological progress report. This PDF includes heatmaps of your motor response accuracy and neural engagement metrics, which can be securely shared with your therapist or neurologist.
