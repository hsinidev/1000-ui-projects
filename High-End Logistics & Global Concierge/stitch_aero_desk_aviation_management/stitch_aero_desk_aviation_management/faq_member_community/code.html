Member Essentials

How do I verify aircraft safety standards?

add

Every aircraft in the Aero-Desk fleet undergoes a rigorous 200-point inspection exceeding FAA Part 135 requirements. We partner exclusively with ARG/US Platinum and Wyvern Wingman rated operators.

Membership vs. Ad-hoc Chartering?

add

While ad-hoc chartering offers ultimate flexibility, membership unlocks fixed hourly rates, guaranteed recovery in case of mechanical issues, and priority booking on peak days.

What is the Concierge response time?

add

Elite members receive a dedicated concierge agent with a guaranteed response time of under 15 minutes, 24/7, for all flight adjustments and catering requests.

Can I request specific cabin crew?

add

Yes. Our platform allows you to profile your preferred cabin crew and flight deck officers. We prioritize these assignments based on aircraft availability.
