How do I activate the global SOS signal? expand_more
The SOS signal can be initiated via the primary sidebar button, your physical AETHER token, or voice command through the encrypted mobile link. Once triggered, a Tier 1 Response Team is dispatched to your GPS coordinates within 120 seconds, and local law enforcement is notified through our private diplomatic channels.
What defines 'Platinum' level confidentiality? expand_more
Platinum confidentiality includes zero-knowledge storage of your personal data, masked financial transactions for all service fees, and an NDA structure that is legally binding across 140 jurisdictions. Your identity is never stored in plaintext on any networked server.
Can I grant secondary access to family members? expand_more
Yes. Primary members may nominate up to three (3) secondary users. Each nominee must undergo a standard security vetting process. Once approved, they receive a 'Silver' tier sub-membership with full access to travel and emergency services.
