Chef's FAQ

How do you guarantee the provenance of Alba White Truffles? expand_more
Every specimen is vetted by our local scouts in Piedmont. We provide a digital certificate of origin and a GPS-logged harvest timeline, ensuring that from the moment of discovery by the trifolau, the chain of custody remains unbroken and chilled.
What is the logistics protocol for Grade A5 Wagyu shipping? expand_more
We utilize custom nitrogen-cooled canisters that maintain a constant -2°C. Real-time telemetry is available to the Chef de Cuisine, reporting internal humidity and temperature every 15 minutes during the trans-Pacific transit.
Can we request bespoke sourcing for extinct or rare cultivars? expand_more
Our Concierge tier offers an "Archaeological Sourcing" service. We work with heirloom seed banks and boutique farmers in the Loire Valley and Kyōto to revive specific flavor profiles for exclusive tasting menus.
