Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data truly anonymous and private? expand_more
Yes. WellnessWare uses end-to-end encryption. Your employer only receives aggregated, de-identified data. We never share individual usage patterns or personal health information.
How does the 'Quick Exit' button work? expand_more
The Quick Exit button instantly navigates your browser to a neutral page (like Google Search) and clears your current session data from the browser's active memory for your safety.
Can I talk to a real person through the app? expand_more
Absolutely. Our 'Coach' and 'Crisis Chat' features connect you with licensed professionals or trained crisis counselors, depending on your current needs and subscription level.
What should I do in a medical emergency? expand_more
WellnessWare is a support tool, not a replacement for emergency medical services. In a life-threatening emergency, please dial your local emergency services (e.g., 911 or 999) immediately.

Still have questions?

Our dedicated support team is available via secure message 24/7 to help you find the right path forward.
