What is the SkillMap methodology? expand_more

SkillMap utilizes a proprietary data-driven framework to identify skill gaps based on industry benchmarks. We analyze your current profile against desired career paths to provide a structured roadmap for professional progression, ensuring every learning module is relevant to your ultimate goals.

How are mentors vetted? expand_more

All mentors undergo a rigorous four-stage verification process, including deep-dive technical interviews, portfolio review, and educational background checks. Only practitioners with at least 10 years of experience in their respective fields are invited to the platform.

Do you offer corporate invoicing? expand_more

Yes, our Enterprise tier supports direct invoicing, purchase orders, and multi-user seat management. Contact our sales department to set up a centralized billing account for your organization.

What is your refund policy for certifications? expand_more

SkillMap offers a full refund within 14 days of purchase, provided that less than 10% of the course material has been accessed. Examination fees are non-refundable once the test voucher has been redeemed.
