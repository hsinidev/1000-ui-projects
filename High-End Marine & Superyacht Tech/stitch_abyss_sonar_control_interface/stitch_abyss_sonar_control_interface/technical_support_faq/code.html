Life-Support Diagnostics

DOC-441-B VERIFIED

At extreme pressures, the primary sensors may experience a drift of +/- 3%. To recalibrate:

  • 01. Enter the Engineering terminal and execute command sys.cal_scrub --force.
  • 02. Wait for the pressure equalization valve to cycle (approx. 45 seconds).
  • 03. Cross-reference digital readings with the analog gauge on the port side panel.
