FREQUENTLY ASKED PROTOCOLS

How do I force a satellite re-acquisition? expand_more

Navigate to Hardware Health > VSAT Control > Advanced and select 'Recalibrate Azimuth'. Ensure the yacht is at a stable heading for at least 3 minutes during the procedure.

Latency spikes on Guest Wi-Fi 2? expand_more

Check for internal RF interference from deck galley microwave units. If persistent, adjust the channel frequency to 5.8GHz within the Network Security dashboard.

Updating the Crew VPN credentials? expand_more

Global rotation occurs every 30 days. New tokens are distributed via the secure Fleet Dashboard messaging system. Admins can manually reset tokens in 'Security Settings'.
