Autograft

The gold standard in bone grafting. Tissue is harvested from the patient's own body, typically from the iliac crest or mandibular ramus, ensuring maximum histocompatibility.

  • check_circle Osteoinductive & Osteogenic potential
  • check_circle Zero risk of immunological rejection

Allograft

Processed human cadaveric bone. Available in mineralized and demineralized forms. Undergoes rigorous sterilization to eliminate pathogenic risks.

Sterilization: Gamma Irradiation 99.9% Purity
