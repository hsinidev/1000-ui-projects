At Sage Periodontics, we believe the path to dental restoration should be as serene as the results. Discover our step-by-step approach to dental implants, where clinical precision meets patient-centered care.

The Restoration Path

Step 01

Discovery & Consultation

We begin with a comprehensive digital assessment. Utilizing advanced 3D imaging, we map your unique anatomy to ensure every implant is placed with absolute surgical precision.

1
2
Step 02

Surgical Excellence

The implant placement is performed in our tranquil surgical suite. We prioritize your comfort, offering tailored sedation options to ensure a completely stress-free experience.

Step 03

Restorative Integration

During the healing period, the implant naturally integrates with your bone. We monitor this "restoration window" closely to ensure a solid, lifelong foundation for your new tooth.

3
4
Step 04

The Final Reveal

Finally, your custom-crafted porcelain restoration is attached. The result is a tooth that looks, feels, and functions perfectly, restoring both your smile and your confidence.
