Dental Implants

Are implants right for me?

Implants are ideal for patients with healthy gums and enough bone to support the post. We utilize advanced 3D scanning to determine eligibility with 99% accuracy.

What is the recovery time?

While the initial healing takes 7-10 days, the osseointegration process takes 3-6 months. Most patients return to work the very next day.
