Which international insurance providers do you accept?

We partner with over 45 global insurance firms including Cigna Global, Allianz Care, and AXA. Our billing department handles direct settlement for most major inpatient and complex outpatient procedures.

How do I verify my coverage before my visit?

Simply upload a digital scan of your insurance card via the Medical Records portal or contact our 24/7 verification line with your policy number.
