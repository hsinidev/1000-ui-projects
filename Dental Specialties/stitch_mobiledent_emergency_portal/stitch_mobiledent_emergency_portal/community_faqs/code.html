Our pricing is transparent and competitive with traditional clinics. A standard on-site visit starts at $150 plus procedure costs. We provide a full quote before any work begins. No hidden travel fees.

MobileDent is an in-network provider for most major PPO plans including Delta Dental, MetLife, and Cigna. We also offer simplified claim filing for out-of-network benefits.

Emergency dispatches aim for arrival within 60-90 minutes of your call. Scheduled appointments have a 15-minute arrival window, guaranteed. You can track your dentist's live location via our app.

We offer full-service dental care including hygiene cleanings, fillings, root canals, extractions, and crown fittings. Our mobile units are essentially "clinics on wheels" with full X-ray and sterilization capabilities.
