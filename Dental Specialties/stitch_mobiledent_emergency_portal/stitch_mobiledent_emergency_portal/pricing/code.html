Billing & Logistics FAQ

Is there a travel fee? expand_more
No. Our listed prices are all-inclusive for our standard service area. We believe in "clinical transparency"—what you see is exactly what you pay.
How do I pay on-site? expand_more
Our mobile vans are equipped with encrypted card readers. We accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay, as well as HSA/FSA cards.

We require payment at the time of service to maintain our rapid-response model. However, we provide an itemized ADA-coded superbill that you can upload to your insurance portal for direct reimbursement.
