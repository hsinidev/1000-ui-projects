While You Wait

01

Rinse Gently

Rinse your mouth with warm salt water to clean the area and reduce bacterial count.

02

Apply Pressure

If bleeding, bite down firmly on a clean gauze pad or a moistened tea bag for 15-20 minutes.

03

Cold Compress

Apply an ice pack to the outside of your cheek to reduce swelling and numb the pain.

04

Preserve Teeth

Place any knocked-out teeth in a small container of milk. Do not scrub the tooth root.
