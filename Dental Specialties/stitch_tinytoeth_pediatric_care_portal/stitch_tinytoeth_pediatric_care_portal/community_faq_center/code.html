When should my child first see a dentist?

expand_more
We recommend the "First Visit by First Birthday" rule. Seeing a dentist early helps acclimate your child to the environment and allows us to provide preventative guidance.

How can I help my child avoid "dentist jitters"?

expand_more
We use positive reinforcement, child-friendly language, and "Tell-Show-Do" techniques to make every visit feel like an adventure rather than a chore.
