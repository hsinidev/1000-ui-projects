# NeoVault: Next-Gen Personal Banking PRD

## Aesthetic
Minimalist-Luxe / Deep Charcoal (#121212), Electric Blue (#007AFF), and Silver (#C0C0C0).

## UX Specifications
- **Glassmorphism**: Apply glassmorphism effects (blur, border, transparency) to card elements.
- **Privacy Mode**: Include a toggle that blurs sensitive balances across the app.
- **Mobile UX**: Premium wallet feel with haptic-ready navigation and biometric login styling.

## Required Pages
1. **Account Overview**: Bento-style layout showing total balance, quick actions, and recent activity.
2. **Transaction History**: List view with AI-categorization (e.g., icons for Dining, Transport, etc.).
3. **Savings Goals**: Visual progress tracking for multiple goals.
4. **Virtual Card Management**: Display and management of virtual cards with glassmorphic styling.
5. **Biometric Login (Additional)**: To capture the requested biometric login styling.
