The Provenance

1745

Commissioned by the Comte de Vaudreuil

Originally commissioned for the private study of the Château de Vaudreuil. Attributed to the workshop of Jean-François Oeben, the King's cabinetmaker.

1922

The Rothschild Collection

Acquired by Baroness Edmond de Rothschild and relocated to London. Recorded in the 1922 estate inventory as a 'masterpiece of the transition period'.

2024

Modern-Heritage Curation

Expertly restored by our Parisian atelier, preserving all original structural integrity and gilding while refreshing the leather inlay to museum standards.
