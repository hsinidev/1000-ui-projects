Monolith House

An exploration of mass and void. The Monolith House stands as a testament to reductionist principles, where structural honesty meets the raw landscape of the coastal cliffside. Every axis is calculated to frame the shifting light throughout the vernal equinox.

Location Cabo de Gata, Spain
Client Private Commission
Area 420 m²
01 / Structural Integrity

Materiality

The use of board-marked concrete allows the structure to age with the environment. Over time, the salt spray from the ocean will etch itself into the facade, creating a living record of the house's existence.
