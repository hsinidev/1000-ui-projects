ELITE LEVEL 12 WEEK BLOCK

HYPERION
STRENGTH

The definitive protocol for metabolic conditioning and maximal strength output. Engineered for athletes who demand a higher standard of performance.

PROGRAM ARCHITECTURE

FREQUENCY 5 DAYS/WK
INTENSITY 90% RPE
DURATION 75 MIN
FOCUS POWER

HYPERION is not a casual routine. It is a systematic progression designed to shatter plateaus through heavy compound movements paired with explosive plyometric finishers. You will focus on three primary phases: Adaption, Hypertrophy, and Maximal Output.

WEEKLY PROTOCOL

WARM-UP (15 MIN)

Dynamic Mobility A

Targeting hip flexors, posterior chain, and thoracic spine expansion.

MAIN LIFT (35 MIN)

Back Squat / Sumo DL

Progressive overload. 5 sets of 3 at 85% 1RM. 180s rest interval.

ACCESSORY (25 MIN)

Bulgarian Splits

3 x 12 reps per leg. Focus on eccentric control and stability.
