SYNTHESIS VR

A hyper-sensory spatial installation exploring the intersection of biological feedback and algorithmic generation. Built for the New Museum's digital wing, Synthesis VR uses real-time neural data to construct infinite procedural landscapes.

THE_PROCESS

The core challenge of Synthesis VR was maintaining 60FPS while rendering over 2 million dynamically displaced vertices. Our solution involved a custom GPGPU (General-Purpose Computing on Graphics Processing Units) pipeline that offloads physics calculations directly to the fragment shader.

Every frame is a direct translation of the user's pulse and galvanic skin response, ensuring that no two sessions are ever identical. The visual language was inspired by early vector displays, re-imagined through the lens of modern refractive glassmorphism and volumetric lighting.
