Scholarly Deep-Dive

The Symbology of Wisteria in Demon Slayer Lore

In the world of Kimetsu no Yaiba, the Wisteria flower acts as a crucial barrier between the human world and the demonic night. But its roots in Taisho-era folklore go much deeper.

Historically, Wisteria has been associated with longevity and immortality, ironic given its lethal nature to the "immortal" demons. Our research explores the floral motifs used by Koyoharu Gotouge to represent the cyclical nature of life and memory.
