Heirloom Tomato & Basil Tart with Saffron Infusion

Prep Time 25 MIN
Cook Time 45 MIN
Difficulty INTERMEDIATE

Ingredients

Readability
Scale Recipe Serves 4
Puff Pastry (Chilled)
320g
Heirloom Tomatoes
450g
check
Dijon Mustard
2 tbsp
Saffron Threads
1 pinch
Fresh Basil Leaves
1/2 cup
Extra Virgin Olive Oil
3 tbsp

Method

01

Pastry Foundation

Roll out the chilled puff pastry on a lightly floured surface to a 3mm thickness. Gently transfer to a parchment-lined baking sheet and prick the base with a fork. Spread a thin, even layer of Dijon mustard across the center, leaving a 2cm border for the crust to rise.

02

Tomato Preparation

Slice the heirloom tomatoes into 5mm rounds. Place them on paper towels and sprinkle lightly with sea salt to draw out excess moisture. Let sit for 10 minutes, then pat dry. This ensures a crisp pastry base that isn't overwhelmed by juices during the bake.

03

The Saffron Infusion

Crush the saffron threads in a small bowl and mix with the extra virgin olive oil. Arrange the tomato slices in overlapping concentric circles on the pastry. Brush the tomatoes generously with the saffron-infused oil and season with freshly cracked black pepper.
