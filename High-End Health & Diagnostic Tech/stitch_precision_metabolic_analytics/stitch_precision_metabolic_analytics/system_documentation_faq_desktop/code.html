01.1

How is the glycemic variability (GV) index calculated?

expand_more

GV is determined using the Coefficient of Variation (CV) method. The platform calculates the standard deviation of blood glucose readings over a 24-hour period divided by the mean glucose value.

CV% = (Standard Deviation / Mean Glucose) * 100

Note: A CV under 36% is considered "Stable" within the Blood-Logic architecture.
