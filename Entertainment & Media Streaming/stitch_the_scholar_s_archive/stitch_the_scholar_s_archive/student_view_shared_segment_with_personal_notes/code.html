Lecture Notes

Timestamp 04:22

The visual weight of the Barbican structure is intentional; it signifies a break from the 'fragility' of post-war reconstruction.

Timestamp 04:38

Focus on the interplay of natural light against the bush-hammered concrete surfaces. This is where materiality meets metaphysics.
