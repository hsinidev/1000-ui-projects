Synopsis

In a world where memories manifest as crystalline structures known as 'Echoes', Kael is a scavenger born without the ability to hear them. When a catastrophic event shatters the Great Spire, the very fabric of reality begins to unravel. Kael discovers he can manipulate the jagged shards of these broken memories, setting him on a path to reclaim a stolen past that might not even belong to him. As he navigates through fractured landscapes and faces entities born of forgotten nightmares, he must decide if some echoes are better left silenced.
