The Silent Horizon

Director

Elena Voss

Duration

94 Minutes

Origin

Iceland / UK

A visual meditation on the nature of solitude and the sound of silence in a world that never stops moving.

Captured entirely on 65mm film, "The Silent Horizon" explores the intersection of geography and psychology. Elena Voss takes the audience through the stark, unforgiving landscapes of the Icelandic Highlands, using long takes and a minimalist soundscape to challenge our modern perception of time.
