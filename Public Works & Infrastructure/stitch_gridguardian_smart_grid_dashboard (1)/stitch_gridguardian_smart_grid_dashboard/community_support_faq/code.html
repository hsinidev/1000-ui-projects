Grid Basics FAQ

How do I synchronize my local meter with System Alpha? expand_more
Synchronization requires a hardware key (v2.0+) and an active encrypted connection. Navigate to Settings > Devices > Add Device and follow the automated handshake protocol. Ensure your GridGuardian firmware is up to date before attempting pairing.
What does the "Orange Pulse" indicator mean? expand_more
The Orange Pulse indicates a peak load notification for your specific grid segment. It is a predictive cue suggesting demand management might be required within the next 15 minutes.
How is renewable credit calculated? expand_more
Credits are calculated in real-time based on your contribution to the grid during peak windows. Check the "Renewables" tab for a detailed breakdown of your production-to-consumption ratio.
