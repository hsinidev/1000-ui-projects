Disposal Protocols

Schedule bulk pickups through the "Route Tracking" dashboard. Items must be placed at the curb by 6:00 AM on your scheduled date. Maximum 3 items per household.

Paint, chemicals, and electronics must be brought to the Municipal Transfer Station. Do NOT place these in standard bins.

Use certified biodegradable bags only. No plastics or non-organic materials are allowed in the green stream containers.
