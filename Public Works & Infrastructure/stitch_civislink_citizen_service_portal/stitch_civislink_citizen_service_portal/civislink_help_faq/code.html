Permitting

4 Questions
How do I apply for a residential building permit? expand_more

To apply for a residential building permit, log into your CityConnect dashboard, select 'New Application', and choose 'Residential Building'. You will need to upload your site plans and proof of ownership. Most residential applications are reviewed within 5-10 business days.

Can I track my permit status online? expand_more

Yes, all permit applications can be tracked via the 'Permit Queue' section in your dashboard. You will receive automated email and SMS notifications at each stage of the approval process.

Billing & Payments

When are property taxes due? add

Property taxes are due semi-annually on March 31st and September 30th.

Is there a fee for online payments? add

ACH transfers are free. Credit/Debit card payments incur a 2.5% processing fee.

Waste & Recycling

What items are recyclable? add

Clean cardboard, plastics #1-5, and aluminum cans are accepted.

How do I request a bulk pickup? add

Log into Services and select 'Request Bulk Waste' to schedule a curbside pickup.

Resetting your password expand_more

Click 'Forgot Password' on the login screen. An email link will be sent to your registered address. For security reasons, the link expires in 20 minutes.

Linking multiple properties expand_more

In 'Account Settings', select 'Manage Properties' to add additional parcels by tax ID or address. Verificaton may take 24-48 hours.
