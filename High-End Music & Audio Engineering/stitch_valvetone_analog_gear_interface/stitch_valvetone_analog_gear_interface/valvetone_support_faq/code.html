Input gain must be matched to the peak output of your interface. For optimal harmonic saturation, aim for a peak digital input of -12dBFS. The analog stage will translate this to a nominal 0VU on the hardware meters.

The ValveTone SV-series uses matched pairs of 12AX7 and 6L6 tubes. Under standard studio use (8 hours daily), we recommend testing emission levels every 12 months.
