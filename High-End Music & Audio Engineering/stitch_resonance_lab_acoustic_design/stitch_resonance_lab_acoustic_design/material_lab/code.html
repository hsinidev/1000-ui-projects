MEASUREMENT STANDARDS

All materials undergo rigorous testing in our reverberation chambers following ASTM C423 and ISO 354 standards. Noise Reduction Coefficient (NRC) is calculated as the average of absorption coefficients at 250, 500, 1000, and 2000 Hz.
