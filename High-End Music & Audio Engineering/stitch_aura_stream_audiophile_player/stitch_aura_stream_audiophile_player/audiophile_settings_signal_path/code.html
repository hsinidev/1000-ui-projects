The Philosophy of Pure Sound

The Bit-Perfect engine is designed to remove the "veil" between the recording studio and your ears. Modern operating systems frequently re-sample audio to a common frequency, introducing quantization errors and jitter. By taking exclusive control of the hardware, we ensure that every bit of data is delivered to your DAC exactly as the artist intended.
