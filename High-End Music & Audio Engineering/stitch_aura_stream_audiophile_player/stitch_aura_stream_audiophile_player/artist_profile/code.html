About the Artist

Based in Stockholm, Elowen Thorne has redefined the boundaries of contemporary ambient music. Her work is characterized by a "sculptural" approach to frequency, utilizing custom-built analog oscillators and traditional orchestral instruments to create massive, immersive soundscapes.
