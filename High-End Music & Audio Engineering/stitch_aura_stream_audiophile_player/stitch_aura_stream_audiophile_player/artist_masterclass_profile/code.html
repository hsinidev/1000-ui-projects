ABOUT THE ARTIST

Elowen Thorne is a classically trained pianist and electronic composer whose work bridges the gap between traditional orchestration and modern synthesis.

Born in Reykjavik and now based in Berlin, her soundscapes are noted for their architectural precision and emotional depth. Thorne’s latest project, 'Nocturnal Sketches', was recorded using a 1920s Steinway in a deconsecrated cathedral, capturing the unique acoustic decay of the space.
