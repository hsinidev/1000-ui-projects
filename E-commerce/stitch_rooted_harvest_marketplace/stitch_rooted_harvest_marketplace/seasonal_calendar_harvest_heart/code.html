Storage & Preparation

Make your harvest last longer and taste better

kitchen

Root Veg Care

Store carrots and beets in the crisper drawer with greens removed to prevent moisture loss from the root.

skillet

Perfect Roasting

Cut squash into uniform cubes and toss with cold-pressed olive oil for an even, caramelized finish at 400°F.

sanitizer

Leafy Longevity

Wash greens in cold water, spin dry, and store in a breathable cotton bag to keep them crisp for up to a week.
