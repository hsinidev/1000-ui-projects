Shipping & Delivery

What are your international shipping regions? expand_more

Gala-Pour facilitates premium white-glove delivery to over 45 countries, specializing in European, North American, and Asian-Pacific luxury hubs. Each shipment is climate-controlled and insured at full valuation to ensure the integrity of your rare spirits.

Do you offer same-day delivery? expand_more

Complimentary same-day delivery is available within the metropolitan areas of London, New York, Paris, and Dubai for orders placed before 2:00 PM local time. This service is executed by our private courier fleet.

Custom Engraving

Can all bottles be personalized? expand_more

Most of our signature crystal decanters and heavy-glass bottles are eligible for laser engraving or 24k gold leaf inlay. Rare vintage bottles with historical labels may be restricted to personalized wooden presentation cases instead.
