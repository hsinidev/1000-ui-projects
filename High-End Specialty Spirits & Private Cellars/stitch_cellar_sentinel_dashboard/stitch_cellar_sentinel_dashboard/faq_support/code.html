sensors SENSOR CALIBRATION PROTOCOLS

v2.4.0-STABLE
01. HYGROMETER DRIFT CORRECTION expand_more

Drift correction requires a saturated salt solution environment (75% RH). Follow strict adherence to the sequence:

  • STEP_A: Place CT-H1 sensor in airtight chamber for 24 hours.
  • STEP_B: Navigate to [Sensors > CT-H1 > Maintenance > Calibrate].
  • STEP_C: Enter offset value: OFFSET_VAL = (75.0 - CURRENT_READING)
02. CO2 SENSOR ZERO-POINT RESET expand_more

Manual zero-point calibration for the NDIR CO2 module should only be performed in fresh outdoor air (approx. 400ppm). Do not calibrate during fermentation peaks.

03. THERMAL PROBE RESISTANCE SYNC expand_more

Syncing probe resistance for CT-T5 series. Ensure lead-wire resistance (LWR) is accounted for in the system bridge settings.
