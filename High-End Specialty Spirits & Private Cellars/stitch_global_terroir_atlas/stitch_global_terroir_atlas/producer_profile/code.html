The Heritage of Sand & Stone

Established in 1842, Domaine de la Clarté has remained a family-held treasure for six generations. Our story is not just one of winemaking, but of stewardship. We believe that the most profound wines are those that act as a vessel for the soil, reflecting the specific minerals and microclimates of our unique hillside location.

Our approach to viticulture is strictly organic, utilizing biodynamic preparations to enhance the natural vitality of our vines. By eschewing synthetic interventions, we allow the intricate tapestry of the Rhone terroir to express itself fully in every bottle.
