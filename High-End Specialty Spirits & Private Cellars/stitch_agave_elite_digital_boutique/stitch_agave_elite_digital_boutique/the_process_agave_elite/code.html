ARTISANAL COOKING

72 Hours of Patience

Our masonry ovens use steam and stone to convert starch into honeyed agave nectar over three days of steady heat.

water_drop

Deep Well Water

Pure volcanic water drawn from 200 meters below the distillery, providing the mineral backbone of our spirit.

Hourglass

Copper Distillation

Small-batch copper pot stills refine the spirit twice, preserving the bright, botanical notes of the high-altitude agave.

Sustainable Legacy

For every agave harvested, we plant three new hijuelos. Our process is circular, returning organic agave fibers back to the earth as compost to nourish the next generation.
