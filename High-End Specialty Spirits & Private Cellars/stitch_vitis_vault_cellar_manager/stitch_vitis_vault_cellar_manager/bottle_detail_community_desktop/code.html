The Heritage

The 2015 vintage marks the 200th anniversary of the iconic neo-palladian villa at Château Margaux. This year yielded a wine of profound elegance and structural perfection. Combining the power of the 2005 with the aromatic complexity of the 2010, it is widely considered one of the finest wines produced by the estate in the modern era.
