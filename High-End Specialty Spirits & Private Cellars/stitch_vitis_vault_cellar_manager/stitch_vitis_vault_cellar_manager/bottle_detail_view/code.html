The Heritage

The 2015 Château Margaux is a historic vintage, marking the 200th anniversary of the completion of the iconic "Versailles of the Médoc." This year also represents the final masterpiece of the late Paul Pontallier, the estate’s legendary technical director who shaped the winery's modern identity over three decades.

Known for its ethereal floral aromatics and unparalleled elegance, this vintage saw a perfect growing season in the Margaux appellation. The wine is a blend of 87% Cabernet Sauvignon, 8% Merlot, 3% Cabernet Franc, and 2% Petit Verdot, aged in 100% new French oak.
