TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

01

How does Spirit-Archive verify physical assets?

Every bottle is embedded with a tamper-proof NFC chip that stores a unique cryptographic key. This key is linked to a non-fungible token (NFT) on the Ethereum blockchain, containing the asset's full providence and sensory data.

02

What happens if the physical seal is broken?

The NFC chip's antenna is integrated into the bottle's seal. Once broken, the cryptographic continuity is severed. The blockchain entry for the specific asset ID will automatically update its status from 'Sealed' to 'Liquidated'.

03

Can I trade assets without shipping them?

Yes. Assets can remain in our climate-controlled 'Hardened Vaults'. Only the digital certificate (NFT) changes ownership. The physical asset is released only upon the 'Redeem' protocol execution by the current verified holder.

04

Is the data accessible to the public?

Asset providence is public and immutable on-chain. However, specific private comments and tasting protocols are reserved for the 'Verified Collector' network, requiring a signature from a whitelisted wallet.
