Pot Still vs. Column Still: The V&C Choice? expand_more
Our commitment to traditional pot still distillation ensures a heavier, more complex spirit. By retaining more congeners from the fermented mash, we achieve the characteristic 'oily' mouthfeel and deep fruit notes associated with our premium reserves. Column stills may be efficient, but our pot stills are essential for character.
Understanding the Char Level 4 Barrel expand_more
Commonly known as the 'alligator char,' Level 4 involves firing the white oak for 55 seconds. This creates deep fissures in the wood, allowing the spirit to penetrate further into the caramelized sugars beneath the carbon layer, resulting in intense vanilla and smoke profiles.
