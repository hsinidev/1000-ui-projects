Authentication & Security

How do we verify the provenance of rare bourbon bottles? expand_more
Our multi-step authentication process involves physical inspection by master distillers, chemical analysis of liquid markers where non-invasive methods permit, and rigorous paper trail verification. Every bottle in the Amber-Rare exchange is assigned a unique digital certificate of authenticity backed by our liability guarantee.
Is my digital vault secure against cyber threats? expand_more
We utilize military-grade encryption and multi-signature cold storage protocols for all digital ownership records. Your assets are mirrored on private ledger systems to ensure transparency and absolute protection against unauthorized access.

Shipping & Insurance

How are high-value bottles transported? expand_more
All shipments are handled via climate-controlled courier services specialized in fragile, high-value cargo. Each bottle is individually packed in custom-molded, vibration-dampening cases and monitored by GPS and temperature sensors throughout transit.
Are the bottles insured during storage? expand_more
Yes. Every asset held in our partner vaults is fully insured for its current market value plus a 10% premium for replacement difficulty. Coverage includes fire, theft, and accidental breakage.
