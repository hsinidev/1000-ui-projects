The Craft: Forging Liquid Gold

A deep dive into the ancestral methods of distillation that define the Amber-Rare standard.

The Alchemy of Copper

Every drop of Amber-Rare begins its journey in the embrace of hand-hammered copper. Unlike modern stainless steel, copper actively interacts with the spirit, stripping away unwanted sulfur compounds and imparting a characteristic silkiness.

Our master coppersmiths maintain stills that have seen three generations of distillers. The "swan neck" design—a closely guarded secret—determines the exact reflux required for our signature profile.
