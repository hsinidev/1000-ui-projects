Surgical Protocols

What makes Titanium robotic surgery different?

expand_more
Titanium Ortho utilizes a proprietary haptic-feedback navigation system that limits the surgical field to within 0.5mm of the pre-operative plan, ensuring sub-millimeter precision that is physically impossible for the human hand alone.

How long do robotic-assisted joint replacements last?

expand_more
Due to the extreme accuracy in alignment and component positioning, we project a 25% increase in longevity compared to manual procedures, targeting a 25-30 year functional lifespan for most titanium-alloy implants.

Is general anesthesia required for robotic procedures?

expand_more
Protocol varies by patient. However, many of our precision-guided procedures can be performed under regional blocks and light sedation, significantly reducing recovery time and systemic stress.
