LASIK vs. PRK: Engineering Perfect Vision

A scientific deep-dive into the two gold standards of refractive surgery. Discover which precision path aligns with your unique physiological needs.

visibility

LASIK

Laser-Assisted In Situ Keratomileusis involves creating a microscopic thin flap in the cornea to reshape the underlying tissue.

  • check_circle Immediate visual recovery (usually 24 hours).
  • check_circle Minimal post-operative discomfort.
  • check_circle Ideal for patients with standard corneal thickness.
biotech

PRK

Photorefractive Keratectomy involves removing the epithelium (outer layer) of the cornea before the laser reshaping process.

  • check_circle No risk of flap-related complications.
  • check_circle Superior for patients with thin or irregular corneas.
  • check_circle Preferred choice for high-impact athletes.
