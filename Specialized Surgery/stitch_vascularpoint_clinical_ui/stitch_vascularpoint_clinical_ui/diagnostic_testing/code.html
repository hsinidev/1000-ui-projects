Vascular Ultrasound (Duplex)

The gold standard for visualizing venous and arterial structures. This high-frequency sound wave technology allows our surgeons to identify blockages, aneurysms, and blood clots with microscopic accuracy. It provides dynamic, real-time data on the velocity and direction of blood flow.

ABI Testing

Ankle-Brachial Index (ABI) measures blood pressure at the ankle and arm to assess for Peripheral Artery Disease (PAD). It is a vital screening tool for circulatory health.
