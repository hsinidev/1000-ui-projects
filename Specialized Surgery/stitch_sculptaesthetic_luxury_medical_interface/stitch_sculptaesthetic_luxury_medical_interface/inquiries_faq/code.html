Safety & Clinical Excellence

What clinical standards govern SCULPTAESTHETIC procedures?

expand_more

Our atelier operates under rigorous triple-A (AAAASF) accreditation standards. Every procedure is performed in a state-of-the-art surgical suite that mimics the sterile precision of top-tier medical institutions, while maintaining the comfort of a private lounge. Our surgical staff consists exclusively of board-certified specialists with over 15 years of individual experience.

How do you manage anesthesia for outpatient procedures?

expand_more

We employ board-certified MD Anesthesiologists for every case requiring sedation. Unlike many clinics that use CRNAs, we believe the presence of a physician anesthesiologist provides an unparalleled level of safety and precise monitoring, ensuring your comfort from the first minute to full awakening.

02

The Recovery Journey

What does the first 48 hours of recovery look like?

expand_more

Post-operative care is highly individualized. During the first 48 hours, you will have access to our 24/7 Concierge Nursing line. We provide a tailored recovery suite at our partner boutique hotel for clients traveling from abroad, including lymphatic drainage therapy sessions to minimize swelling and accelerate healing.

When can I return to social engagements?

expand_more

For facial procedures, the "camera-ready" window is typically 10 to 14 days. For body contouring, we suggest 7 days of complete rest before returning to light social activities. Our team provides high-definition scar-management protocols to ensure that results appear entirely natural and virtually undetectable to the untrained eye.
