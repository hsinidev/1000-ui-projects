Surgical Procedures

What should I expect during pre-operative preparation?

expand_more
Pre-operative preparation involves several clinical assessments, including blood tests, imaging, and a consultation with your anesthesiologist. You will receive specific fasting instructions and a checklist for medications. Our goal is to ensure your body is in the best possible condition for a successful surgical outcome.

How long is the typical recovery period post-surgery?

expand_more
Recovery times vary significantly based on the procedure type (minimally invasive vs. open surgery) and individual patient health. Generally, initial hospital recovery lasts 3-7 days, followed by a 4-6 week gradual return to normal activities. Your surgical team will provide a tailored recovery roadmap.
