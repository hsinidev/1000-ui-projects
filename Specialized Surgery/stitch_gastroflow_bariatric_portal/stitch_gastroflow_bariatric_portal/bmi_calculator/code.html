Beyond the Scale

Bariatric procedures are metabolic interventions. They help reset your body's "set point," regulating hunger hormones and improving chronic conditions like Type 2 diabetes and hypertension, far beyond just reducing a number.

Is surgery right for you?

Clinical guidelines often suggest considering surgical options for a BMI over 35 with weight-related health issues, or a BMI over 40. However, every journey is unique.
