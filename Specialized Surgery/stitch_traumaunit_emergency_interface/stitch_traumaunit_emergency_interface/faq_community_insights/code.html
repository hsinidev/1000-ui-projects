Protocol: Rapid Hemorrhage Control

expand_more

In Level 1 trauma scenarios, immediate pressure application precedes all other interventions. Utilize high-tensile tourniquets at least 2-3 inches proximal to the wound site. For junctional hemorrhage, aggressive packing with hemostatic gauze is the gold standard within the TraumaUnit ecosystem.
