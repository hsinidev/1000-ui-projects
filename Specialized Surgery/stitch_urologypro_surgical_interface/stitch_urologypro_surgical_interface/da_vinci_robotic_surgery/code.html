Smaller Incisions, Better Outcomes

Robotic surgery is performed through a few small incisions, rather than the large open incisions used in traditional surgery. This "Keyhole" approach leads to:

  • check_circle Significantly less blood loss and fewer transfusions
  • check_circle Lower risk of post-operative infection
  • check_circle Reduced scarring and improved cosmetic results
