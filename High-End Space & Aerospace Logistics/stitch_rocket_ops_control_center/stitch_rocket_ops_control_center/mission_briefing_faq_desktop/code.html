Troubleshooting

Comm Link Intermittent?

Check encryption key sync on terminal C-14. Resetting the buffer usually clears the static.

Sensor Ghosting?

Verify thermal shielding integrity. Ice buildup on external probes may cause -5% variance.

LOX Pressure Low?

Initiate recirc-pump override. If pressure doesn't stabilize within 30s, abort fueling.
