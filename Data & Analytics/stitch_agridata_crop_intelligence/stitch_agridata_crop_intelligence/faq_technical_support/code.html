Frequently Asked Questions

How do I recalibrate the Model-S moisture sensors after a heavy rain event? expand_more

Sensors should be manually reset through the Operator Panel. Navigate to Telemetry > Sensors > Model-S and select 'Re-zero Atmospheric Delta'. Ensure sensors are clear of surface debris before initiating.

TECHNICAL NOTE:

Wait 4 hours post-precipitation for accurate ground-truth baseline readings.

Why is there a discrepancy between NDVI and EVI layers in Sector 7? expand_more

EVI is more sensitive in high-biomass areas. In Sector 7, the dense canopy of corn at V12 stage causes NDVI saturation. We recommend utilizing the EVI layer for late-season growth analysis to maintain precision.

What are the API rate limits for real-time telemetry streaming? expand_more

Standard enterprise accounts are limited to 1,000 requests per minute. For higher frequency GIS syncing, please contact the Precision Agriculture Division for dedicated throughput configuration.
