4 RESULTS
How do I report a vehicle maintenance issue? expand_more
Navigate to the 'Fleet' tab and select 'Maintenance Request'. Upload photos of the issue and specify the urgency level (Level 1-4) for immediate dispatcher review.
Procedure for hazardous material delay? expand_more
Immediately secure the area and use the 'Emergency Alert' button on the dashboard. Do not attempt to move the cargo until Safety Control has verified the perimeter stability.
Updating biometric ELD login credentials? expand_more
Credentials must be updated in person at a verified terminal hub. Contact technical support for a one-time bypass code if locked out during a route.
Warehouse gate code synchronization? expand_more
Gate codes are refreshed every 4 hours. Ensure your LogiData app has 'Sync Background Data' enabled to receive the latest encrypted access keys.
