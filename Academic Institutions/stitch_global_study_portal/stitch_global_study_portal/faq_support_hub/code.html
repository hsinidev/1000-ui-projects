Visa Requirements

Typically, you will need an acceptance letter from GlobalStudy, proof of financial sufficiency, a valid passport, and recent photographs. Specific requirements vary by host country.

  • Official Acceptance Letter (Form I-20 or equivalent)
  • Financial statements from the last 6 months
  • Passport valid for at least 6 months beyond stay
