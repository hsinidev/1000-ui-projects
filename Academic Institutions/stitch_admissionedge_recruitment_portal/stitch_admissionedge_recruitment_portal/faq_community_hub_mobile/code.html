Trending Questions

When is the early decision deadline? expand_more
Early Decision applications are due by November 1st. You will receive your admission notification by mid-December. This plan is binding for students who are certain AdmissionEdge is their first choice.
How do I track my application status? expand_more
You can track your status via the AdmissionEdge Portal. Login credentials were sent to the email address you used for your Common App.
Are merit scholarships automatic? expand_more
Yes! All applicants are automatically considered for merit-based scholarships during the review process. No additional application is required.
