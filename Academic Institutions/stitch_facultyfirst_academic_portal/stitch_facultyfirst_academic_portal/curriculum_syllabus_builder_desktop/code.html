College of Architecture chevron_right Curriculum 2024

ARCH 402: Sustainable Urban Design

Core Undergraduate Program • Spring Semester 2024

Learning Objectives

LO-01: ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACT

Analyze the carbon footprint of urban developments using standardized life-cycle assessment (LCA) tools.

LO-02: SOCIAL EQUITY

Evaluate urban design proposals through the lens of social inclusivity and equitable access to public infrastructure.

LO-03: TECH INTEGRATION

Implement digital twin simulations to predict microclimate variations in high-density urban canyons.

LO-04: POLICY COMPLIANCE

Synthesize design decisions with municipal zoning laws and international sustainability benchmarks.
