University FAQ

Instant answers to campus protocols, student services, and residential life policies.

search

What are the move-in protocols for 2024?

Scheduled arrivals begin Aug 25. Check your student portal for specific time slots and vehicle gate access instructions.

How do I access late-night dining?

The Late-Night Grill at Commons is open until 2:00 AM daily. Mobile ordering is available via the IvyPortal app.

Where can I find mental health resources?

Counseling services are located in the Wellness Pavilion. Walk-ins are welcome 9 AM - 5 PM, or call the 24/7 helpline.

How do I register a new student organization?

Applications for new groups open in September. You need 5 founding members and a faculty advisor to begin the process.
