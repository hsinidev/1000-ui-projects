Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my project's ethical clearance? expand_more
Navigate to Research Projects, select your active project, and click 'Compliance Documents' in the sidebar. You can upload renewed certificates directly to the IRB portal through LabNetwork integration.
Equipment booking is greyed out. Why? expand_more
This typically occurs if your safety certification has expired or if your grant budget for 'Instrumental Usage' has been depleted. Contact your PI or check the 'Equipment Booking' tab for specific status messages.
Can I export lab results to external datasets? expand_more
Yes, LabNetwork supports CSV, JSON, and XML exports. Note that all data exports are logged for institutional auditing and must comply with the University's data privacy policy (Section 4.2).
