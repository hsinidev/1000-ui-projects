Getting Started

How do I create my first course module? expand_more
Navigate to the 'Courses' tab on your dashboard. Click the 'Add New Module' button in the top right corner. You'll be prompted to provide a module name, description, and initial learning objectives. Once created, you can drag and drop assets directly into the curriculum builder.
What are the system requirements? expand_more
ScholarCloud is a web-based platform optimized for the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the best experience, we recommend a stable internet connection of 5Mbps+ and at least 4GB of RAM.

Technical Support

How do I report a bug in the forum system? expand_more
If you encounter any issues, please click the 'Help' icon in the bottom right corner of your screen and select 'Report a Technical Issue'. Be sure to include a screenshot and the steps to reproduce the error.
Integration with Learning Management Systems (LMS)? expand_more
ScholarCloud supports LTI 1.3 standards, allowing seamless integration with Canvas, Blackboard, and Moodle. Access your institution settings to find your unique consumer key and secret.

Grading Policies

How is the final grade calculated? expand_more
Grades are calculated using a weighted average based on module completion (30%), task performance (50%), and forum participation (20%). Individual instructors may adjust these weights within the course configuration settings.
Can I appeal a grade through the platform? expand_more
Yes. Within the 'Grades' tab, select the specific assignment and click 'Request Review'. This opens a formal communication channel with your educator to discuss specific rubric points.
