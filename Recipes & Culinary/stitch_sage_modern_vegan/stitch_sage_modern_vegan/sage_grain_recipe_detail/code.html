Grilled Tempeh &
Seasonal Greens

An intentional blend of fermented soy protein and vibrant organic spring vegetables. Infused with a smoke-house marinade and served over a bed of flash-seared kale and charred asparagus.

The Elements

The Process

01

Infuse and Prepare

Whisk together the tamari, liquid smoke, and a splash of olive oil. Submerge the tempeh slices in the marinade for at least 15 minutes. While the tempeh absorbs the flavors, wash and dry your greens thoroughly to ensure they sear rather than steam.

02

The Grill

Heat your cast iron pan over medium-high heat. Once wisps of smoke appear, place the tempeh on the grill. Cook for 4-5 minutes per side until deep, caramelized grill marks appear. Remove and let rest under foil.

03

Searing the Greens

In the same pan, add the asparagus. Sear for 3 minutes, then toss in the kale and a squeeze of lemon juice. The residual heat will wilt the kale while keeping it bright and slightly crisp. Season with sea salt and cracked pepper.

04

Composition

Plate a generous bed of the seared greens. Arrange the grilled tempeh on top. Garnish with lemon zest, toasted pumpkin seeds, and a final drizzle of high-quality olive oil.
