The Origin Story

From the Chalky Soils of Bordeaux

Our Rouge de Bordeaux is sourced exclusively from the Moreau family farm, where regenerative agriculture practices have been refined over four generations. This heritage land, rich in limestone and minerals, imparts the distinctive mineral complexity found in the grain's flavor profile.

We mill every batch on our Austrian Osttiroler stone mills just 48 hours before shipment. This slow-speed milling process ensures the grain never exceeds 40°C, preserving the delicate oils and enzymes that are often destroyed in commercial roller milling.
