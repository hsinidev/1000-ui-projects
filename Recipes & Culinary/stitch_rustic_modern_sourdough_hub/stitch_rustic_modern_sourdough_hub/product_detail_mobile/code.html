The Bordeaux Heritage

Rouge de Bordeaux is a legendary French heritage wheat that dates back to the 18th century. Unlike modern industrial varieties, this grain has been preserved through generations of artisanal farmers who refused to compromise on flavor for yield.

Each batch is stone-ground at low temperatures in our Cotswolds mill, preserving the delicate oils and nutty, cinnamon-like aroma that defines this extraordinary grain. The resulting flour is deep red-gold, yielding a crumb that is as aromatic as it is resilient.

info Baker's Note

"Rouge de Bordeaux is highly fermentative. Expect a faster activity in your sourdough starter. We recommend a long, cold bulk fermentation to unlock the full potential of its phenolic profile."
