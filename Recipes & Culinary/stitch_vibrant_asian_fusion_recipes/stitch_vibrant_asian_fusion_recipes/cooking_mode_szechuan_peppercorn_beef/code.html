Active Technique

The Dry Toast

Peppercorns should jump and smoke slightly. Do not let them turn black; we want aromatic, not burnt.

Step 03

Toast the peppercorns until fragrant and slightly smoking.

restaurant

Equipment Needed

Small dry skillet or carbon steel wok.

warning

Chef's Note

Once they become fragrant, immediately remove from heat and crush using a mortar and pestle for the best numbing effect.
