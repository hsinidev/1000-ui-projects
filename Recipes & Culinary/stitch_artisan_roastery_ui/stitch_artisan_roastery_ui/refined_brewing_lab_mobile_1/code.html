About the Bloom

"The bloom is the degassing of carbon dioxide that occurs when fresh hot water first hits the coffee grounds. This 30-second ritual prepares the bed for even extraction."

Pour approximately double the weight of your coffee in water (37g) and stir gently. Watch for the expansion—this indicates your beans are fresh and full of aromatic oils.
