Sourced with Intentionality

Our Ethiopia Yirgacheffe comes from the Aricha station, where over 700 smallholder farmers deliver cherry daily. This specific lot was fermented for 36 hours before being washed and dried on raised beds for 12 days. We roast in small 12kg batches to preserve the volatile aromatics of jasmine and citrus.
