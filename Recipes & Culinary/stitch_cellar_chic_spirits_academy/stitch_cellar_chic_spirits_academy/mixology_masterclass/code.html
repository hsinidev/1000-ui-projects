The Vintage Negroni: A Study in Bitters

liquor

The Composition

Instructional Methodology

01

The Chilling Phase

Begin by chilling both your mixing glass and your serving coupe. Precision in temperature is the foundation of a superior Negroni. Let the ice sit for exactly 60 seconds.

02

The Harmonization

Combine the gin, vermouth, and bitters in the mixing glass. Stir with a long-handled spoon with deliberate, circular motions. Aim for 30 rotations to achieve perfect dilution.

03

The Expression

Strain the mixture over a clear ice sphere. Express the oils of a fresh orange peel over the glass, then swipe the rim to ensure the citrus notes precede the palate.
