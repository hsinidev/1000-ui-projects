THE PERFECT POUR

Muscadet Sèvre-et-Maine

Loire Valley, France

TASTING NOTES

Sea Salt Green Apple Lees Aging High Acidity

WHY IT WORKS

The bone-dry nature and saline undertones of Muscadet mirror the oceanic salinity of fresh oysters. Its high acidity cuts through the silky texture, creating a balanced, refreshing finish.
