'Safe-to-Slice' Veggie Sticks

Learn to slice like a pro with safe, kid-friendly tools! Crunchy, colorful, and super healthy.

schedule 15 Mins
star Beginner
restaurant Healthy Snack
shopping_basket

Ingredients

  • check_circle 2 Large Carrots
  • check_circle 1 Cucumber
  • check_circle 1 Bell Pepper (any color!)
  • check_circle Your favorite dip
handyman

Tools Needed

  • swords Kid-safe plastic knife
  • border_outer Cutting board
  • person Adult helper for peeling

auto_stories Step-by-Step

1

Wash and Peel

Wash your veggies in cool water. Ask a grown-up to help peel the carrots.

2

The Bridge Cut

Hold your veggie like a bridge. Slice down the middle with your safe knife.

3

Stick Shapes

Turn the flat side down and slice them into long, crunchy sticks.

4

Dip and Crunch

Arrange them on a plate and serve with your favorite dip!
