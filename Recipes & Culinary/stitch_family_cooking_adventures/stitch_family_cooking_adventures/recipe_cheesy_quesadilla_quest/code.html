Main Quest

Cheesy Quesadilla Quest

schedule 10 mins
kid_star Beginner Friendly
shopping_basket

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Optional Beans or Corn
flatware

Tools Needed

skillet Non-stick pan
flatware Spatula
content_cut Kid-safe cutter

Cooking Adventure Steps auto_awesome

1

Lay it Flat

Place one tortilla on a plate or the cold pan. Make sure it's nice and centered!

2

Cheese Rain

Sprinkle cheese evenly all over the tortilla. Use as much as you like for maximum cheesiness!

3

Fold & Press

Fold it in half like a big, soft taco. Press down gently with your clean hands.

4

The Golden Flip warning

With a grown-up's help, cook on the stove until the cheese melts and the tortilla is crunchy and golden.

5

Slice it Up

Use your safe cutter to make triangles. Perfect for dipping in salsa or sour cream!
