01.

What is the standard retention period for biological samples?

GENERAL SOP-204
expand_less

Per GLP Section 4.2, all primary biological samples must be retained for a minimum of 5 years unless otherwise specified by the trial protocol. For infectious materials, please refer to the Biosafety Level 3 (BSL-3) specific annex.

info Note: Cold storage samples require temperature logging every 4 hours.
