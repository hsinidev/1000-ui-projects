What is a Digital Product Passport (DPP)?

Understanding the "soul" of your physical garment.

expand_more
A Digital Product Passport is a unique, blockchain-encrypted record that stores the entire lifecycle of your item. It includes raw material sourcing, manufacturing certifications, ownership history, and repair logs. By scanning the NFC tag in your item, you access this immutable "Digital Soul," ensuring radical transparency and authenticity.
