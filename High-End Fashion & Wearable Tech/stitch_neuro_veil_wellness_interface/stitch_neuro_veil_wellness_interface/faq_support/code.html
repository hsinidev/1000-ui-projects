Sensory Technology

Our Neuro-Veil utilizes microscopic silver-thread sensors woven into the fabric. These sensors monitor skin conductance and rhythmic micro-vibrations of your pulse, translating physiological data into stress-response patterns through our proprietary neural engine.

The movement is designed to be haptic and subtle. While the fabric does expand and contract slightly (approx. 2-3mm), it appears as a natural drape adjustment to the casual observer, maintaining your personal privacy while providing comfort.

Data Privacy

Where is my bio-data stored?

All raw biological signals are processed locally on your device. Only encrypted, anonymous stress-trends are synced with our secure cloud for long-term insights, unless you opt-out of cloud syncing entirely.

Can I delete my neural history?

Yes. Through the 'Privacy Portal' in the Neuro-Veil app, you can trigger a permanent 'Data Purge' which wipes all history from our servers and your wearable hardware instantly.
