How does "Liquid" ventilation adapt during peak exertion? expand_more
Our Stitch™ fibers utilize shape-memory polymers. When thermal sensors detect a core temperature increase of >1.2°C, the knit geometry expands by 14% to maximize airflow precisely over primary sweat glands.
Calibrating biometric sensors with the KINETIC CORE app expand_more
Ensure your Aero-Knit garment is properly seated against the skin. Open the Admin Panel, navigate to Sensor Calibration, and perform a 30-second low-intensity movement profile to establish your thermal baseline.
Post-wash conductive fiber maintenance expand_more
Aero-Knit is rated for 200 cycles. Use cold-wash settings and avoid silicon-based softeners which can coat the silver-infused micro-threads and degrade signal integrity by up to 40%.
