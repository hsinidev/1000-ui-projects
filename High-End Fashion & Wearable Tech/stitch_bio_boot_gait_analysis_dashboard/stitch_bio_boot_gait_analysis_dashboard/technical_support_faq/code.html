TECHNICAL_FAQ

Reference documentation for the BIO-BOOT biometric integration system. All protocols must be strictly followed to ensure data integrity during high-impact operation.

sensors 01_SENSOR_CALIBRATION

CODE: PRTCL-77
HOW DO I INITIATE FORCE-PLATE REZEROING? expand_more
Ensure the boot is placed on a level, non-vibrating surface. In the 'GAIT' tab, select 'DEVICE SETTINGS' > 'RECALIBRATE'. Remain stationary for 10 seconds. The LED on the lateral heel will pulse Safety Orange during this phase.
GYROSCOPE DRIFT DETECTED IN DASHBOARD. ACTION? expand_more
Drift typically occurs due to local electromagnetic interference. Move 5 meters away from high-voltage equipment or large metal masses. Perform a 'Figure-8' movement with the boot for 30 seconds to re-align the IMU.

build 02_HARDWARE_MAINTENANCE

CODE: MNT-CORE-09
CLEANING PROTOCOLS FOR BIO-SYNTHETIC UPPER? expand_more
Use only technical-grade isopropyl wipes (70%). DO NOT submerge the chassis in water. Avoid the sensor charging ports located under the arch flap. If moisture enters the port, wait 24 hours before connecting to a power source.
BATTERY REPLACEMENT CYCLE FOR INTEGRATED MODULES? expand_more
The internal Solid-State battery is rated for 5,000 charge cycles. If total capacity drops below 75% (Status: SERVICE), contact your field technician for a core module swap.

analytics 03_DATA_INTERPRETATION

CODE: ANLYT-X
UNDERSTANDING 'FATIGUE_COEFFICIENT' RATINGS? expand_more
A rating above 0.85 indicates critical neural-muscular degradation. The system will automatically trigger a 'REST_MANDATED' notification. High values are typically driven by increased ground-contact time and reduced vertical oscillation.
SUPINATION VS. OVERPRONATION WARNINGS expand_more
Check the 'METRICS' screen for real-time pressure mapping. Warnings are generated if the center of pressure (CoP) deviates by more than 12% from the neutral axis for 500 consecutive steps.
