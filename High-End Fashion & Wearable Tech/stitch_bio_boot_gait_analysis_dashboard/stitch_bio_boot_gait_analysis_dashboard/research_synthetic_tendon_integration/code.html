01_THE_CARBON_DELTA

The integration of dual-density PEBAX foams with a curved carbon-fiber plate architecture creates a mechanical advantage previously unseen in industrial biometric footwear. However, the 'Bio-Boot' testing phase identified a critical threshold at the 350km mark.

Initial hypotheses suggested that vertical stiffness would remain constant, but ultrasonic scanning revealed micro-fractures in the lateral support wing. These disruptions directly correlate with increased pronation and energy leakage in the terminal stance phase of the running gait.

02_OPERATIONAL_MITIGATION

To counter the structural degradation, we recommend a recalibration of the embedded Bio-Boot sensor array. By increasing the sampling frequency of the IMU to 1000Hz, athletes can receive haptic feedback 50ms earlier, allowing for neuromuscular compensation before joint strain occurs.
