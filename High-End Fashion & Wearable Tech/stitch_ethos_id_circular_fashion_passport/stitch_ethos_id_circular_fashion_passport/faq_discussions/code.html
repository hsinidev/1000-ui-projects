System Protocols & FAQ

Our blockchain-backed ecosystem ensures that every stitch has a story and every garment a permanent digital legacy. Explore how we maintain the loop.

How does blockchain verification prevent counterfeiting? add

Each garment is embedded with a secure NFC chip linked to a unique cryptographic hash on the chain. This digital twin records ownership, origin, and material certifications (GOTS, FSC) that cannot be altered or duplicated by third parties.

What qualifies a garment for the 'Ethos Repair' program? add

Any item registered with an active Ethos-ID is eligible. We prioritize natural fiber degradation and hardware replacement. Once repaired, the event is logged into the garment's Provenance Chain, often increasing its verified resale value.

How is the 'Transparency Score' calculated? add

The score is a weighted average of Tier 1-4 supplier disclosure, carbon footprint data per SKU, and the biodegradability of the specific textile blend used.

Can I transfer ownership for private resale? add

Yes. By initiating a 'Passport Transfer' in your dashboard, the blockchain record updates to the new owner's wallet while preserving the entire historical life-cycle of the piece.
