Technology & Security

Every haptic pulse is encrypted using 256-bit AES protocols, ensuring that the intimate "Digital Touch" between you and your partner remains entirely private. We do not store biometric patterns on our servers.
Our Haptic-Luxe pieces utilize Low-Energy Bluetooth (BLE) to sync with your device. While real-time connection offers immediate feedback, your device can queue pulses for when you reconnect.
We use only ethically sourced 18k gold and conflict-free stones. To maintain the haptic sensor sensitivity, clean with the provided microfiber pearl cloth and avoid ultrasonic cleaners.
