Technology & Connectivity

Our proprietary Haptic-Sync module utilizes Ultra-Low Energy Bluetooth 5.2. It pairs seamlessly with the Haptic-Luxe mobile application on both iOS and Android, ensuring an encrypted, secure connection between your jewelry and your partner's device.
Latency is minimized through our global relay servers, typically resulting in a delivery speed of under 300ms across continents. It mimics the speed of a physical gesture.
