Invisible Tech

How does Neural Weave maintain durability through daily wear? expand_more

Our proprietary Neural Weave integrates liquid-state sensors directly into silk fibers at a molecular level. Unlike traditional wearables, Silk-Sensor garments can withstand up to 500 dry cleans without biometric degradation. The silk itself is treated with a bio-mimetic polymer that shields the sensors from pH fluctuations and environmental moisture.

What is Kinetic Power Harvesting and how does it fuel the sensors? expand_more

Every movement of your body—from a heartbeat to a stride—generates micro-joules of energy. Our Kinetic Power Harvesting system captures this energy through piezoelectric fibers. This creates a self-sustaining loop where your life's cadence provides the power for your life's monitoring, eliminating the need for invasive charging ports.

Privacy & Biometric Encryption

ENCRYPTION LEVEL: ELITE

How is my biometric data protected?

All data is encrypted on the garment's local Neural Chip using Quantum-Resistant Encryption. No raw biometric data ever leaves the fabric; only distilled 'Insights' are transmitted to your dashboard via a secure, one-time pairing protocol.

Can I deactivate sensors manually?

Yes. The 'Stealth Mode' toggle in your Vital Dashboard physically severs the connection between the sensor weave and the transmission chip, rendering the garment electronically inert while maintaining its aesthetic elegance.
