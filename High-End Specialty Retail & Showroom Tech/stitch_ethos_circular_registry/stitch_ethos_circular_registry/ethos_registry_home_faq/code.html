Registry Intelligence

Common inquiries regarding the Ethos-Registry ecosystem, security protocols, and our commitment to circular luxury.

How is provenance verified?

add

We utilize a combination of forensic physical analysis and verifiable supply chain documentation. Our "Provenance Protocol" requires origin certification and a complete chain of custody before an item can be registered.

What security measures protect my Digital Passport?

add

Each passport is secured using enterprise-grade encryption and stored on a private, permissioned blockchain. Access is managed through multi-factor authentication and hardware-bound private keys.

How does the buy-back program function?

add

To promote a circular economy, items registered through Ethos-Registry are eligible for our secondary marketplace. We guarantee a baseline buy-back valuation for items maintained in 'excellent' condition, as verified by their digital logs.

Can I register items I already own?

add

Yes, through our Legacy Onboarding service. You may submit existing luxury assets for retrospective verification. Once approved, we issue a Digital Passport that begins tracking the item's future history.
