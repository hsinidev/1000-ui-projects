How do I achieve the perfect fluid drape for silk garments?

expand_more

To achieve the signature Atelier fluid drape, ensure your AR Mirror is calibrated to 'Silk Mode' in the Lighting menu. Use the gesture control to 'pin' the fabric at the natural shoulder line. Our smart mirror technology calculates gravity in real-time, simulating 22momme silk weight.
