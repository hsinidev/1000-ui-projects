Artist Statement

Elena Vance

"My work exists in the tension between the physical and the ephemeral. I explore how light interacts with shadow to reveal truths we often overlook in the noise of daily life."

Based in Berlin, Elena Vance has spent the last decade pioneering 'Minimalist Digitalism'—a movement that uses high-key contrast and void-based compositions to evoke a sense of digital prestige and architectural calm. Her pieces are held in private vaults across four continents.

Philosophy of the Void

The artist believes that in an age of constant notification, the most radical act is silence. Vance's work is a digital sanctuary—a space designed to allow the viewer to exist without the pressure of interpretation, simply experiencing the presence of light and form.
