Acquisitions

How does the private acquisition flow work?

Each transaction is managed via a smart-escrow protocol. Upon selection of a piece, a legal deed of transfer is generated alongside the cryptographic hash, ensuring immediate ownership verification across our partner vaults.

Are fractional ownership options available for the Vault?

For select blue-chip digital assets in the Curator-Wall Vault, we offer tiered acquisition. This allows multiple collectors to hold verifiable shares in a single masterpiece, governed by a DAO-structured collective agreement.

Digital Provenance

Cryptographic Authenticity

Every pixel is hashed and recorded on-chain. We utilize zero-knowledge proofs to verify artist signatures without exposing private transactional metadata, maintaining the highest level of privacy for our patrons.

The Permanent Ledger

Curator-Wall assets are stored using IPFS and Filecoin, ensuring your acquisition remains accessible for eternity, regardless of the platform's status. The ledger provides a transparent history of exhibitions and prior custodians.
