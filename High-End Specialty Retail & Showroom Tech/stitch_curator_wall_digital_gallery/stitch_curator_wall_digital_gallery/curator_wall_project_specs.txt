# Curator-Wall: Project Specification

## Brand Identity
**Name:** Curator-Wall
**Aesthetic:** Artistic-Moody / Deep Space Black, Silver, and Vibrant Accents.
**Tone:** Premium, Immersive, Futuristic, Sophisticated.

## Required Screens (Mobile Optimized)
1. **The Exhibition (Active Rotation):** Immersive display of current digital art pieces with fluid transitions.
2. **Artist Statements:** Deep dives into the creators, their philosophy, and high-res portfolios.
3. **AR 'In-Home' Preview:** A tool for collectors to visualize pieces in their own space via augmented reality.
4. **Purchase Inquiry:** A streamlined, elegant form for high-value acquisitions.

## UX & UI Requirements
- **Fluid Animations:** Use scroll-triggered transitions and subtle motion.
- **Social-Viewing Mode:** Integrated 'trending' feed to show what other collectors are watching.
- **Mobile Priority:** 'Discover' feeds are front-and-center; simplified 'Inquire' buttons for quick action.
- **Visuals:** Dark mode by default, silver accents, vibrant glowing highlights.
