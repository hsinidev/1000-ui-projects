Phase 1: Axis Neutralization

Updated: 04.22.24 Critical Task

Precise balancing is the foundation of high-performance gimbal operation. Before engaging the FLOW-RIG auto-tune algorithms, ensure the camera payload is physically neutral across all three axes. A "dead-stick" test should result in zero pendulum effect when the rig is powered down.

  • check_circle Slide the tilt axis bracket until the lens remains level at 45° vertical tilt.
  • check_circle Adjust the roll arm to counteract side-heavy wireless transmitters or follow-focus motors.
  • check_circle Ensure the pan axis is perfectly vertical relative to the sled post.

TECH NOTE #884

Avoid balancing with lens caps or filters removed if they will be used during flight. Every gram shifts the motor COG (Center of Gravity).
