1954 Leica M Serial #12903

The Summicron Archetype

The 50mm f/2 Summicron was a watershed moment in optical engineering. Introduced in 1953, it utilized Thorium glass to achieve a level of micro-contrast that was previously considered theoretically impossible. This specific unit features the collapsible mount, a testament to mid-century portability requirements.
