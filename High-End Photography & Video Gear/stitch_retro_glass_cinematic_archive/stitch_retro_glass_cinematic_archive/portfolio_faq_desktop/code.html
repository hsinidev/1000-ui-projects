TECHNICAL INQUIRIES

01. WHAT IS THE RE-HOUSING PROCESS?

We dismantle the original vintage lens barrels, clean the internal elements via ultrasonic bath, and transplant the glass into a precision-engineered aerospace-grade aluminum housing with standardized 114mm front diameters and PL or LPL mounts.

02. HOW DO YOU CALCULATE MTF CURVES FOR AGED GLASS?

Every lens is benchmarked on our Chrosziel lens projector. We map the Modulation Transfer Function at various apertures to ensure that while the "character" is preserved, the mechanical centering is perfect for modern high-resolution sensors.

03. COMPATIBILITY WITH FULL-FRAME SENSORS?

Most vintage lenses were designed for 35mm film, which matches modern Full-Frame digital sensors. However, we also offer "Expander" services for medium format coverage using custom-ground rear elements.

04. DOES RE-HOUSING AFFECT THE VALUE OF THE GLASS?

For collectors, original housing is key. For cinematographers and working photographers, re-housing exponentially increases value by making the glass reliable, mountable, and focus-puller friendly.
