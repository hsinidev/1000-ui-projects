FROM RELIC TO REFINEMENT

PHASES

01

Optical Inspection

Every journey begins with a forensic evaluation. We use spectral analysis to assess the integrity of vintage coatings and micro-inspect for balsam separation. Only the pristine survive.

02

Mechanical Design

We translate optical geometry into modern mechanical housing. Our designs prioritize cam-driven focus systems and consistent gear placement for modern cinema workflows while honoring the lens's soul.

architecture
03

Precision Machining

Aerospace-grade aluminum and brass are carved to micron-level tolerances. Each component is finished with hard-anodized coatings to ensure a lifetime of tactile reliability.

04

Final Calibration

The alchemist's touch. We calibrate focus scales manually across the entire throw, ensuring the center of the image and the character of the lens remain perfectly balanced for the sensor.

flare

The Seal of Excellence

Each lens is engraved with its unique archive number, signifying its permanent place in our heritage register.
