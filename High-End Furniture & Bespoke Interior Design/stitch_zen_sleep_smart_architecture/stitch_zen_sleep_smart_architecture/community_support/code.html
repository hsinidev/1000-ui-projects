Thermal Scheduling

How does thermal oscillation aid REM sleep? expand_more

Our system gently lowers the core mattress temperature by 1.5°C during the onset of REM cycles, mimicking the natural biological cooling required for deep restorative phases.

Can I sync temperature with my local sunrise? expand_more

Yes. The 'Natural Wake' feature gradually warms the surface area 30 minutes before your scheduled alarm, signaling your circadian rhythm to begin the arousal process naturally.

Snore Mitigation

How does the 'Silent Adjust' work? expand_more

Upon detecting specific acoustic frequencies of snoring, the bed frame subtly adjusts the head incline by 7 degrees. This clears the airway without fully waking the sleeper.

Is my audio data kept private? expand_more

Audio analysis is performed locally on the 'Bedroom Cluster Alpha' hardware. No raw audio files ever leave your local mesh network; only metadata on sleep quality is stored.
