Initial Calibration Guide expand_more
Begin by sitting with your feet flat on the floor. Aura's biomechanic sensors will auto-detect your weight distribution. Follow the haptic pulses to adjust your pelvic tilt to the neutral 12-degree zone.
Pressure Map Accuracy expand_more
The high-precision matrix uses 2,000 sensors. Ensure your clothing is relatively fitted for the most clinical accuracy. Avoid thick outer layers like heavy coats during the 3D calibration phase.
Cloud Sync & Data Privacy expand_more
Your biometric data is encrypted on-device. Cloud sync only stores anonymous metadata to refine the global ergonomic algorithms, unless you opt-in for personal posture history.
Bio-Mechanical Adjustments expand_more
Adjustments are made via the liquid sliders in the "Adjust" tab. Each change takes 3-5 seconds as the pneumatic air cells re-pressurize to your new configuration.
