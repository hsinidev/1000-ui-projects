General Inquiries

Volume 01 / Issue 04
How often should the retrieval arm be calibrated?
add
The AETERNA mechanical armature performs an autonomous self-check every 24 hours. A deep manual calibration is only required if the precision falls below 0.5mm, typically after 5,000 retrieval cycles. You can monitor this in the System Logs tab.
Integrating non-RFID tagged items into the collection?
add
While RFID tags ensure 100% accuracy, our visual recognition engine can identify untagged items via the 4K scan-port. Simply place the item in the staging bay for analysis. We recommend using our proprietary Rose Gold silk-thread tags for elite-tier tracking.
Climate control settings for vintage silk vs. technical fabrics?
add
Aero-Closet utilizes Micro-Zoning. You can assign specific humidity levels to different rail segments. We suggest 45% for vintage silks and 30% for technical sportswear to prevent moisture retention and fiber degradation.
