We bridge the gap between human encroachment and animal survival. From immediate field triage to long-term habitat restoration, your support funds the frontlines of wildlife protection.

Our Mission: Turning Crisis into Coexistence.

Wildlife Rescue was founded on a simple, urgent realization: the wild is shrinking, and its inhabitants are paying the price. We don't just treat injuries; we heal ecosystems.

"The measure of our humanity is how we treat those who cannot speak for themselves."

We operate a 24/7 emergency dispatch system and a world-class veterinary complex capable of treating everything from orphaned elephants to injured raptors. Our goal is always release, but our commitment is lifelong.
