Avocado
dangerous Toxic

Contains persin, a fungicidal toxin. Can cause respiratory distress, congestion, and heart failure in most birds within 12-24 hours.
ALL SPECIES
Apples
check_circle Safe

Flesh is highly nutritious. CAUTION: Seeds must be removed as they contain trace amounts of cyanide.

Peanuts
warning Caution

High risk of Aflatoxin (mold). Only serve human-grade, roasted, shell-free peanuts in limited quantities as a high-fat treat.
Large Parrots
Spinach
warning Caution

Contains oxalates which can bind calcium. Safe in small, occasional quantities. Not recommended for birds with kidney issues.
