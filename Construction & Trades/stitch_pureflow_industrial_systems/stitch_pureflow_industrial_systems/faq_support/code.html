How to reset the PF-DR-001 pressure sensor?

CAT_001

Initiate a hard reset by holding the MASTER_CTRL trigger for 10 seconds while the unit is in standby mode. Confirm the blue light cycle before rebooting.

What does 'Error Code X-99' signify?

CAT_ERR

X-99 indicates a sync mismatch between the inventory node and the central core. Check wireless signal strength and recalibrate the antenna array.

Updating firmware on disconnected nodes?

CAT_SYS

Disconnected nodes must be manually updated via the physical COM-PORT using the standard PLUMB-OS serial cable (v.4.0 compliant).
