01 // COMPLIANCE & CLOUD expand_more

Q. What are your AWS certifications and cloud infrastructure standards?

STEELFRAME maintains AWS Foundational Technical Review (FTR) status and is compliant with SOC2 Type II standards. All structural data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit via TLS 1.3, ensuring high-availability clusters across multiple availability zones.

Q. How do you handle site safety data and OSHA reporting?

Our platform integrates directly with IoT sensor arrays on-site to monitor structural load and worker proximity in real-time. Incident reports are automatically formatted for OSHA 300A compliance and can be exported as cryptographically signed PDFs for legal audits.
02 // STRUCTURAL INTEGRITY expand_more

Q. What precision level is guaranteed for 3D steel frame modeling?

We support LOD 400 (Level of Development) for all structural steel components, providing exact shop-ready geometry including bolt holes, welds, and plate thicknesses within a 0.5mm tolerance range.
