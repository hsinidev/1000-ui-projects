Frequently Asked Questions

What are the specific subfloor requirements for marble inlays?

expand_more
All marble inlays require a structural subfloor with a deflection rating of L/720 or better. We recommend a cured sand-cement screed or high-strength self-leveling underlayment. Any moisture levels must be below 4% before installation begins to prevent efflorescence.

How often should commercial-grade walnut flooring be refinished?

expand_more
Our commercial walnut features a proprietary UV-cured ceramic finish. Under high-traffic conditions, we recommend a professional "screen and recoat" every 5-7 years. A full sand and refinish is typically only necessary after 15-20 years of heavy use.

Can Aethelred Epoxy Terrazzo be applied over existing tile?

expand_more
While technically possible, we strongly advise removing existing tile. If the substrate is structurally sound, it must be mechanically abraded to a CSP-3 profile. Any loose tiles or hollow sounds will compromise the integrity of the terrazzo pour.

What is the fire rating for your material specifications?

expand_more
Most of our stone and terrazzo products are inherently non-combustible (Class A). Our engineered hardwood products meet Class II fire ratings as standard, with Class I treatments available for high-occupancy commercial projects upon request.
