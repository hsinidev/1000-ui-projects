Knowledge Base

Typically, TPO systems have a lifespan of 15 to 20 years. Replacement should be scheduled when core samples show significant moisture saturation in the insulation layer or when seam failure exceeds 15% of the total roof area.
Our drones utilize FLIR high-resolution thermal sensors to detect temperature differentials. Saturated insulation retains heat longer than dry areas. By flying at dusk, we identify "hot spots" that indicate trapped moisture beneath the membrane.
PMMA (Polymethyl Methacrylate) offers a seamless, cold-applied solution for complex penetrations. It is highly resistant to UV degradation and mechanical stress, making it superior to traditional bitumen-based flashing in high-traffic industrial zones.
