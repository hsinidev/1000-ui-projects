Regarding the "Meridian Atrium" installation: how did you manage the thermal expansion tolerances on those 12m vertical fins? We've seen significant deflection in similar load-bearing glass structures during extreme seasonal shifts.

verified
Project Team Official Response 45m ago

We utilized a specialized slip-joint bracket at the head connection that allows for +/- 15mm of vertical movement. The horizontal silicone seals are high-elongation grade to maintain the airtight barrier without transferring stress to the glass.
