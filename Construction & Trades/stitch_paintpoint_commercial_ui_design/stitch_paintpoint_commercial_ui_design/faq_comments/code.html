What OSHA standards do your teams follow?

Our technicians are 100% OSHA-30 certified. We implement rigorous fall protection systems, respiratory programs, and hazardous material handling protocols on every industrial site without exception.

02 / COATINGS add

How long is the curing time for epoxy floors?

Standard industrial epoxy systems require 24 hours for foot traffic and 72 hours for heavy machinery. We offer high-performance polyaspartic options that cure in as little as 6 hours for critical operations.

03 / ESTIMATES add

Are mobilization costs included in the quote?

Yes. Our transparent pricing model includes all site preparation, specialized equipment rental, crew travel, and final site cleanup. No hidden industrial fees, ever.

04 / COATINGS add

Do you provide NACE-certified inspections?

We provide full NACE Level 2 and Level 3 inspection reports upon request for all heavy-duty anti-corrosive projects to ensure compliance with architectural specifications.
