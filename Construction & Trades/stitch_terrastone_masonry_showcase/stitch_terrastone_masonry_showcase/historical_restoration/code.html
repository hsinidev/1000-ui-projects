The Somerset Regency Facade Restoration

history_edu Project Log: Methodology

PHASE 01
OCT — NOV

Non-Abrasive Steam Cleaning

Utilizing high-pressure, low-volume steam to gently lift carbon sulfate crusts and biological growth without damaging the friable limestone substrate beneath.

PHASE 02
DEC — FEB

Traditional Tuckpointing

Manual removal of failed Portland cement mortars. Re-pointing with historically accurate hydraulic lime mortar (NHL 3.5) mixed with local crushed aggregate to match the original hue and porosity.

PHASE 03
MAR — APR

Seismic Grouting

Internal consolidation of the masonry core using gravity-fed lime grouts to fill voids and restore structural monolithic behavior without altering the facade's external appearance.
