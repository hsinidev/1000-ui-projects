Specialized Services

Brickwork & Tuckpointing

Structural permanence achieved through traditional bond patterns and meticulous mortar restoration. We specialize in the preservation of heritage masonry using historically accurate lime mortars and artisanal tuckpointing techniques.

Structural Bond Patterns

English Bond

Characterized by alternating courses of stretchers and headers. Historically regarded as the strongest bond, it is favored for load-bearing structural walls where durability is the primary metric.

Grade IV Strength Heritage Standard

Flemish Bond

Created by laying headers and stretchers alternately in every course. Often used for its aesthetic balance, this bond provides a sophisticated facade texture while maintaining significant structural integrity.

Aesthetic Grade Balanced Load
TECHNICAL PROCESS

Tuckpointing Execution

01
RAKING
Removal of decayed mortar to a depth of 20mm-25mm using hand tools to avoid brick damage.
02
FLUSH POINTING
In-filling joints with base mortar color-matched exactly to the existing masonry substrate.
03
RIBBON APPLICATION
Precise application of a fine 'fillet' or 'ribbon' of contrasting lime putty to create the illusion of perfect joints.

SUBSTRATE ANALYSIS

A chemical analysis of existing mortar ensures our new mix matches the compression strength and vapor permeability of the original wall.

LIME COMPONENTS
