What is the expected lifespan of TerraStone facade panels?

expand_more

Our facade systems are engineered for a service life exceeding 100 years. Using high-density sedimentary rock and advanced mortar-less anchoring systems, we minimize the thermal stress and moisture ingress that typically leads to degradation in traditional masonry.

How do environmental factors impact restoration timelines?

expand_more

Restoration project timelines are strictly governed by humidity and ambient temperature to ensure mortar curing and sealant integrity. Typically, a standard facade inspection takes 2-3 weeks, followed by a restoration phase of 4-12 months depending on the building's scale and exposure level.

Are TerraStone systems compatible with historical retrofitting?

expand_more

Yes. We specialize in "Invis-Anchor" technology which allows modern high-performance stone panels to be integrated into existing historical steel or masonry skeletons without compromising the aesthetic heritage or structural load limits of the original building.
