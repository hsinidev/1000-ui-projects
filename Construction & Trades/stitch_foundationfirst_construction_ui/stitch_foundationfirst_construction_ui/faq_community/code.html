Concrete Curing Times

expand_less

Standard concrete reaches approximately 70% of its design strength within 7 days. Full specified strength (typically 3,000 to 4,000 PSI) is achieved at 28 days. Heavy-duty commercial foundations must remain undisturbed for a minimum of 48 hours post-pour.

7D 70% STRENGTH
28D DESIGN LOAD
