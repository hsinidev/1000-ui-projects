Frequent Questions

How do I access high-resolution live streams? expand_more
Our live streams are broadcast in 4K resolution. To access, ensure your internet connection is at least 25Mbps. You can toggle resolution settings in the player controls located at the bottom-right of the studio viewing window.
Can I request a private studio viewing? expand_more
Yes, Collector Tier members may schedule one private 30-minute studio viewing per quarter. These can be booked through the "Studio Visits" tab in your sidebar. Availability depends on the artist's current exhibition schedule.
What is the policy for original artwork acquisitions? expand_more
Original works featured in Atelier-Live are available first to our active community members. Shipping is handled via specialized fine art couriers. We provide archival certificates of authenticity for every acquisition made through the platform.
Is technical support available during live sessions? expand_more
A live chat moderator is present during all scheduled broadcasts to assist with immediate technical hurdles. For more complex issues, please use the "Help" button in the sidebar to open a priority support ticket.

Still have questions?

Our concierge team is available Monday through Friday, 9am - 6pm EST to assist with your specific needs.
