auto_awesome Minting & Drops

How do I participate in a scheduled generative drop?

expand_more
Scheduled drops occur on the "Auctions" page. You must have your wallet connected and enough native currency for both the mint price and network gas fees. Each drop has a specific countdown timer.

What is a "Blind Mint" phase?

expand_more
In a blind mint, users purchase the NFT before the visual output is revealed. The metadata is updated on-chain 24-48 hours after the drop is completed, ensuring total fairness in distribution.

account_balance_wallet Wallets & Security

Which wallets are supported by Aura-NFT?

expand_more
We currently support MetaMask, WalletConnect, and Coinbase Wallet. For the highest security on high-value generative pieces, we recommend using a hardware wallet (Ledger or Trezor) linked via MetaMask.

I lost access to my wallet. Can Aura-NFT recover it?

expand_more
No. Aura-NFT is a non-custodial marketplace. We never have access to your seed phrase or private keys. Your security and access are solely your responsibility on the blockchain.
