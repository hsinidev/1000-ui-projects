Print Care
& Shipping

Always use acid-free glassine paper between sheets. Store flat in a climate-controlled environment, away from direct UV light to prevent ink degradation.

Global transit typically takes 7-14 business days. All prints are shipped in reinforced 4-inch diameter heavy-duty tubes.

Every Chroma-Draft print features a hand-embossed seal and a unique artist signature on the reverse side or margin.
