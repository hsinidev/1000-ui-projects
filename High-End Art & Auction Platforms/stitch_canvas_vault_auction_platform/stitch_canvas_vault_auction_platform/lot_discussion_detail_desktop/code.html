Archival Narrative

Commissioned in 2021, "Abstract Resonance No. 12" represents the pinnacle of Moretti's monochromatic exploration. The work employs a specialized archival linen treated with cold-pressed gesso to ensure a lifespan exceeding three centuries without structural degradation.

The provenance of this piece is immaculately documented, having transitioned through only two private collections since its debut at the Venice Biennale. Each stroke is a testimony to the interaction between physical pigment and the void, creating a visual rhythm that demands silence from the viewer.
