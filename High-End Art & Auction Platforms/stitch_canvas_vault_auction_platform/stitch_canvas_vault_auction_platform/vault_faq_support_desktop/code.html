01.01 FUNDAMENTALS

Blockchain Provenance & Immutability

Every asset within the CANVAS-VAULT ecosystem is anchored to a decentralized ledger. This ensures that the provenance—the chronological history of ownership and location—is immutable and globally verifiable.

We utilize a hybrid-layer architecture that pairs high-resolution metadata with cryptographic hashes, ensuring that even the most minute details of an artwork's history are preserved without risk of alteration.

01.02 FUNDAMENTALS

Digital Twin IDs

What is a Digital Twin?

A Digital Twin is a precise, high-fidelity digital counterpart to a physical artwork. It includes multi-spectral imaging, chemical composition data, and 3D surface mapping. This 'DNA' of the artwork is what we use to verify authenticity during every vault entry and exit.

Verification Frequency

Verification occurs at every stage of movement. From the moment an asset leaves a collector's hands to its placement in our climate-controlled cells, the Digital Twin is cross-referenced using AI-driven visual analysis.

02.01 THE VAULT

Clinical-Grade Vault Security

ATMOSPHERIC CONTROL

Precision-tuned environments maintaining a constant 20°C (±0.5°) and 50% Relative Humidity (±2%) to prevent any molecular degradation of canvas or pigment.

SECURITY PROTOCOL

Biometric dual-gate entry, seismic sensors, and 24/7 kinetic response teams ensure absolute physical sovereignty over the collection.

Need Human Assistance?

Our archivists and security experts are available for private consultation regarding high-value acquisitions and vaulting logistics.
