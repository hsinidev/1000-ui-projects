Investor FAQ

Understanding Fractional Ownership & Art Liquidity

What exactly is fractional art ownership? expand_more
Fractional ownership allows multiple investors to own a portion of a high-value physical artwork through blockchain-backed security tokens. Each token represents a direct legal interest in the physical piece, enabling investors to access blue-chip art assets with lower capital requirements.
How is the art valued and verified? expand_more
Our internal curators and third-party appraisers from institutions like Sotheby's and Christie's provide quarterly valuations. Every piece is verified for provenance and stored in climate-controlled freeports in Geneva or Delaware.
How do I exit my position? expand_more
You can sell your fractions on our proprietary secondary marketplace at any time, providing liquidity that traditional art ownership lacks. Alternatively, if the platform votes to sell the physical work to an external collector, all fractional holders receive their pro-rata share of the sale price.
