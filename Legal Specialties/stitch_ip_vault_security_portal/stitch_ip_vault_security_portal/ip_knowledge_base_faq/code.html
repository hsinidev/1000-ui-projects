FREQUENTLY ASKED QUERIES

How do I transfer ownership of a secured patent? expand_more

Ownership transfers require a verified Assignment Deed uploaded via the 'Vault' interface. Both the assignor and assignee must provide biometric confirmation or hardware key authentication to finalize the cryptographic ledger update.

What is the status of my 2023 WIPO application? expand_more

You can track real-time WIPO status by entering your tracking ID in the 'Patents' dashboard. Status updates are pulled directly from the International Bureau every 4 hours.

Can I store digital assets other than legal documents? expand_more

Yes. The Vault supports encrypted storage of CAD files, source code repositories, and proprietary algorithms. Each asset is timestamped and hashed for immutable proof of existence.

How are trademark disputes mediated within the platform? expand_more

IP-Vault provides a 'Resolution Chamber' where legal teams can exchange evidence and proposals within a monitored, secure environment, significantly reducing traditional litigation timelines.
