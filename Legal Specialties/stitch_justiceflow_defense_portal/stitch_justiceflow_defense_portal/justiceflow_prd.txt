# JusticeFlow Project Requirements

## Brand Identity
- **Name:** JusticeFlow: Criminal Defense & Civil Liberties
- **Aesthetic:** Courageous & Bold
- **Colors:** Deep Charcoal, Alert Red, Pure White
- **UX Goal:** Trust-centric, stable, authoritative

## Required Pages (Mobile)
1. **Home / Practice Areas:** Overview of Felony, DUI, and White Collar defense.
2. **'Your Rights' Education Hub:** Resource center for legal education.
3. **'What to do if Arrested' Emergency Guide:** Quick-access mobile guide for urgent situations.
4. **Attorney Profiles:** Introducing the legal team to build trust.
5. **Case Consultation Form:** Lead generation and contact point.

## UX Specifications
- **Floating Action Button (FAB):** Prominent 'Call Now' button.
- **Header/Nav:** 24/7 Legal Hotline always visible or easily accessible.
- **Typography:** Bold sans-serif headers.
- **Psychology:** Focus on 'Stability' and courage.
