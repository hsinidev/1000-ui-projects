IF YOU ARE ARRESTED: ACT NOW

The first 60 minutes are critical. Your actions and words during this window determine the trajectory of your defense. Stay calm, stay silent, and call JusticeFlow.

security

CRITICAL CHECKLIST

1

DO NOT RESIST

Even if the arrest is unlawful, resisting physically will only lead to additional charges and potential injury. Comply with physical commands but remain verbally silent.

2

EXERCISE THE 5TH

"I AM EXERCISING MY RIGHT TO REMAIN SILENT AND I WANT AN ATTORNEY."

Say this once. Do not answer questions about where you were, who you were with, or what you were doing.

3

NO CONSENT TO SEARCH

Do not give permission to search your car, home, or phone. If they proceed anyway, do not physically stop them, but state clearly: "I DO NOT CONSENT TO THIS SEARCH."

THE LEGAL TIMELINE

HOUR 0-2

Booking & Processing

You will be taken to a station, photographed, and fingerprinted. Do not discuss your case with other detainees or officers. Request your phone call immediately.

HOUR 2-24

Initial Appearance

A judge will inform you of the charges and decide on bail. Having a JusticeFlow attorney present during this window significantly increases your chances of being released on your own recognizance.

DAY 2-5

Evidence Review

Our investigators begin securing bodycam footage, witness statements, and electronic data before it can be lost or "misplaced" by the prosecution.

Know Your Miranda

If the police fail to read your rights before an interrogation while you are in custody, the statements you make may be inadmissible in court. However, do not rely on this—stay silent regardless.

URGENT Q&A

Q: What if I can't afford my one phone call?

A: You have a legal right to contact an attorney. If you cannot afford the call, the precinct must facilitate access to legal counsel.

Q: Can I talk to my family about what happened?

A: NO. All jail calls are recorded and monitored. Discuss the facts of your case ONLY with your JusticeFlow attorney during private consultations.

Q: How soon can JusticeFlow get there?

A: Our rapid response team is dispatched immediately upon verification of your location. We aim for representation within 4 hours in major metro areas.
