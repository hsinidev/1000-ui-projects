How does the 2024 CRISPR mandate affect retroactive patents?

Retroactive enforcement is limited to genetic edits performed after January 1st, 2024. See section 14.b of the Federal Bio-Equity Act for specific clause exceptions regarding somatic cell therapy.

Compliance requirements for synthetic sequence archival?

All synthetic sequences exceeding 200 base pairs must be logged with the Central Bio-Registry (CBR) within 48 hours of synthesis.
