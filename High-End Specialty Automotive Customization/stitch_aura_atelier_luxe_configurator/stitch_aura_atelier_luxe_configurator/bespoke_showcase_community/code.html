Bespoke Manufacturing FAQ

Can I request a finish not listed in the configurator? expand_more

Yes. Our Atelier Program allows for complete material freedom. We can work with exotic alloys, custom anodization, or specific wood grains upon request. Please contact a Bespoke Specialist to begin the consultation.

What is the structural weight limit for the S2? expand_more

The Aura Signature S2 is engineered for a static load of up to 85kg when assembled with our reinforced architectural glazing. For custom commercial applications requiring higher load bearing, we provide reinforced titanium frame options.

How is shipping handled for international bespoke orders? expand_more

We utilize white-glove architectural logistics. Each piece is crated in a custom-built, climate-controlled enclosure and shipped via air freight. On-site assembly by our technicians is available for selected regions.
