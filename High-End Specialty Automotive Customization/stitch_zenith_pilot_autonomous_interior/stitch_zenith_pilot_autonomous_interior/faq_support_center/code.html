How do I initiate the pre-flight sequence?

expand_more

Ensure all internal systems are green on your primary console. Navigate to the cockpit interface, select 'Systems Check,' and follow the automated sequence. For manual override, refer to the tactile safety manual located in the recessed compartment beneath seat 1-A.

What are the critical safety protocols for high-altitude flight?

expand_more

At altitudes exceeding 30,000 feet, the vehicle enters pressurized cruise mode. Ensure the 'Bio-Sync' sensors are active to monitor oxygen levels. In the event of cabin depressurization, the vehicle will automatically initiate a rapid descent to a safe atmospheric level within 45 seconds.

Managing your vehicle's connectivity and satellite sync

expand_more

Your Zenith-Pilot utilizes the Aurora Satellite Network. Connection is typically seamless; however, if you encounter latency in remote regions, go to 'Connectivity Settings' and toggle 'Global Mesh Mode' to prioritize low-earth orbit arrays.

Routine maintenance schedule for Zenith-Pilot modules

expand_more

Autonomous modules require diagnostic scans every 10,000 km. Zenith Concierge will automatically schedule a drone-assisted service during your vehicle's stationary hours at your primary residence.
