The Vintage EV Handbook

To maintain the tactile feel of original unassisted drum or disc brakes while maximizing range, we recommend the 'Brake-Link' calibration. This ensures the first 15% of pedal travel engages the magnetic motor resistance before the mechanical calipers bite.
Heat dissipation is critical. Our low-profile liquid cooling plates are designed to sit beneath original carpeting without compromising the 1960s interior aesthetic. Ensure airflow vents are cleaned bi-annually.
The Heritage-EV Digital-to-Analog (DTA) interface allows your original tachometer and fuel gauge to display RPM and State of Charge respectively, maintaining the period-correct dashboard soul.
