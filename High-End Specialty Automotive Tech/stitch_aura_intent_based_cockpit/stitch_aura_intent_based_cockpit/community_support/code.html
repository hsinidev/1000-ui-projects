Vehicle Performance

How does the Adaptive Aero-Logic optimize drag at high speeds? expand_more

The Aura GT utilizes active carbon-fiber flaps that adjust in real-time based on velocity and lateral G-forces. This reduces drag by up to 12% during straight-line cruising while maximizing downforce during aggressive cornering.

Calibration procedures for the Track-Specific Suspension. expand_more

Suspension calibration can be initiated via the Cockpit interface under 'Chassis Dynamics'. Ensure the vehicle is on a level surface with tire pressures adjusted to track specifications before starting the 3-minute diagnostic cycle.

bolt

Power & Charging

Optimal temperature range for Ultra-Rapid charging. expand_more

The 800V architecture performs optimally when the battery pre-conditioning system brings the pack to 28°C-32°C. Charging at 350kW will throttle slightly if cell temperatures exceed 45°C to preserve long-term battery health.

system_update

Software & OTA Updates

How to verify the integrity of an OTA firmware package? expand_more

All updates are signed with a unique cryptographic key. You can view the 'Software Version' panel in the cockpit to see the SHA-256 hash of your current firmware, matching the release notes provided in your Aura App.
