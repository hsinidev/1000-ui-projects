Air Purification Systems

filter_alt How often should I replace the HEPA-Bio membrane?
expand_more
Our proprietary HEPA-Bio membranes are designed for longevity. For standard urban environments, we recommend a replacement every 12 months. However, the BioLiving app monitors molecular saturation in real-time and will notify you exactly when the filtration efficiency drops below 98.4%.
waves Can the system filter out organic volatile compounds (VOCs)?
expand_more
Yes. The secondary activated carbon layer is specifically treated to neutralize VOCs from furniture off-gassing, paints, and household cleaners. The system automatically ramps up intensity when detecting sudden spikes in chemical signatures.

science What minerals are added during the remineralization stage?

Circadian Lighting Harmony

wb_twilight How does the 'Sunset Protocol' improve sleep quality?
expand_more
Starting 90 minutes before your scheduled sleep time, the system removes all blue light wavelengths below 480nm. It transitions through warm amber tones that signal your pineal gland to begin natural melatonin production, ensuring deep, restorative REM cycles.
