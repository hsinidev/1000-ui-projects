Technical Inquiries

Our automated DSP calibration uses impulse response measurements to map the specific resonance of your space. We recommend recalibrating whenever physical furniture shifts more than 30cm or if wall treatments are modified.

The "Hidden Audio" series requires a minimum wall cavity of 75mm. For acoustic transparency, ensure the plaster thickness does not exceed 12mm over the active diaphragm. Use only approved sonic-transparent finishes.
