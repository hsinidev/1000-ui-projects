How does circadian syncing integrate with KNX systems? expand_more

Lumina's proprietary control bridge provides native translation layers for KNX, Control4, and Lutron protocols. By mapping the 24-hour spectrum onto standard architectural telegrams, our systems maintain precise Kelvin temperature curves (2200K to 6500K) while respecting existing scene hierarchies.

What is the maximum run length for Lumina Linear Graze? expand_more

Our Graze Series utilizes high-efficiency constant current drivers that allow for continuous runs up to 12 meters (40 feet) on a single home run. For longer architectural expanses, we recommend distributed power architecture to maintain voltage drop within 2%.

Can I program custom light scenes without a professional integrator? expand_more

While we advocate for professional commissioning, the Lumina Studio mobile application provides a refined "User Layer" interface. This allows homeowners to modify presets and adjust intensity within safe architectural parameters defined during the initial installation.

Maintenance requirements for outdoor lumen maintenance? expand_more

All Lumina exterior fixtures are IP67-rated with marine-grade anodic finishes. We recommend bi-annual inspection of gaskets and lens cleaning with pH-neutral solutions to ensure 98.4% optical transmission remains consistent over the L70 lifetime of 100,000 hours.
