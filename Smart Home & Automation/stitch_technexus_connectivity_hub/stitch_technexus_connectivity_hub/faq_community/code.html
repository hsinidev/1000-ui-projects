How do I bridge Matter devices from other ecosystems?
expand_more

To integrate Matter-enabled devices, ensure your Nexus Hub is running firmware v2.4 or higher. Open the "Add Device" menu, select "Matter over Thread/Wi-Fi," and scan the QR code provided with your hardware.
