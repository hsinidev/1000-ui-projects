Investment Thesis

The Ritz-Carlton Reserve, Marbella represents a generational opportunity to acquire a trophy asset in the most resilient luxury corridor of Southern Europe. Strategically positioned between the Golden Mile and Puerto Banús, the property captures both the traditional aristocratic demand and the surging ultra-high-net-worth segment from the emerging technology hubs in Malaga.

Recent capital improvements have elevated the property beyond standard luxury tiering, achieving the "Reserve" designation—the highest mark of exclusivity within the Marriott portfolio. The acquisition includes prime seaside expansion rights and an unutilized 4,000 sqm development parcel currently zoned for luxury wellness facilities.
