Investment Strategy

expand_more
What criteria are used to select target multi-family properties? add
We utilize a rigorous 14-point proprietary data model focusing on sub-market rental growth, local employment diversification, and asset-level value-add opportunities. Our team prioritizes Class B+ assets in high-growth Sunbelt and Mountain West corridors where institutional capital is concentrated.
How does the fund manage interest rate risk? add
The MultiFamilyHub utilizes long-term, fixed-rate financing through agency lenders (Fannie Mae/Freddie Mac) and interest rate caps for any floating-rate bridge debt to ensure capital preservation.

Reporting & Distributions

When are quarterly performance reports released? add
Quarterly reports are typically uploaded to the Investment Portal within 20 business days following the close of each calendar quarter.
What is the standard distribution schedule? add
Distributions are processed on a quarterly basis, typically on the last business day of the month following the quarter end.
