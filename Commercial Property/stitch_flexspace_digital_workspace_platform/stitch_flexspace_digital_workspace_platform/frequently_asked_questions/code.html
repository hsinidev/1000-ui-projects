What is included in the Resident Membership? expand_more

Resident members enjoy 24/7 access to their home location, a dedicated desk with ergonomic seating, 10 hours of monthly meeting room credits, and full access to all community networking events and wellness workshops.

Can I pause my membership? expand_more

Yes, members can pause their accounts for up to 3 months per calendar year for a small administrative fee. Please notify your community manager 15 days before the next billing cycle.

calendar_month Booking

How do I book a conference room? expand_more

You can book rooms directly through the 'Booking' tab in your member portal or mobile app. Simply select your location, desired time slot, and room size. Credits will be automatically deducted from your monthly balance.

What kind of technical support is available? expand_more

We provide on-site IT support during standard business hours (9 AM - 6 PM) for WiFi connectivity, printing issues, and presentation equipment setup in conference rooms.
