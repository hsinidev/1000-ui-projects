Frequently Asked Questions

Leasing Terms expand_more

Our standard leasing agreements are designed for high-frequency corporate engagement. VenueVault facilitates tiered contracts ranging from single-day gala exclusivity to multi-year preferred vendor status. All contracts include mandatory liability coverage and professional cleaning escrow as standard prestige requirements.

AV Customization expand_more

Elite venues listed on our platform are equipped with state-of-the-art 4K projection mapping and Dolby Atmos integration. Customization requests must be submitted 14 business days prior to the event date to allow for onsite architectural acoustic calibration.

Catering Guidelines expand_more

To maintain the integrity of our vaulted surfaces, all external catering must be selected from our pre-vetted 'Grand Tier' list of Michelin-recognized partners. Custom kitchen setups require specific load-in permits which can be managed directly through your VenueVault Concierge.
