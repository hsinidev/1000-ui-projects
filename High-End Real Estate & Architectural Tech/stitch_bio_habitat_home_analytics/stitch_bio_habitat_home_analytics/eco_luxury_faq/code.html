Energy Independence

Harnessing ambient forces for complete off-grid autonomy.

How does the Mycelium-Battery storage system perform in winter? expand_more
Our proprietary Mycelium-Battery units utilize a biological thermal-inertia layer that keeps the core temperature stable even at -20°C. The efficiency drop is limited to less than 4% compared to standard lithium-ion batteries which can lose up to 30%. This allows for consistent energy discharge throughout the darkest winter months.
Can surplus energy be traded with the local neighborhood grid? expand_more
Yes, every Bio-Habitat home comes equipped with a Smart-Node interface that automatically manages peer-to-peer energy trading via decentralized protocols. You can set your preference to prioritize local community centers or sell back to the primary utility during peak demand.
