Buying Process

Details on elite property acquisition and digital transaction security.

How do I schedule a private AR walkthrough?

expand_more
Scheduling is handled exclusively through your assigned concierge. Once your credentials are verified, you can select a time slot within the Vista-AR portal. A custom AR lens link will be generated for your specific device, allowing a 1:1 real-time walkthrough with our lead brokers.

Are digital contracts legally binding in all jurisdictions?

expand_more
Vista-AR utilizes enterprise-grade smart contracts that comply with the Uniform Electronic Transactions Act (UETA) and eSign Act. For international acquisitions, our legal team ensures compliance with local property laws in the EU, Middle East, and Asia-Pacific regions.

What verification is required for high-value transactions?

expand_more
To maintain the exclusivity of our marketplace, all potential buyers must undergo a secure Proof of Funds (POF) verification process. This is handled via a secure, encrypted bridge to your financial institution, ensuring your privacy while satisfying AML compliance requirements.

Can I invite my architect to the AR session?

expand_more
Yes. Our "Collaborative Viewing" feature allows you to invite up to three verified guests to any AR walkthrough. Guests can interact with the environment, leave spatial notes, and view the same high-fidelity property models simultaneously from different global locations.
