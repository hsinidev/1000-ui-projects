How do I commission a custom atelier project? expand_more
Our Studio services are exclusively by invitation. To request an assessment, navigate to the Studio tab and submit your project overview. A curator will review your proposal within 72 hours to ensure alignment with the MUSE aesthetic.
Sourcing authenticity and material traceability. expand_more
Every piece featured in the Library is accompanied by a certificate of provenance. We prioritize artisans who utilize sustainable extraction methods for marble and recycled metals for our signature rose gold finishes.
Shipping logistics for global installations. expand_more
We provide white-glove, international logistical support for all physical acquisitions. This includes temperature-controlled transport for delicate wood and glass elements.
