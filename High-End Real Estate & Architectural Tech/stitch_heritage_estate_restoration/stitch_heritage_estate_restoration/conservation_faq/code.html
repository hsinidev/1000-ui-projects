What defines a Grade I listing mandate?

expand_less

Grade I buildings are of exceptional interest, sometimes considered to be internationally important. Only 2.5% of listed buildings are Grade I. Restoration requires specific consent for any internal or external alterations, often necessitating a 'Statement of Significance' before any work commences.
