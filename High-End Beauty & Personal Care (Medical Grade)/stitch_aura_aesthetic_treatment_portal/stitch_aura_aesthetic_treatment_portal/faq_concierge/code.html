Pre-Treatment Guide

General Consultation Preparation expand_more

To ensure you receive the most personalized care during your visit to Aesthetica, we recommend the following preparations:

  • Arrive 15 minutes prior to your scheduled appointment.
  • Avoid alcohol and blood-thinning medications 24 hours prior to injectables.
  • Please arrive with a clean face, free of heavy makeup or skincare products.
  • Bring a list of your current medications and skincare routine.
Laser Treatment Requirements expand_more

Strict sun avoidance is required for 4 weeks prior to most laser services. Please discontinue use of Retinol products at least 7 days before your appointment.

Post-Care Excellence

ice_skating

Dermal Fillers & Botox

Avoid strenuous exercise for 24 hours. Keep upright for 4 hours post-treatment. Minor swelling is normal and can be managed with cold compresses.

face_5

Chemical Peels & Facials

Avoid direct sun exposure for 7-10 days. Apply SPF 30+ daily. Do not pick or pull at any peeling skin to prevent scarring.
