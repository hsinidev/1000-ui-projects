Pre-Op Preparation

What medications should I avoid before surgery? expand_more

Patients should strictly avoid blood thinners, including Aspirin and certain herbal supplements like Vitamin E or Ginko Biloba, for at least 7 days prior to their procedure. A complete list is provided in your clinical dashboard.

Is a scalp massage regimen required? expand_more

Yes, for FUT procedures, 10-20 minutes of daily scalp laxity exercises are recommended for 4 weeks prior to improve donor area flexibility.

Recovery Protocols

When can I return to high-intensity exercise? expand_more

We recommend light walking from Day 2. However, heavy weightlifting or high-impact cardio should be avoided for 14 days to prevent increased blood pressure and graft displacement.

How do I manage post-op crusting? expand_more

Gentle washing with our provided surgical shampoo starting on Day 3 will gradually soften crusts. Do not pick or scratch the grafts.

shopping_basket

Maintenance Products

Can I use Minoxidil immediately after? expand_more

Topical Minoxidil should be paused for at least 10 days post-procedure to ensure the recipient sites are fully closed and avoid irritation.
