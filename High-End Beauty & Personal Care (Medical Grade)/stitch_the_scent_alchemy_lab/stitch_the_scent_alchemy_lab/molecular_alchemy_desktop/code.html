THE METHODOLOGY

Alchemy of the Invisible

The extraction of scent is a sacred transformation. We employ traditional steam distillation alongside modern supercritical CO2 extraction to preserve the most delicate molecular structures of the botanicals.

Every formulation begins with the 'Silence'—a phase of absolute stillness where the raw materials are aged in tempered glass carboys, away from the light, allowing the natural resins to stabilize.

Our alchemists monitor the maturation process using high-precision chromatography, ensuring each batch meets the uncompromising standards of the Essence-Lab heritage.
