Frequently Asked Questions

Explore the alchemy of medical-grade fragrance, our bespoke design philosophy, and the preservation of ephemeral scents.

The Alchemy

What defines a 'medical-grade' fragrance? expand_more
Our medical-grade scents are formulated using hypoallergenic, bio-identical molecules and pure distilled resins. Unlike commercial perfumes, we eliminate phthalates and synthetic fixatives, ensuring the fragrance interacts harmoniously with the endocrine system while maintaining olfactory complexity.
How does the molecular extraction process work? expand_more
We utilize Supercritical CO2 Extraction. This low-temperature process preserves the fragile volatile compounds of rare botanicals that are often destroyed by traditional steam distillation, resulting in a scent profile that is a perfect mirror of the living plant.

Signature Blends

Can I recreate a scent from a specific memory? expand_more
Yes. Our 'Olfactory Memory Mapping' service allows you to provide sensory descriptors or reference objects. Our master perfumers then translate these into a molecular structure that triggers specific neural pathways associated with nostalgia and location.
What is the lead time for a custom formulation? expand_more
The creation of a signature blend requires 4-6 weeks. This includes the initial consultation, three trial iterations, and a mandatory 14-day maceration period where the molecules fuse into their final, stable profile.

Preservation

How should I store my Essence-Lab vial? expand_more
Fragrances should be kept in their original UV-shielded glass. Store in a cool, dark environment away from humidity and direct sunlight. Optimal storage temperature is between 12°C and 18°C (54°F - 64°F).
Does the scent expire? expand_more
Because our scents are medical-grade and free of synthetic stabilizers, they are 'living' formulations. If stored correctly, they remain potent for 24-36 months. Over time, the top notes may soften, while the base notes deepen in complexity.
