1. Objective

The primary objective of this Global Data Privacy Policy is to outline the mandatory guidelines for the collection, handling, and storage of personal identifiable information (PII) within the organization and its third-party affiliates.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all global operations, including subsidiaries and joint ventures where the organization maintains a majority stake. Special emphasis is placed on EEA-based data processing centers.

3. Data Classification

Data must be classified according to the Enterprise Data Classification Framework:

  • Public: Information intended for public consumption.
  • Internal: Information relevant to employees and contractors.
  • Confidential: Sensitive PII, financial records, and strategic assets.
  • Restricted: Highly sensitive data requiring executive clearance.

4. Retention Requirements

PII shall not be retained longer than necessary for the purpose for which it was collected. Standard retention cycles are set at 7 years for financial records and 3 years for marketing opt-ins.
