How do I verify the authenticity of an automated alert? expand_more

All alerts generated by Sentinel-SOC are cryptographically signed. To verify, click the 'Shield' icon in the incident header. If the signature is valid, you will see a 'VERIFIED_ORIGIN' status in green. If the signature is missing or invalid, immediately isolate the console and report to Tier 4.

What is the standard response time for Priority 1 events? expand_more

Per SLA v2024.1, all Priority 1 (P1) events require an initial assessment within 5 minutes of timestamped detection. Full containment protocols must be initiated within 15 minutes. Documentation can follow containment.

How can I suggest a new detection rule? expand_more

Rules suggestions should be submitted via the Sigma-Converter tool in the 'Investigation' tab. Ensure you include sample logs (sanitized) and the expected logic flow. Your submission will be reviewed by the Rule Engineering Team within 48 hours.
