TECHNICAL FAQ

01. How do you address low-frequency modal issues?

We utilize tuned pressure-based membrane absorbers and limp mass barriers specifically designed for the room's calculated resonant frequencies.

02. Is Room-in-Room construction always necessary?

Only for high-isolation requirements (STC 60+). We analyze structural flanking paths before recommending decoupled floor systems.

03. What is the standard measurement protocol?

We follow AES guidelines using swept sine measurements at 8 calibrated positions to ensure spatial statistical relevance.

04. Do you provide material certificates?

Yes, every material specified in our blueprints comes with verified NRC and absorption coefficient data from certified labs.
