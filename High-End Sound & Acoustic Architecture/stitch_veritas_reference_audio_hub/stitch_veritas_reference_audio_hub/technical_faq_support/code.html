Priority Documentation

Q-0114 How do I reset the V-REF-3000 to factory acoustic calibration?
expand_more

To perform a hard reset, disconnect all inputs and hold the CALIBRATE and STANDBY physical switches for 5 seconds. The DSP will cycle through its self-test phase.

> sys --reset --acoustic-params --force
Q-0209 Managing thermal overhead during 96kHz continuous processing.
expand_more

Ensure a 2U clearance in rack mounting. If internal temperature exceeds 75°C, the DSP will automatically downsample to 48kHz to preserve component integrity. Check system fans for obstruction.
