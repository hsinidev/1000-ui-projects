Published: 24 OCT 2023 | Author: Dr. Elias Thorne

"The V-REF-3000 achieves a linear transparency previously reserved for passive laboratory monitors, delivering transient response times in the sub-millisecond range."

Our rigorous testing protocols revealed an exceptionally flat frequency response from 20Hz to 40kHz, with a deviation of less than ±0.5dB. The integration of the new DSP Tuning core allows for real-time phase alignment that significantly reduces vertical lobing in critical near-field listening environments.

Distortion metrics remain negligible even at high SPL levels, maintaining a THD+N of 0.0003% at 1kHz. This is largely attributed to the custom-designed Hardware Admin interface which ensures optimal power distribution across the Class-D amplification stages.
