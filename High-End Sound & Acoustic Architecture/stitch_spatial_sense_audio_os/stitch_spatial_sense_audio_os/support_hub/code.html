auto_awesome Frequently Asked

How do I calibrate my room for Atmos playback? expand_more

Calibration requires the Spatial-Sense mobile application and a compatible microphone. Navigate to Settings > Calibration > Room Scan. Place your mobile device in the primary listening position at ear level.

info

Tip: Ensure all physical obstructions are removed from the line of sight between the speakers and the listening position for 192kHz accuracy.

Can I use third-party wireless transmitters? expand_more

While Spatial-Sense supports standard WiSA and DLNA protocols, we recommend using the proprietary Spatial-Link bridge to ensure zero-latency synchronization between multi-room zones. Third-party transmitters may introduce jitter in high-resolution audio streams exceeding 96kHz.

Setting up Zone Grouping for the first time expand_more

Open the Sources tab in the control interface. Long-press on any active Zone and drag it onto another Zone to create a "Cluster." You can then rename the cluster and adjust individual volume levels within the group while maintaining master volume control.

Mastering the Equalizer settings expand_more

The Advanced Equalizer uses a 32-band parametric engine. For a natural acoustic profile, we recommend starting with the "Linear Flat" preset and making subtle ±2dB adjustments in the 2kHz–5kHz range to enhance vocal clarity without adding harshness.
