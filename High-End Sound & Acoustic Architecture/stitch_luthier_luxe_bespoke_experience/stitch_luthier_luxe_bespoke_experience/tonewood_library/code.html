Top Wood

Sitka Spruce

Picea sitchensis

The gold standard for modern acoustic tops. Known for its broad dynamic range and crisp articulation, Sitka provides a robust fundamental tone that matures beautifully over decades of play.

Sound Profile 0:14
Harvested: British Columbia, Canada
Back & Sides

Brazilian Rosewood

Dalbergia nigra

The "Holy Grail" of tonewoods. Unsurpassed for its glassy chime, deep bass response, and complex overtones. Highly sought after and protected, these sets are sourced from pre-convention stock.

Sound Profile 0:22
CITES Certified Legacy Stock
Body & Neck

Genuine Mahogany

Swietenia macrophylla

Prized for its warm, punchy, and fundamental-driven tone. Mahogany emphasizes the mid-range frequencies, providing a "woody" and focused sound that is preferred by blues and folk musicians.

Sound Profile 0:18
Sustainably Sourced: Honduras
