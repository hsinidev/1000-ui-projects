Master's Guide

The Custom Build Timeline

expand_more

Every instrument crafted in our atelier undergoes a rigorous 12 to 18-month journey. From the initial selection of tonewoods to the final nitrocellulose lacquer curing, we prioritize resonance over speed. You will receive monthly status updates through your Artisan Admin portal.

Global Transit & Climate Control

expand_more

We utilize bespoke, carbon-fiber reinforced flight cases for all shipments. Each instrument is monitored via a humidity and temperature logging sensor during transit, ensuring it arrives in the exact micro-climate of our workshop.

Selecting Resonant Tonewoods

expand_more

We source aged Spruce from the Fiemme Valley and master-grade Mahogany from sustainable reserves. During your consultation, we perform tap-testing on various billets, allowing you to choose the specific wood set that matches your desired tonal profile.
