Technical Schematics

Vacuum Tube Bias Alignment expand_more
Achieving the golden-ratio bias for the KT88 series requires a dampening period of 30 minutes. Use the integrated brass rotary knobs to align the needle to the 12 o'clock position on the VU meter for optimal harmonic resonance.
Phono-Stage Impedance Matching expand_more
Our discrete JFET phono-stage allows for manual loading between 47kΩ and 100kΩ. For Moving Coil cartridges, ensure the steel toggle is set to 'Low' to maintain the signal-to-noise ratio.

Maintenance Protocols

Walnut Chassis Preservation expand_more
Each Aureum chassis is hand-rubbed with natural beeswax. To maintain the grain's depth, use a lint-free silk cloth and apply minimal pressure in a circular motion every six lunar cycles.
Brass Contact De-oxidation expand_more
Solid brass terminals develop a patina over time, which can slightly alter conductivity. Use the provided precision brush and a drop of anhydrous alcohol to gently cleanse the binding posts annually.
