Technical Setup

How do I configure bit-perfect playback on macOS? expand_more

To achieve bit-perfect output, navigate to the Muse-Streaming Audio Settings and enable 'Exclusive Mode'. This bypasses the macOS system mixer, ensuring that the audio stream is delivered directly to your DAC at its native sampling rate without any resampling artifacts.

Which external DACs are natively supported? expand_more

Muse-Streaming supports all USB Class 2.0 compliant digital-to-analog converters. We have specific driver optimizations for the following architectures:

  • ESS SABRE
  • AKM VELVET SOUND
  • CHORD FPGA
  • R2R LADDER DACs

Lossless Audio Specs

MASTER What is the peak resolution of Muse Master tracks?
expand_more

Our Master tier offers PCM audio up to 24-bit / 192kHz. These files are sourced directly from studio master tapes or high-resolution digital masters, ensuring zero compression loss. For specific titles, we also provide DSD64 and DSD128 stream options where available from the label.

Does Muse-Streaming support spatial audio formats? expand_more

Yes, we support immersive audio via Object-Based mixing. Currently, our library includes a vast selection of albums mixed in Dolby Atmos and 360 Reality Audio. These can be decoded natively by supported hardware or virtualized for any pair of high-quality headphones.

How do I upgrade to the Audiophile Master tier? expand_more

Access your 'Audiophile Member' profile from the navigation drawer. Under 'Subscription Details', select 'Manage Tier' and choose 'Master Tier'. Upgrades are processed instantly, unlocking immediate access to the 24-bit lossless library and offline master downloads.
