Travel Requirements

What health certifications are required for international flights? expand_more

For international travel, companions require a USDA-endorsed international health certificate (Form 7001) issued within 10 days of travel. Specific requirements vary by destination—our Concierge team will provide a personalized checklist 30 days before your departure.
