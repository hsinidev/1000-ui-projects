Frequent Inquiries

What protocols govern private air travel for large breeds? expand_more

Our Canine-Elite Travel Concierge handles all manifest details. Large breeds reside in-cabin on chartered flights, provided they meet our behavior certification. We manage all international health certifications, quarantine bypasses, and ground transportation in climate-controlled luxury sedans.

How are biometrics integrated into the daily wellness feed? expand_more

The Elite Collar Sync transmits real-time heart rate variability, sleep cycle efficiency, and calorie expenditure directly to your dashboard. This data is reviewed weekly by our veterinary partners to preemptively adjust nutrition and exercise regimens.

Can the Gourmet Nutrition plan accommodate rare allergies? expand_more

Absolutely. Our culinary team works alongside immunologists to develop bespoke recipes using human-grade, sustainably sourced proteins. Every meal is formulated in a sterile environment to ensure zero cross-contamination.
