POST-SURGERY

Passive Range of Motion (PROM)

1

Gently guide the limb through its natural range until slight resistance is met. Hold for 5 seconds.

2

Slowly return the limb to a resting position. Repeat this cycle 10 times for each affected joint.

play_arrow
SENIOR MOBILITY

Core Stability Balance

1

Encourage your pet to stand on a soft, non-slip surface. Gently shift their weight from side to side.

2

Lift one paw slightly off the ground for 3 seconds, encouraging the use of core stabilizers.
