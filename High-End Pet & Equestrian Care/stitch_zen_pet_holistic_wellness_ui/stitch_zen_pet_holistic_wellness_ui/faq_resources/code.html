Treatment Prep

For your initial assessment, please bring any previous veterinary records, including X-rays or surgical reports. We also recommend bringing your pet's favorite low-calorie treats and a well-fitted harness. A calm environment is key, so arriving 10 minutes early to let your pet sniff and acclimate to the space is highly beneficial.
