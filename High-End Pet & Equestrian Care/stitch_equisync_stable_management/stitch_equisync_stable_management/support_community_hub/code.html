FREQUENTLY ASKED

01
How do I authorize emergency vet alerts for individual staff members?

Access the 'Staff Admin' portal and select the individual profile. Under 'Permissions', toggle the 'Emergency Alerts' switch. This will ensure they receive push notifications for critical health biometric dips.

02
Managing data-privacy for high-profile owners?

Ascot Stables uses end-to-end encryption for all owner-horse associations. You can set specific horses to 'Private Mode' in Horse Management, which hides their lineage and location from general staff access.

03
Automating monthly board and training invoices?

Navigate to Financials > Billing Cycles. You can define tiered board rates and link them to specific stalls. Invoices are generated on the 1st of every month and sent via the secure portal.
