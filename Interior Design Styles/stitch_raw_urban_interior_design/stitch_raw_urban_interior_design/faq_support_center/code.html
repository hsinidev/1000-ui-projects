Material Specs

We utilize cold-rolled Grade 50 structural steel exclusively. Each member is treated with a proprietary oxidizing agent to achieve the weathered aesthetic without compromising structural integrity. Our finishes are sealed with a matte architectural-grade poly-sealant to prevent further oxidation indoors.

Our timber is salvaged from decommissioned textile mills across the Northeast. Each beam is kiln-dried to a consistent 8% moisture content to prevent warping in climate-controlled environments.

  • Species: White Oak & Heart Pine
  • Origin: 19th Century Industrial Sites
  • Load Capacity: Structural Grade A
