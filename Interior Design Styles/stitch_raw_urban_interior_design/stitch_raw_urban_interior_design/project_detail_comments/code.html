RESIDENTIAL / ADAPTIVE REUSE

THE FOUNDRY LOFTS

A meticulous restoration of a 19th-century metalworking facility into eighteen bespoke luxury residences. Structural honesty meets contemporary refinement in the heart of the industrial district.

COMPLETION 2024
TOTAL AREA 42,000 SQFT

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Designed with an uncompromising commitment to the heritage of the site, The Foundry Lofts preserves the raw tectonic expression of its industrial past. Every weld, rivet, and brick has been analyzed for its contribution to the narrative of permanence. The interior program emphasizes open-plan fluidity, carved out of the massive volumes of the original structure.

architecture

Structural Core

Exposed reinforced concrete columns and blackened steel trusses form the primary aesthetic and structural framework of the units.

texture

Materiality

The palette includes hand-troweled concrete, reclaimed white oak flooring, and custom-cast iron hardware throughout.
