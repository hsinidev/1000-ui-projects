Our Preservation Philosophy

We believe that architecture is a living archive of human aspiration. Our remodeling practice does not seek to erase the past, but to dialogue with it. By meticulously researching original blueprints and sourcing period-authentic materials, we breathe new life into structures while honoring their structural soul.

Whether it's restoring the structural integrity of a post-and-beam frame or integrating modern HVAC systems into a classic Eichler, our approach is surgical. We utilize 1950s walnut joinery techniques alongside contemporary energy-efficient glazing to create homes that are both historical artifacts and modern sanctuaries.
