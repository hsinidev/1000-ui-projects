Sourcing & Materials

A curated selection of natural textures that define our tactile architectural language. Each material is ethically sourced and chosen for its ability to age with grace.

Pale Oak

Denmark / Sustainable Forestry

Precision-milled planks with a silk-touch finish, designed for warmth and acoustic dampening.

Raw Linen

Belgium / Traditional Weave

Heavyweight textile with natural slubs, providing a soft, diffused light when used as architectural drapery.

Island Stone

Gotland / Volcanic Origin

Hand-picked volcanic rock, honed to a velvet-matte surface for monolithic furniture and floor transitions.

Sustainability Standard

We prioritize suppliers with FSC certification and carbon-neutral logistics. Every material in our journal is vetted for environmental impact and longevity.

Custom Dimensions

All our stone and wood products can be tailored to specific architectural dimensions. Our workshop in Stockholm handles bespoke milling and finishing.

Maintenance & Patina

We believe in the beauty of age. Our materials are treated with natural oils that allow them to develop a unique patina over decades without losing structural integrity.

inventory_2

Lead Times

Sourcing rare materials requires patience. Average lead times range from 8 to 14 weeks depending on the quarry or mill's current extraction cycle.
