01. Ethical Harvest

We only partner with suppliers who guarantee fair wages and safe working conditions, verified through independent annual audits.

02. Circular Life

Every material in our catalog is chosen for its ability to be safely returned to the earth or repurposed into new forms.

03. Local Craft

80% of our sourcing happens within a 500km radius of our studio to minimize transport emissions and support local heritage.
