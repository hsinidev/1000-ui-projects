Design Commandments

palette
Identify a Common Thread

Success lies in the palette. Ensure all chosen patterns share at least one unifying color. This visual 'anchor' allows disparate prints to talk to each other without shouting.

02. Rule of Odds

Mixes of three or five patterns feel more natural and curated than even numbers, which can appear too forced or symmetrical.

"The eye seeks rhythm, not perfection."
03. Breathable Space

Intersperse busy patterns with solid textures like velvet or linen to give the eye a place to rest.
