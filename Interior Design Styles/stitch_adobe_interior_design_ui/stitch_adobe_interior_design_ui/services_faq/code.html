Common Curiosities

Answering the most frequent inquiries about building and living within the mirage.

search
How do you handle high desert heat? expand_more
We utilize thermal mass engineering—thick adobe walls that absorb heat during the day and release it slowly at night. Strategic orientation and wind-catchers further ensure natural cooling without over-reliance on energy-heavy systems.
What is the timeline for a full renovation? expand_more
Typically, a comprehensive tactile renovation takes between 6 to 9 months, including the sourcing of artisanal materials and specialized craftsmanship.
Do you offer remote consulting? expand_more
Yes, our Blueprinting and Interior Curation services are available globally through digital collaboration, while Landscape execution is currently limited to the Southwest.
