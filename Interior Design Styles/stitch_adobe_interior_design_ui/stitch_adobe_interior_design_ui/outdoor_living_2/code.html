"Any work of architecture which does not express serenity is a mistake."

Sculptural Xeriscaping

Our approach to xeriscaping transcends simple water conservation. We curate living galleries of drought-resistant flora—Silver Torch cacti, blue agaves, and wild sage—arranged as organic sculptures within fields of crushed volcanic rock and river stones.
