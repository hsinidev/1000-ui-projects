# Nordic Soul Project Specification

## Brand Identity
- **Name:** Nordic Soul
- **Style:** Scandinavian & Hygge Minimalism
- **Aesthetic:** Airy & Bright
- **Color Palette:** Pale Oak, White, Charcoal
- **Vibe:** High-end coffee-table book, editorial, minimalist

## UX Requirements
- Maximum white space
- Thin-line icons
- Interactive "Light & Texture" slider (simulating natural light changes)
- Mobile-first, editorial layout

## Required Screens
1. **Portfolio:** A mix of grid and full-width layouts showcasing interior design projects.
2. **The 'Hygge' Philosophy:** Editorial content page explaining the brand's core philosophy.
3. **Sourcing & Materials:** Information on materials used, emphasizing texture and quality.
4. **Client Consultation:** A refined intake form/contact page for potential clients.
