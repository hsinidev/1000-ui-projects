Questions of Process

Sustainability & Sourcing expand_more
We prioritize reclaimed timber from the Kyoto prefecture and ethically sourced Scandinavian Oak. Every piece is finished with natural oils, ensuring zero-VOC emissions and a lifecycle that honors the earth from which it came.
Shipping & Placement expand_more
Our logistics partners are trained in the handling of delicate joinery. Each shipment includes a guide on 'Ma'—how to position your new piece to maximize negative space and airflow within your dwelling.
The Design Process expand_more
It begins with silence. We observe the material's grain for three days before the first cut. This ensures the structural integrity of the wood dictates the final silhouette, resulting in furniture that feels discovered rather than manufactured.
