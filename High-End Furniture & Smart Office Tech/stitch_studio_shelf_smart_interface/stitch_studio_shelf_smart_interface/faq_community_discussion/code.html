Essential Inquiries

Synchronization is achieved via the integrated Hub module. Once your shelf is physically connected, use the Studio-Shelf application to detect the mesh network. Each brass joint acts as a high-fidelity conductor for both power and data.

Our hand-finished Oak and Walnut surfaces should be treated with the included Studio-Oil once every six months. For metallic components, a dry micro-fiber cloth is recommended to maintain the soft champagne luster without micro-abrasions.

Each vertical shelf unit is engineered to support up to 45kg when mounted to structural masonry. For drywall installations, please utilize our Technical Specs section for specific anchor requirements based on your configuration height.
