How is the 'Carry' calculated on a deal-by-deal basis?

Carried interest is calculated as a percentage of profits (typically 20%) after the Limited Partners have received their initial capital contribution plus a preferred return (hurdle rate), usually set at 8% IRR.

Triggering 409A valuation recalculations?

A new 409A valuation is required every 12 months or upon a "material event," such as a new financing round, a significant change in business model, or an acquisition proposal. Failure to comply can result in severe tax penalties for option holders.

Standard 1x Non-Participating Liquidation Preference?

This is the industry standard for early-stage deals. In an exit, investors choose between:
  • Receiving their initial investment back (1x) plus any declared dividends.
  • Converting their preferred shares to common and taking their pro-rata share of the proceeds.
