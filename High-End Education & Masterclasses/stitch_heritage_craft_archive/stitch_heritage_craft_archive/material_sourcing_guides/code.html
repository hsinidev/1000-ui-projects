Vegetable-Tanned Vachetta

Sourced from the heart of Tuscany, this leather is tanned using traditional mimosa and chestnut extracts. It possesses a firm temperament and a smooth, unrefined grain that develops a deep, honeyed patina over decades of handling.

Heritage Specimen 02

Blued Carbon Steel

Heat-treated at 590°F to achieve a precise oxide layer. This material offers superior corrosion resistance with an iridescent depth that shifts from ink-black to vibrant cornflower blue depending on the angle of light.

TACTILE: COLD FINISH: SPECULAR
JAPAN
Heritage Specimen 03

Kuroki Selvedge Denim

Woven on vintage Toyoda shuttle looms in Okayama. This 14oz denim features a slow-dye immersion process, resulting in a textile that is incredibly rigid at first but breaks down into a personalized map of the wearer's movements.

TACTILE: ROUGH WEIGHT: 14OZ
ENGLAND
Heritage Specimen 04

Air-Dried English Walnut

Harvested from sustainably managed estates, this walnut is air-dried for seven years to stabilize the internal moisture. Known for its high-contrast grain and structural density, it is the preferred choice for heirloom furniture.

TACTILE: VELVET AGED: 7 YEARS
