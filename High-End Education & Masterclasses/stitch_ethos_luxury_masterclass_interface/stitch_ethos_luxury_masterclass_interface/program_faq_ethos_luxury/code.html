Enrollment

What are the criteria for selection? expand_more

Candidates are evaluated based on their professional trajectory, leadership potential within the luxury sector, and their ability to contribute to the cohort's collective intelligence. We typically require a minimum of five years in a management capacity.

Are there financial sponsorship options? expand_more

We partner with several global luxury conglomerates who provide full or partial tuition coverage for their high-potential directors. Private installment plans are also available upon request.

Curriculum

How is the program structured? expand_more

The masterclass is divided into twelve modules, spanning heritage management, digital transformation, and the anthropology of luxury consumption. Each module concludes with a live seminar and case study analysis.

Elite Access

Does the program include physical networking? expand_more

Yes. Enrollment grants you access to bi-annual 'Heritage Retreats' in Paris and Milan, where you will engage directly with creative directors and CEOs of established houses.

Certification

How is the certification recognized? expand_more

The Ethos-Luxury Certification is globally recognized as a benchmark for excellence in the luxury domain, endorsed by our council of industry veterans and academic partners.

Unresolved inquiries?

Our concierge team is available to discuss the program's specifics and assist with your application process.
