Core Network Protocols

Frequently asked questions regarding elite platform functionality.

How is the annual cohort selected? expand_more

The Global-Cohort utilizes a proprietary peer-nomination and algorithmically-verified vetting process. Potential members are evaluated on three core pillars: net-positive societal impact, strategic influence, and discretionary wealth transparency.

What are the Obsidian Tier privacy guarantees? expand_more

Obsidian members benefit from zero-knowledge proof identity verification. All internal communications are end-to-end encrypted with rotating keys generated on-device, ensuring that not even the Global-Cohort administrators can access private exchanges.

Accessing the Physical Vault network expand_more

Our network of physical safe-havens and lounge spaces can be accessed via the digital key located in your personal Vault. Use the biometric signature on your smartphone to unlock entry at any of our global concierge locations.

Nomination and Invitation Protocol expand_more

Each member is allotted two exclusive invitations per fiscal year. Nominations must be seconded by a member of the Board of Governors before the formal vetting process commences.

Need direct assistance?

Our Executive Concierge team is available 24/7 for Obsidian and Titanium members.
