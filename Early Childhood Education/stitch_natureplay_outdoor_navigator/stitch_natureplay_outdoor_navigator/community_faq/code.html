Common Inquiries

What should my child wear for forest school? expand_more
Layering is key! We recommend a moisture-wicking base, a thermal mid-layer, and a waterproof/windproof outer shell. Wool socks and durable boots are essential for keeping toes warm and dry.
How do you handle rainy days? expand_more
We embrace the rain! Our educators are trained to find shelter under canopies for focused tasks, and rain gear is strictly required for all students to ensure comfort while exploring puddles and mud.
Is there a ratio for educators to students? expand_more
Yes, NaturePlay maintains a strict 1:6 ratio for forest activities to ensure maximum safety and personalized guidance during outdoor exploration.
