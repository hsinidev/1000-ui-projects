What happens during a Lockdown?

expand_less

In the event of a lockdown, all external doors are electronically sealed. Parents will receive immediate SMS alerts via the PANIC protocol. Staff are trained to move children to designated 'Safe Zones' away from windows. Access is strictly controlled until law enforcement clears the site.
