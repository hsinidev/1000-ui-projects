How do you keep 5-year-olds engaged with Phonics sounds?

By SarahM_Parent

Hi everyone! My son is currently working through the 'Level 2: Vowel Blends' section, and he's starting to lose steam. He loved the initial letter games but finds the longer words a bit daunting.

Does anyone have fun physical activities or 'off-screen' tips that complement the LiteracyLevelUp lessons? I'd love to hear how you keep the energy high during study sessions!

Recent Comments (12)

DadOfThree_Tech 45 mins ago

We use 'Phonics Hopscotch'! I draw the vowel blends on the driveway with chalk. He has to jump on the sound as I say the word. It really helps get the energy out while learning.

TeacherMom_Lara 1 hour ago

Try the 'Magic Wand' approach. Give him a stick or a literal toy wand and let him 'cast a spell' on the words as he reads them. Making it a game of power over the daunting words helps them overcome the fear!
