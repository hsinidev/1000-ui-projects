Getting Started

How do I invite my family members? expand_more
Navigate to your Profile Settings and select 'Manage Family'. From there, you can send email invites or generate a unique QR code for your family members to scan and join your private circle.
Is GlobalSeed free for educators? expand_more
Yes! We offer a dedicated 'Roots' program for verified classroom teachers which provides free access to our primary educational resources and cultural exchange tools.

Language Exchange

How are language partners matched? expand_more
Our algorithm matches families based on shared interests (like cooking or music), time zone compatibility, and age-appropriate levels for children. We prioritize consistent, long-term connections over one-off chats.
What if we miss a scheduled call? expand_more
Life happens! You can use the built-in calendar to reschedule. We encourage families to send a quick 'Village Note' through the app to let their partners know as soon as possible.

Family Stories

Who can see our uploaded family stories? expand_more
Privacy is our top priority. By default, stories are only visible to your immediate family group. You must explicitly toggle 'Share with Partner' for specific stories you want your exchange family to see.

Community is at our core.

GlobalSeed is more than just an app; it's a growing network of parents and educators. If you can't find what you need in our FAQ, our community ambassadors are standing by to guide you.
