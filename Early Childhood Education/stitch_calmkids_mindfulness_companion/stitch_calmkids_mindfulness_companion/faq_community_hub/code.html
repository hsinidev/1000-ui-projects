Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a meditation session for my child?

expand_more
Simply navigate to the "Breathing" tab in the top navigation. You'll find a variety of guided exercises specifically designed with soft animations to help children focus and find their calm.

Can I track my child's emotional progress?

expand_more
Yes! The "Feeling Wheel" allows your child to log their daily mood. Over time, you can view these trends in the Account dashboard to see how their mindfulness practice is impacting their emotional well-being.

Are the stories curated by professionals?

expand_more
Every story on ZenPal is written in collaboration with child psychologists and SEL (Social-Emotional Learning) experts to ensure they are supportive, age-appropriate, and emotionally grounding.
