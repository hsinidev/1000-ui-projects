Frequently Asked Questions

How do I record a high-quality video response? expand_more

To ensure the best experience for recruiters, we recommend finding a quiet environment with natural lighting. TalentStream works best when you are positioned centrally in the frame.

  • Use a wired internet connection if possible.
  • Check your microphone levels in the "Preview" screen.
  • Keep eye contact with the camera, not the screen.
Can I share evaluation reports with external partners? expand_more
Yes, managers can generate "Guest Links" that provide view-only access to specific candidate video profiles and ratings for a set period. This ensures security while facilitating external stakeholder input.
What browser permissions are required for recording? expand_more
You must grant access to both your Camera and Microphone. If you are experiencing issues, please check your browser's site settings (usually found by clicking the lock icon in the address bar) and ensure TalentStream is permitted to use these devices.
How are collaborative ratings calculated? expand_more
We use a weighted average based on team roles. Primary hiring managers' ratings carry slightly more weight in the "Global Score," though all feedback is visible in the avatar stack on the candidate card.
