Frequently Asked Questions

01.

How does the AI handle non-English legal terminology?

Our engine utilizes a multi-lingual transformer model trained on millions of legal documents across 42 jurisdictions, ensuring nuanced understanding of localized terminology and civil law nuances.

expand_more
02.

Is the biometric signature recognized under the ESIGN Act?

Yes. LegalCloud biometric signatures meet and exceed ESIGN and eIDAS requirements by providing a comprehensive audit trail, intent verification, and cryptographically linked identity markers.

03.

Can we customize the risk weighting for specific contract types?

Enterprise administrators can configure 'Risk Profiles' that adjust the sensitivity of the AI detector based on specific business units, contract values, or risk appetite thresholds.
