How often should I calibrate the airflow sensors?

expand_more
For optimal clinical serenity, we recommend a bi-weekly calibration check. The Feline-Flow automated system handles minor adjustments, but a manual sensor wipe with a dry micro-fiber cloth ensures 99.9% particulate filtration accuracy.

What do the lavender status pulses indicate?

expand_more
The rhythmic lavender pulse indicates the habitat is in 'Flow State', meaning air quality, temperature, and humidity are all within the medical-grade comfort zone for your cat.

Connecting the biometric collars to the hub?

expand_more
Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your central Feline-Flow terminal. Once the collar is within 3 meters, the pulse indicator will turn solid lavender to confirm a successful health-data sync.
