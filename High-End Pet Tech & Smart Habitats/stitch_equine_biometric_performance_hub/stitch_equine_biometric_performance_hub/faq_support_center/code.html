Sensor Calibration

How often should I re-calibrate sensors? expand_more
Calibration is recommended every 14 days or after any major environmental change (e.g., transitioning from turf to sand).
Dealing with magnetic interference expand_more
Ensure the stable area is free of high-voltage wiring during the sync process. If errors persist, reset using the "Hard Cycle" button.
analytics

Gait Analysis

Understanding symmetry scores expand_more
A symmetry score above 90% is optimal. Scores below 85% trigger an automatic "Vet Alert" and flag the session for veterinarian review.
Identifying early lameness markers expand_more
Look for consistent deviations in "Propulsion Force" over three consecutive training sessions rather than a single data point.

Account Security

Multi-user Access Levels expand_more
Owners can restrict "Write Access" for temporary grooms or visiting trainers while maintaining "Read Only" data transparency.
Biometric Lock & Data Privacy expand_more
All horse health records are encrypted using AES-256 standards. Only authorized medical ID holders can view full medical histories.
