HOW OFTEN SHOULD I INSPECT TENSION-LOADED HARNESSES?

Daily inspections are mandatory for all kinetic-grade equipment. Focus on the high-stress stress points and load-bearing buckles for signs of micro-fractures in the polymer or fraying in the nylon webbing. Replace immediately if any structural integrity is compromised.

#RECOVERY-04 open_in_new
POST-TRAINING THERMAL REGULATION PROTOCOLS?

Active cooling should begin within 5 minutes of training cessation. Use hydro-immersion or evaporative cooling vests. Monitor core temperature every 15 minutes until baseline is achieved. Failure to regulate can lead to prolonged recovery times.

WHAT ARE THE HOURS FOR THE HIGH-INTENSITY ZONE?

The H.I. Zone is open 24/7 for Elite Members. Standard members have access from 05:00 to 22:00 daily. All sessions must be logged via the K9 KINETIC terminal for safety and equipment tracking purposes.

#NUTRITION-02 open_in_new
OPTIMAL PROTEIN RATIOS FOR WORKING BELGIANS?

Working lines require a minimum of 30% protein and 20% fat dry matter basis. During peak training seasons, caloric density should increase by 15-20% through high-bioavailability animal fats and amino acid supplementation.
