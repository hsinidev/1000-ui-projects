Commonly Asked Questions

Quick solutions for frequent husbandry challenges.

Most T5 High Output bulbs maintain effective UV output for 12 months. However, we recommend testing with a Solarmeter every 3 months. If the UV output drops below 70% of its initial reading at the primary basking site, it's time to replace the bulb. Note that the visible light may still function long after the UV gases have depleted.
