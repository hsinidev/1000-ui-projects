Logistics & Handling

How do you manage international white-glove transport? expand_more
Our logistics partners specialize in climate-controlled, secure transit for high-value assets. Every shipment includes a dedicated courier, real-time GPS monitoring, and end-to-end insurance coverage tailored to the specific medium of your artwork.
What is the standard lead time for private viewing delivery? expand_more
Viewings within major art hubs (London, New York, Hong Kong) typically require 48-72 hours for preparation and transport. Global transfers are coordinated with specific customs brokerage teams to ensure minimal exposure.
security

Security & Privacy

How is my collection data anonymized in the AI network? expand_more
We use zero-knowledge proofs to verify provenance without revealing ownership details. Your collection remains invisible to other users unless you explicitly grant access to our community features.
What physical vault standards do you require for insurance? expand_more
All partner facilities must be UL-rated with 24/7 biometric access control and independent fire suppression systems. We provide a full audit checklist upon your first acquisition.
