ERA DETAILS: THE ESTE REGENCY (1469-1505)

person PRIMARY OWNER
Duke Borso d'Este

Duke of Ferrara, Modena and Reggio. A renowned patron of the arts who commissioned the Great Bible and this specific Missal for private liturgy.

location_on LOCATION
Palazzo dei Diamanti

Ferrara, Italy. The manuscript was housed in the Duke's private study, accessible only to his immediate family and personal chaplains.

history ARCHIVAL NOTES
Commission Records

Payments recorded in the Este treasury ledgers between August 1469 and March 1471. Scribe identified as Pietro Paolo Marone.
