Wallet Security

How is my private key handled? expand_more

AETHER VOID is non-custodial. Your private keys never touch our servers. Integration is handled entirely through EIP-1193 compatible providers.

Recovering lost assets? expand_more

Since assets are decentralized on the Ethereum L2, recovery requires your seed phrase. We cannot reset passwords for on-chain identities.
