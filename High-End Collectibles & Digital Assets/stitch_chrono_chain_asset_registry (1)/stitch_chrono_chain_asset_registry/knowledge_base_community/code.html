Registry Knowledge Base

Technical documentation regarding the immutable verification protocols and the cryptographic foundations of digital watch twins.

Digital twins are minted using a dual-phase cryptographic process. First, high-resolution physical scans of the movement are hashed into a unique identifier. This is followed by an NFC-linked on-chain transaction that anchors the metadata to the specific caliber serial number.
