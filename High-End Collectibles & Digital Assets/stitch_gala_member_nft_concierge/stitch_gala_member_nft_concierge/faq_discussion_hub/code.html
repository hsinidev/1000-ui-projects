What are the specific requirements for the 'Sovereign' Tier? expand_more

Access to the Sovereign Tier requires a verified holding of the 'Apex Gala NFT' and a minimum governance participation score of 85. Members at this level receive 24/7 dedicated concierge service and priority boarding for all international summit transfers.

Can I upgrade my tier mid-cycle? expand_more

Yes, tier upgrades are processed instantly upon verification of new assets or contributions. The system will pro-rate your remaining benefits and grant immediate access to newly unlocked lounges and events.
