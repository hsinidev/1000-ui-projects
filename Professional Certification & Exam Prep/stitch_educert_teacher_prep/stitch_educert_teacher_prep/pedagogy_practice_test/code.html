A middle school teacher notices that student participation significantly drops during teacher-led lectures. According to active learning theories, which strategy would be most effective for increasing student engagement and retention?

Excellent choice! Think-Pair-Share is a high-leverage engagement strategy that forces cognitive processing. It allows students to validate their ideas in a low-stakes environment (the pair) before speaking to the larger group.
