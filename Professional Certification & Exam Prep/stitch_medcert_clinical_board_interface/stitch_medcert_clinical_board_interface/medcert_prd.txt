# MedCert: Medical Boards & USMLE Prep - Product Requirements

## Overview
A professional medical board exam preparation platform (USMLE) designed for high-stakes learning.

## Aesthetic
- **Style:** Clinical & Precise
- **Colors:** Sterile White, Deep Teal, Silver
- **Tone:** Authoritative, high-trust, focused

## Required Screens
1. **Anatomy Atlas (Interactive):** Visual exploration of anatomical structures.
2. **Q-Bank (Question Bank):** The core testing interface for practice exams.
3. **Performance Analytics:** Detailed breakdown of performance by specialty using high-contrast charts.
4. **Study Group Forums:** Collaborative space for medical students.

## UX Specifications
- **Data Visualization:** High-contrast charts for performance tracking.
- **Interactive Elements:** 'Symptom-to-Diagnosis' logic tree.
- **Mobile Optimization:**
  - 'Quick-Review' micro-lessons.
  - Push-notification layout for daily high-yield facts.
  - Interactive medical illustrations.
