How is the predictive score calculated? expand_more
Our AI engine utilizes a Bayesian inference model trained on over 20 years of historical bar exam results. It analyzes your performance across substantive legal subjects and identifies patterns in your reasoning to provide a statistically significant prediction of your actual exam score.
Can I share my study groups across institutions? expand_more
Yes. JurisMaster is designed for universal legal collaboration. You can invite colleagues from any recognized ABA law school to join your study deck or case briefing sessions.
Are the citations automatically formatted? expand_more
All citations generated within the platform adhere strictly to the latest edition of The Bluebook. Users can toggle between standard academic citations and practitioner-focused formats for court filings.
