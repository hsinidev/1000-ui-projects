Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI evaluate my legal analysis?

expand_more
Our proprietary engine analyzes your input for the IRAC (Issue, Rule, Analysis, Conclusion) framework. It evaluates the logical nexus between the stated Rule and your factual Analysis, comparing it against a database of over 50,000 peer-reviewed model answers and judicial opinions.

Is JurisMaster content updated for recent SCOTUS rulings?

expand_more
Yes. Our academic team updates the legal database within 72 hours of major appellate decisions. All practice modules and flashcard decks are version-controlled to ensure you are studying the current law.

Can I share my subscription with a study group?

expand_more
Individual subscriptions are licensed for single-user access to preserve the integrity of progress tracking and AI personalization. However, we offer Academic Group Rates for cohorts of 5 or more students. Contact our billing department for a custom quote.

What happens if I lose internet during a timed exam?

expand_more
JurisMaster features a local-cache recovery system. If your connection drops, your progress is saved locally in your browser. Once reconnected, the application will automatically synchronize your timestamped responses with our central servers.

Still need assistance, Counselor?

Our human support team consists of former law clerks and legal educators available Monday through Friday for specialized inquiries.
