How do I track my continuing education credits?

expand_more
You can track your credits directly through the "Modules" tab. Each completed module automatically updates your credit ledger, which is synchronized with the state licensing board every 24 hours. You can also export a PDF summary from the Dashboard at any time.

What happens if I fail a practice exam?

expand_more
Practice exams can be retaken an unlimited number of times. After each attempt, you'll receive a detailed breakdown of your performance by topic area. We recommend reviewing the specific modules associated with your lowest-scoring sections before re-attempting.

Can I use these credits in multiple states?

expand_more
Many of our modules are ARELLO certified and accepted across multiple jurisdictions. Please check the "Reciprocity" section in the course details for a full list of states where the specific module is approved for credit.

How long is my certification valid?

expand_more
Most real estate licenses require renewal every two years. RealEstateCert certificates are dated upon completion and remain in our secure archive for five years to satisfy any audit requirements from regulatory bodies.
