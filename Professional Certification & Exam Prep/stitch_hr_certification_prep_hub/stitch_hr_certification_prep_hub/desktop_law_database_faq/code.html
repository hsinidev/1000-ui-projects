Common Legal Questions (FAQ)

Most Searched

How do I determine if a role is Exempt or Non-Exempt?

expand_more
Determining exemption requires applying both the salary basis test and the duties test under FLSA. Most professional roles earning over $35,568 annually may qualify if they exercise independent judgment.

What constitutes a 'Hostile Work Environment' under Title VII?

expand_more
A hostile work environment is created when unwelcome conduct based on protected characteristics is so pervasive it creates an intimidating or offensive atmosphere.

Are verbal agreements legally binding in employment?

expand_more
In many jurisdictions, verbal contracts can be binding, though they are notoriously difficult to prove. Written offer letters and employment agreements are always the standard recommendation.
