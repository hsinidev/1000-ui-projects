# HR-Leader: Certification Prep (SHRM & PHR)
## Product Requirements Document

**Core Purpose:** High-fidelity preparation for HR certification exams with a focus on compliance and strategic people operations.

**Required Screens:**
1. **Flashcard Hub:** Interactive study tool for key HR concepts and terminology.
2. **Employment Law Database:** Searchable, categorized repository of federal and state labor laws.
3. **Employee Relations Scenarios:** Case-study based learning modules with interactive decision paths.
4. **Strategic Planning Modules:** High-level HR business strategy and workforce planning tools.

**UX Specifications:**
- **Compliance Risk Quiz:** Interactive component to test knowledge on legal pitfalls.
- **Daily HR Law Updates:** Priority feed for mobile users.
- **People Ops Glossary:** Simplified, quick-access dictionary.
- **Aesthetic:** Human-centric, professional, using Soft Purple, Navy, and White.
- **Imagery:** Warm, human-focused office photography.
