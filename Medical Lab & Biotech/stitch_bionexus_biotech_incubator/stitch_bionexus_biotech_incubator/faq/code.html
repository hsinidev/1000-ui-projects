Funding & Equity

What is the standard equity stake for Seed stage entry? expand_more
BioNexus typically assumes a 5-10% equity position depending on the technological readiness level (TRL) and intellectual property valuation at the time of incubation. This includes full access to our Tier-4 wet lab facilities and mentor network.
Are there follow-on funding guarantees? expand_more
While there are no contractual guarantees, BioNexus Ventures maintains a $500M reserve for Pro-Rata participation in Series A rounds for top-performing incubator graduates.
How is IP ownership handled? expand_more
All IP generated prior to entry remains 100% yours. IP generated using BioNexus equipment is subject to a standard first-right-of-refusal for licensing by our corporate partners, though ownership remains with the startup.

Lab Access

Guests are limited to the non-restricted executive lounge. Lab access for non-members is strictly prohibited for insurance reasons.
