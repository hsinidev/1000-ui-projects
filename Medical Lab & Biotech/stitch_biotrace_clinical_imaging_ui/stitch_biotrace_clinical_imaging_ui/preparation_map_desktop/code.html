Dietary & Metabolic Control

FASTING REQUIREMENTS

No solid food consumption for 6 hours prior to scanning. Clear liquids (water only) are permitted to maintain hydration levels.

warning NO CAFFEINE OR SUGAR

HYDRATION CALIBRATION

Consume 500ml of water 60 minutes before arrival. This optimizes contrast agent distribution and renal clearance post-procedure.
