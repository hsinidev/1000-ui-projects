How should I prepare for a Contrast-Enhanced MRI?

expand_more

Contrast-enhanced MRI requires specific protocols to ensure both image clarity and patient safety. Most procedures require fasting for at least 4 hours prior to the scan to minimize potential nausea from the gadolinium contrast agent.

  • Drink plenty of water 24 hours prior to improve kidney function.
  • Wear loose, comfortable clothing without metallic fasteners.
  • Inform your radiologist if you have any history of kidney disease.
