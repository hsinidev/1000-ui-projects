Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my overnight sleep study?
expand_more
Please bring comfortable sleeping attire (two-piece pajamas are preferred), any evening medications you normally take, and personal hygiene items. We provide clinical-grade bedding, but you are welcome to bring your own pillow if it helps you achieve a natural sleep state. Avoid caffeine or alcohol 12 hours prior to arrival.
