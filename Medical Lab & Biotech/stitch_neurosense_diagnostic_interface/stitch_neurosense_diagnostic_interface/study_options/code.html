Home Sleep Apnea Test (HSAT)

Screening focused on Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA) detection in comfortable environments.

check_circle Pros

  • ▶ Higher comfort and familiar sleep environment
  • ▶ Significantly lower cost and higher accessibility
  • ▶ Minimal sensor profile (4-7 data channels)

cancel Cons

  • ▶ Risk of data loss due to sensor displacement
  • ▶ Cannot diagnose complex comorbidities (e.g., Narcolepsy)
  • ▶ No EEG data; cannot measure exact sleep stages

In-Lab Polysomnography (PSG)

Comprehensive clinical evaluation with real-time technician monitoring and multi-channel analysis.

check_circle Pros

  • ▶ 16+ channels of data (EEG, EOG, EMG, ECG)
  • ▶ Real-time intervention (e.g., CPAP Titration)
  • ▶ Accurate staging and comprehensive diagnosis

cancel Cons

  • ▶ "First Night Effect" can lead to unnatural sleep
  • ▶ Higher scheduling wait times and higher cost
  • ▶ Extensive sensor array may be uncomfortable

psychology Full EEG Cap
visibility EOG Electrodes
