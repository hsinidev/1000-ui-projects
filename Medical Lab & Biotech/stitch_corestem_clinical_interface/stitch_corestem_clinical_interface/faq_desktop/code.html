Admissions

Your journey from initial consultation to recovery and post-care.

What makes CoreStem's approach unique in regenerative medicine?

expand_more
Our protocol utilizes proprietary isolation technology that ensures 98% viability of harvested mesenchymal cells. Unlike traditional clinics, we combine this with precision biological scaffolding to guide cellular regeneration directly to the site of injury, mirroring the body's natural healing environment.

Are there any side effects or risks associated with the therapy?

expand_more
Because we utilize autologous (your own) cells, the risk of rejection is non-existent. Most patients experience minor localized swelling or tenderness for 24-48 hours. Every procedure is monitored by a board-certified anesthesiologist to ensure maximum comfort and safety throughout the process.

How long does the admission process typically take?

expand_more
The journey begins with a comprehensive 90-minute digital consultation. Once candidacy is confirmed, patients are usually scheduled within 14-21 business days. The actual admission for the procedure is typically a day-stay event, allowing you to return to a controlled home environment by evening.

Is stem cell therapy covered by standard insurance protocols?

expand_more
Currently, most regenerative therapies are considered elective and are not fully covered by traditional insurance providers. However, CoreStem offers structured medical financing through specialized health partners to ensure our science remains accessible to those who need it most.
