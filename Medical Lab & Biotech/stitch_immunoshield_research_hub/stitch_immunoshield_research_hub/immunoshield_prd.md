# ImmunoShield Design Specifications

## Aesthetic: Biomorphic & Scientific
- **Shapes:** Use organic, rounded shapes that mimic cell structures (lymphocytes, antibodies). Avoid sharp corners.
- **Palette:** Soft Teal (Base), White (Surfaces), Blood-Red (Accents for alerts/critical data).
- **Glassmorphism:** Use subtle transparency and blur to represent biological membranes.

## Screens to Generate:
1. **Allergy Testing:** List-based view of diagnostic tests. High scannability.
2. **Autoimmune Profiles:** Data visualization of patient immune markers.
3. **Research Publications:** Abstract-heavy feed with search and filter capabilities.
4. **Clinical Trials:** Detailed view for trial enrollment and information.

## UX Feature:
- **'Immune Response' Infographic:** An interactive, biomorphic visual explaining how the immune system reacts to pathogens.
