What are the specific ICH Q3D guidelines for elemental impurities?

expand_more

The ICH Q3D Guideline for Elemental Impurities applies to drug products and requires a risk-based approach to control impurities. Our lab utilizes ICP-MS/ICP-OES methodologies to ensure compliance with permissible daily exposure (PDE) limits across all 24 identified elements.
