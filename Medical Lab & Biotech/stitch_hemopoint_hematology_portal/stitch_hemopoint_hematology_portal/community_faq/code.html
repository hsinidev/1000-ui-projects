General Clinical Labs
Monitoring frequency varies by specific pathology and treatment phase. Typically, during active induction, counts are checked daily. In maintenance phases, bi-weekly or monthly intervals are common as per clinical guidelines.
Indicators include Mean Corpuscular Volume (MCV), Mean Corpuscular Hemoglobin (MCH), and visual analysis for anisocytosis, poikilocytosis, and specific inclusions like Howell-Jolly bodies.
Samples suspected of cold agglutinins must be kept at 37°C during transport and processed quickly to prevent spurious results in MCV and RBC counts.
