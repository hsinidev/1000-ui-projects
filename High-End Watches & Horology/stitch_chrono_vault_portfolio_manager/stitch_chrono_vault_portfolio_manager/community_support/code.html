Protocol & Procedure

Knowledge Center

Your biometric data is never stored on our servers. It is encrypted at the hardware level using the Secure Enclave of your device, providing a zero-knowledge architecture for all vault access attempts.

Every timepiece is issued a non-fungible Digital Identity Token (DIT). This includes a high-resolution microscopic fingerprint of the movement, service history, and a chain of ownership that is immutable and publicly verifiable yet pseudonymized for privacy.

Chronos Vault partners with Tier 1 underwriters to provide immediate, app-based coverage. Rates are dynamically adjusted based on real-time market value and your vault's physical security rating.

For market transactions, funds are held in a smart-contract escrow. Physical transfer is handled via armored transit with end-to-end GPS tracking visible directly in your Chronos Dashboard.
