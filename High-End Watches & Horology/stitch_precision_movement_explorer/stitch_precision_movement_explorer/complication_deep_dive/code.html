The Tourbillon

A gravity-defying masterstroke of horological engineering. The CX-Tourbillon rotates the entire escapement within a lightweight titanium cage every sixty seconds.

Perpetual
Calendar Logic

Beyond the whirlwind of the tourbillon lies the Perpetual Calendar—a mechanical computer of extraordinary complexity. It tracks the day, date, month, and leap years without manual intervention until the year 2100.

The logic is governed by a 48-month program wheel, featuring varying notch depths that tell the mechanism how many days are in each month. It is a symphony of brass and steel calculating the passage of time across generations.
