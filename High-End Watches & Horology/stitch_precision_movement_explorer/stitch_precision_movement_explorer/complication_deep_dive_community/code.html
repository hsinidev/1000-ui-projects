PHASE 01 // GRAVITY OFFSET

By rotating the entire escapement assembly (the balance wheel, pallet lever, and escape wheel) inside a carriage, any timing errors caused by gravity are averaged out over a full 360-degree cycle.

PHASE 02 // FRICTION REDUCTION

The flying bridge architecture reduces the vertical mass profile, allowing for a thinner movement profile while maintaining the structural integrity required for 4Hz oscillation.
