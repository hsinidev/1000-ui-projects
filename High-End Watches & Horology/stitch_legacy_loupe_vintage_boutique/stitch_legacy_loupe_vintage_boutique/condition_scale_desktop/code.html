The Temporal Condition Scale

Provenance is more than just paper; it is etched into the metal and dial of every horological artifact. Our proprietary grading system provides a definitive framework for assessing the preservation of mechanical history.

GRADE 01

New Old Stock (NOS)

PRIMARY STATE: UNTOUCHED

Reserved for artifacts that have remained in a vault-like state since production. Zero signs of human interaction beyond assembly.

  • check Original factory finish
  • check Zero lume degradation
  • check Unstretched movement architecture
GRADE 02

Exceptional Preservation

PRIMARY STATE: GENTLY CURATED

Worn sparingly with reverence. The case remains unpolished, retaining its original geometry and proportions.

  • check Sharp lugs and original bevels
  • check Minor patina on tritium/radium
  • check Full movement service history preferred
GRADE 03

Honest Provenance

PRIMARY STATE: CHARACTERFUL

A timepiece that has lived. Visible scuffs and potentially light historical polishing, but no loss of core identity.

  • check Light bezel ghosting or fading
  • check Some loss of sharpness in lug edges
  • check Stable, attractive dial aging
GRADE 04

Historical Artifact

PRIMARY STATE: WEATHERED

Significant signs of life. May include 'Tropical' dial transformations or heavy case rounding. Valued for its unique story.

  • check Evident case polishing
  • check Significant dial discoloration (Tropical)
  • check Replacement parts (Period correct)
