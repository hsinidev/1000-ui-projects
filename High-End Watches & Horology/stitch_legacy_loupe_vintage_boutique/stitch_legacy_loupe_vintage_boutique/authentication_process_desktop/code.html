ARCHIVAL METHODOLOGY

THE FOUR PILLARS OF CERTAINTY

01

Physical Inspection

Macroscopic analysis of the case geometry, dial patina, and surface textures under controlled spectrum lighting to detect modern intervention.

02

Movement Calibration

Internal verification of caliber authenticity, timing variance analysis, and microscopic scrutiny of individual component finishing.

03

Provenance Verification

Cross-referencing of archival papers, original sales records, and ownership history against our proprietary global timepiece database.

04

Digital Certification

Generation of an immutable digital asset anchored to the physical object, providing a permanent, transferable record of legitimacy.
