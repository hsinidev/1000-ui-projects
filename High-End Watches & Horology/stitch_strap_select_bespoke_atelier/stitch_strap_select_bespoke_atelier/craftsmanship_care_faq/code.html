Leather Care Rituals

How do I maintain Alligator leather? expand_more
Alligator leather requires a specialized lanolin-based conditioner. Avoid excessive moisture and direct sunlight. To maintain the characteristic high-gloss or matte finish, we recommend a professional "bespoke buffing" every six months with our Atelier Care Kit.
Is the Ostrich leather water-resistant? expand_more
While Ostrich skin contains natural oils that provide some resistance, it is a porous organic material. If exposed to water, gently pat dry with a soft lint-free cloth. Do not apply heat, as this can cause the follicles to crack.

The Precision Sizing

Measuring your wrist for Bespoke fit expand_more
Use a flexible measuring tape directly above the wrist bone. For a "Tailored" fit, add 5mm to the circumference. For "Classic" comfort, add 10mm. Our straps are hand-cut to ensure zero-slack integration with your timepiece.
Lug width compatibility expand_more
We offer widths from 16mm to 24mm in 1mm increments. Ensure you measure the internal distance between the lugs of your watch case using a digital caliper for absolute precision.
