history_edu Historical Context

The Richter lineage traces back to the royal stables of Bavaria, noted for their exceptional temperament and structural integrity. Baron von Richter III represents the culmination of fifty years of selective preservation of the 'Richter Prime' trait—a specific bone density and cognitive agility observed in the 1974 Foundation Sire.
