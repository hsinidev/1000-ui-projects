PEDIGREE VERIFICATION

What constitutes "Verified Heritage" within the Registry?

expand_more

Verified Heritage is the gold standard of the Imperial Archives. It requires a documented 5-generation lineage where every sire and dam has been registered with the Registry Official Seal. Any entry lacking physical parchment evidence or digital biometric confirmation is marked as "Under Review" until secondary authentication is provided.

How are genetic clearances integrated into historical records?

expand_more

Since the Protocol of 2008, all registry entries must include a verified genetic profile. These are stored as "Wax Seal" digital tokens. Our laboratory partners transmit raw data directly to the Archive to prevent tampering, ensuring that every lineage maintains its functional and health integrity.

MEMBERSHIP & ACCESS

How can one apply for a "Seal Authenticity" designation?

expand_more

The Seal Authenticity is reserved for established breeders with a 10-year clean record. Applications are reviewed quarterly by the Imperial Council. Applicants must submit a physical ledger of their breeding program and undergo an on-site audit of their archival preservation methods.
