Stable Protocols

Authoritative procedures for daily stable management and data integrity.

verified How do I synchronize biometric sensors with the central unit?

expand_more

To ensure maximum precision, sensors must be calibrated while the horse is at rest. Secure the hock-mounted sensors using the EquiSync leather straps, then initiate the 'Sync Sequence' via your mobile console. A green ledger icon will appear on the 'Stable Dashboard' once high-fidelity communication is established. For multi-horse synchronization, we recommend a sequential pairing to avoid signal cross-contamination.

verified What is the protocol for emergency medical alerts?

expand_more

The system monitors resting heart rate and temperature 24/7. Should a vital sign exceed the preset 'Elite Safety Margin', a high-priority notification is dispatched to all assigned managers.

verified Can I export performance data for veterinary review?

expand_more

Yes. All performance metrics are available as encrypted PDF ledgers, formatted specifically for clinical review.
