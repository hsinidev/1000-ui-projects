Session Steps

Total Duration: 15 mins

1

Initial Positioning

Ensure the pet is lying comfortably on their non-affected side on a soft surface.

2

Hip Support

Gently cup the stifle joint with your palm while stabilizing the hip.

3

Controlled Extension

Slowly guide the leg back until natural resistance is met.

4

The 3-Second Hold

Maintain the position for three full breaths before releasing.

warning Safety Check

Stop immediately if you notice panting, dilated pupils, or vocalization. Consult the rehab team if resistance increases significantly.
