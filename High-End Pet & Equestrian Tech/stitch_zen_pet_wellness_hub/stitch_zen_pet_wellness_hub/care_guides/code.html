Home Care Guide

Consistency is the bridge between injury and recovery. These guides are designed to be performed in a quiet, distraction-free environment.

lightbulb

Expert Tip

Always reward your pet with gentle praise or a low-calorie treat after each successful stretch to build positive association.

1

Preparation & Warmth

Find a non-slip surface, like a yoga mat or rug. Ensure the room is comfortably warm to help muscles relax. Spend 2 minutes gently stroking your pet's back to signal the start of therapy.

2

Passive Movement

Slowly guide the affected limb through its natural range of motion. Do not force any movement. If you feel resistance, stop and hold for 10 seconds before gently releasing.

3

Rest & Observation

Allow your pet to rest for 15 minutes post-exercise. Monitor their walking gait and note any signs of discomfort or increased fluidity in the app tracker.
