01 Safety Protocols

Pre-flight Veterinary Assessment expand_more
Every Aero-Pet journey begins with a specialized aviation health assessment. Our network of partner veterinarians ensures your companion is fit for high-altitude travel, reviewing respiratory health and stress-management indicators 48 hours before departure.
In-Cabin Pressurization Monitoring expand_more
Our fleet is equipped with advanced climate and pressure sensors calibrated specifically for pet physiology. Real-time data is monitored by our ground-based medical team throughout the duration of the flight.

02 Documentation

International Health Certificates expand_more
We handle the complex filing of USDA APHIS Form 7001 and specific country-level health declarations. Our concierge team manages the digital "Pet Passport" ensuring all vaccinations are up-to-date for your destination.
Liability & Insurance Coverage expand_more
Aero-Pet provides industry-leading transit insurance. This bespoke coverage includes specialized veterinary care upon arrival and comprehensive travel protection for your companion.
