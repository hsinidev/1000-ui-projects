Scoring & Ranking FAQ

01 / YPS CALCULATION

How is Yards Per Second calculated?

YPS is determined by the total course distance (as measured by the judge) divided by the handler's recorded time. This provides a pure metric of speed regardless of course complexity.

02 / FAULT IMPACT

How do faults affect my global rank?

Clean runs (zero faults) are prioritized in all ranking tiers. A slower clean run will often rank higher than a faster run with a 5-point fault, emphasizing precision alongside speed.

03 / LEVEL ADVANCEMENT

When do I move to Masters?

Advancement requires 3 'Qualifying' (Q) scores under three different judges. Once confirmed, your profile automatically transitions to the Masters leaderboard circuit.
