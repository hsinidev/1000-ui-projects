How do I resolve 'Hemolysis Interference' flags?

expand_more
Hemolysis flags are triggered when the optical density exceeds the baseline calibration of the HIL (Hemolysis, Icterus, Lipemia) sensor. Re-centrifuge the specimen at 2000g for 10 minutes and resubmit the serum sample.
