Spirometry Basics

How do I calibrate my lung health monitor? expand_more
Calibration should be performed every 24 hours or before your first daily session. Ensure the device is at room temperature for at least 30 minutes. In the app, navigate to 'Device Settings' and follow the 'Manual Sync' prompt while keeping the device on a flat surface.
Interpreting your FEV1/FVC results expand_more
Your FEV1/FVC ratio indicates the proportion of your lung capacity you can exhale in one second. A healthy ratio is typically above 70%. If your dashboard shows a Sky Blue indicator with a 'Pulse Motif' dip, consult your clinical dashboard notes or contact your provider.

Hyperbaric Protocols

Pre-session preparation checklist expand_more
Wear 100% cotton clothing, remove all electronic devices, and avoid carbonated beverages 2 hours prior to your session. Ensure your 'Lung Health Chip' status is 'Ready' on the home dashboard.
Managing ear pressure during sessions expand_more
Use the Valsalva maneuver or gently swallow as the 'Liquid Air Indicator' shows increasing pressure. If discomfort persists, alert the clinician via the portal's 'Active Session' comms button immediately.

App Troubleshooting

Data sync issues with clinical portal expand_more
Check your local Wi-Fi connection and ensure 'Background Refresh' is enabled for Breathe-Ease. If data is missing from the Air Quality Map, try refreshing your location permissions in the system settings.
