Heart-Guard Sensor Calibration v4.2

STABLE history Last Updated: Oct 24, 2023 person Dr. Elias Thorne

To ensure clinical-grade telemetry, Heart-Guard sensors must undergo a Zero-Point Calibration every 72 hours of active monitoring. Failure to calibrate may result in signal drift or inaccurate baseline reporting.

CRITICAL WARNING

Do not attempt calibration during active patient cardiac events. Calibration resets all active alarm thresholds to factory defaults.

Calibration Procedure

  1. Ensure the device is connected to the Vitalis Cloud via a secure 5G or Wi-Fi channel.
  2. Navigate to the Admin Panel > Hardware > Calibration.
  3. Place the sensor on the V-Test Plate or a neutral, non-conductive surface.
  4. Execute the init_zero_calibration() command.
