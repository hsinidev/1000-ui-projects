Pre-Scan Preparation

PROTOCOL_ID: DX-4402 // REQUIRED FOR CLINICAL ACCURACY

CORE REQUIREMENTS warning MANDATORY
fastfood

FASTING PROTOCOL

No food or caloric beverages for 4-6 hours prior to the scan. Water is permitted and encouraged for hydration consistency.

apparel

ATTIRE & METAL INTERFERENCE

Wear athletic clothing without metal components (zippers, underwires, buttons). Metal artifacts compromise density readings.

medication

SUPPLEMENTS & RADIOLOGY

Do not take calcium supplements 24h prior. Ensure no recent Barium or Contrast Dye tests within the last 7 days.

ESTIMATED ACCURACY

BASELINE 99.2% OPTIMAL

Adherence to all checklist items ensures laboratory-grade precision in body fat and bone density metrics.
