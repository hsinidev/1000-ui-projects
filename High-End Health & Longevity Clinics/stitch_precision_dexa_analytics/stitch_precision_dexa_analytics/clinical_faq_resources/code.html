PROTOCOL_ID: 04-A MANDATORY

To ensure clinical accuracy in visceral adipose tissue (VAT) measurements, subjects should fast for 4-6 hours prior to scanning. Consumption of fluids is permissible, but large volumes of water immediately before the procedure can affect lean mass readings due to hydration variance.

  • Avoid heavy exercise 12 hours prior.
  • Standardize scan time for serial measurements.
  • Wear metallic-free clothing (no zippers/underwire).
