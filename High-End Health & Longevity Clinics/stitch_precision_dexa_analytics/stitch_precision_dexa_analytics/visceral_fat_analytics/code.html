Visceral fat is metabolically active and secretes pro-inflammatory cytokines. Unlike subcutaneous fat, VAT is drained by the portal vein directly to the liver, contributing to insulin resistance and dyslipidemia. DEXA is the gold standard for differentiating between VAT and SAT (Subcutaneous Adipose Tissue).
