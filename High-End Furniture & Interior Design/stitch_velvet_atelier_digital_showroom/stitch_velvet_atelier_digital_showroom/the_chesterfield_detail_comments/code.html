Artisan's Note

The Chesterfield is more than a silhouette; it is a commitment to a dying craft. We utilize traditional techniques—eight-way hand-tied springs, solid beechwood frames, and individual studding—that modern manufacturing has long since abandoned. When you touch this velvet, you are feeling the culmination of three generations of upholstery expertise. We don't just build furniture; we create heirlooms that command a room.
