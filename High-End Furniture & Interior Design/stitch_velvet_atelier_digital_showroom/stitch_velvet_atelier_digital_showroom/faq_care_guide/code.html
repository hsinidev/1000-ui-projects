expand_more
How do I treat a spill on my velvet sofa?

Blot immediately with a clean, dry cloth. Do not rub, as this can damage the pile. Use a gentle, circular motion with a specialized velvet brush once dry to restore the sheen.

Can I steam my upholstered items?

Low-heat steaming is recommended for most of our signature weaves, but always maintain a 6-inch distance to prevent fiber distortion.
