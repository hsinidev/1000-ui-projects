Can LushLife walls be installed on any surface?

expand_more
Our modular systems are engineered to be lightweight yet structural. They can be mounted on drywall, masonry, or specialized steel frames. During the site assessment, our team evaluates load-bearing capacity and plumbing access to ensure a permanent, thriving installation.

How often does the irrigation system need checking?

expand_more
Our automated systems are IoT-enabled and self-monitoring. However, we recommend a physical diagnostic every 3 months. The Growth Progress Bar in your Client Portal will alert you if moisture levels or nutrient concentrations deviate from the optimal range.

What happens if a specific plant begins to wilt?

expand_more
Living walls are biological ecosystems. Occasional plant turnover is natural. Our "Swap & Bloom" service ensures that any underperforming botanical elements are replaced within 48 hours to maintain the aesthetic integrity and air-filtering performance of your wall.

How does the system contribute to LEED certification?

expand_more
LushLife walls contribute directly to Indoor Environmental Quality (IEQ) credits. By naturally regulating humidity and removing VOCs from the air, our vertical gardens help buildings achieve higher sustainability ratings while reducing HVAC energy consumption by up to 15%.
