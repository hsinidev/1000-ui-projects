The Steel Standard

Sourced from decommissioned structural plants, our steel undergoes a proprietary cold-rolling process that preserves the raw mill scale. Each piece maintains its individual grain, resulting in a finish that is tactile, non-reflective, and incredibly durable.

RECLAIMED OAK

Harvested from mid-century tobacco barns in the American South. Every plank tells a story of weather, work, and eighty years of natural curing.
