01 TECHNICAL SPECS

Structural Load Capacity expand_more

Our steel-frame dining surfaces are engineered to support up to 450kg (990lbs) of static load. Each joint is MIG-welded and reinforced with internal gussets for maximum rigidity.

  • • Material: Cold-rolled ASTM A36 Steel
  • • Weld Type: Full penetration MIG
  • • Finish: Matte Powder Coat or Hand-Burnished Oil
Modular Assembly Protocols expand_more
Pieces are designed for modular installation using M12 industrial-grade hex bolts. A complete structural toolkit is provided with every architecture shipment.

03 MATERIAL CARE

SURFACE TYPE

  • Patina Steel
  • Cast Concrete
  • Reclaimed Oak

Our Patina Steel pieces are designed to evolve with their environment. To maintain the structural finish while allowing natural aging:

Clean only with a dry microfibre cloth to remove dust. Avoid moisture and chemical solvents.

Apply industrial-grade beeswax once every 6 months to preserve the rust-resistant barrier.

Minor oxidation is expected in humid environments and contributes to the unique industrial character of the piece.
