forest Materials & Care

How should I maintain my solid oak pieces? expand_more
Our solid oak is finished with a breathable organic oil. For daily care, use a soft, slightly damp cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals or excessive heat, which may disrupt the wood's natural aging process. We recommend re-oiling every 12-18 months to maintain the Pale Oak luster.
Are your linens pre-shrunk and organic? expand_more
Yes, all our textiles are sourced from flax grown in the EU and are OEKOTEX® certified. We pre-wash our linens to ensure a soft, lived-in feel and to prevent further shrinkage when following our care instructions.

local_shipping Shipping & Logistics

Do you offer White Glove delivery? expand_more
For all furniture orders, we provide complimentary White Glove delivery. Our specialist team will place the item in your room of choice, assemble it, and remove all packaging materials for proper recycling.
International shipping timelines? expand_more
Most curated pieces ship within 4-6 weeks to international destinations. Bespoke orders require additional time for custom crating and climate-controlled transport to ensure material integrity.

brush Bespoke Services

How do I start a custom furniture commission? expand_more
Begin by booking a virtual consultation with our design studio. We will discuss your space requirements, material preferences, and structural needs. Commissions typically take 12-16 weeks from blueprint approval.
Interior Curation & Layout Planning expand_more
Our "Atelier Curation" service provides a comprehensive furniture and material plan for your home. We focus on the concept of 'Ma'—ensuring that every piece has a purpose and that the space between them is honored.
