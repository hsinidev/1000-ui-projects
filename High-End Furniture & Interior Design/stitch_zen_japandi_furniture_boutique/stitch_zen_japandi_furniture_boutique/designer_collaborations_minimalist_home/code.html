SOREN NIELSEN

For Nielsen, functionality is a form of poetry. His work for Minimalist Home centers on the honesty of materials—specifically cold-pressed ash and recycled linens. Each piece is a testament to the Scandinavian philosophy of light-filled living.

Tanaka’s approach to ceramics and textile design is an exercise in restraint. Her collaboration with Minimalist Home explores the concept of "Wabi-Sabi"—finding beauty in the imperfect and transient. Every piece is finished by hand in her Kyoto studio.
