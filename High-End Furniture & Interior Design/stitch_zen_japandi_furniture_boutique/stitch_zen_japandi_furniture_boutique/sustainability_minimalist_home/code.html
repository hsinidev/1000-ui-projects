Our philosophy is rooted in "Ma"—the celebration of space. We believe that what fills that space should be as pure as the space itself. Every material is chosen for its lifecycle, its tactile narrative, and its ability to age with grace.

Transparency in Craft

The Traceability Project

01 / ORIGIN

Each piece comes with a digital certificate tracing the timber back to its specific forest plot.

02 / TREATMENT

We use zero-VOC botanical oils and beeswax instead of synthetic lacquers for all wood finishes.

03 / END OF LIFE

Design for disassembly ensures every component can be separated and recycled or composted at its journey's end.
