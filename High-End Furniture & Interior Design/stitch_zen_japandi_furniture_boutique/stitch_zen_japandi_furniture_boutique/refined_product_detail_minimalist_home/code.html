THE PHILOSOPHY

Inspired by the Japanese concept of "Ma" — the celebration of empty space — the Shizuka Lounge Chair is a study in restrained elegance. Each piece is hand-carved from sustainably sourced Pale Oak, honoring the natural irregularities of the timber.

CRAFTSMANSHIP

Tenon-and-mortise joinery crafted by third-generation artisans in Kyoto. No metallic fasteners are used, ensuring a silhouette that feels fluid and organic.

Grade-A White Oak with a natural wax finish and hand-woven Danish paper cord seating. Sustainably harvested from FSC-certified forests.
