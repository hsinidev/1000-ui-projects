Certification of Origin expand_more

Every piece in our collection is accompanied by a physical folio of provenance, including dendrochronology reports where applicable and sales history dating back at least three generations.

We collaborate with leading horologists and furniture historians globally. Customers may request an independent verification from a list of approved international houses.

White Glove Delivery expand_more

Transit is managed via climate-controlled vehicles and air-cushioned freight. Installation is performed by technicians trained in the handling of delicate mahogany and gilded finishes.

International Customs expand_more

Heritage Antiques handles all CITES documentation and export licenses for materials such as rosewood or antique ivory, ensuring full legal compliance for your border crossings.

Conservation Philosophy expand_more

We prioritize the "minimal intervention" approach. Our master craftsmen utilize period-accurate glues, shellac finishes, and reclaimed timber to preserve the original integrity of the piece.

Bespoke Restoration expand_more

Private clients may commission restoration for items outside our current collection. This requires a physical inspection at our London or Paris atelier to determine feasibility.
