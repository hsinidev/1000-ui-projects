Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries regarding protocol governance.

Voting power is proportional to the number of governance tokens held in your connected wallet at the time of the proposal snapshot. Some sub-DAOs utilize a quadratic voting model to ensure broader community representation, which is clearly marked on relevant proposal pages.

To submit a Core Proposal, a user must hold at least 10,000 tokens or have that amount delegated to them. A draft period of 72 hours on the community forum is required before moving to the on-chain voting phase.
