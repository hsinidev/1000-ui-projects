PIP-124: Quarterly Treasury Diversification Strategy

Proposed by 0xGovernance_Lead • Ends in 2 days, 4 hours

ACTIVE VOTING

Proposal Description

This proposal outlines the strategic allocation of 15% of the Protocol Treasury into stable liquidity pairs and institutional-grade debt instruments. The goal is to maximize capital efficiency while maintaining a risk-averse profile for the DAO's long-term sustainability.

Strategic Objectives:

  • Establish a 10M USDC reserve for operational contingencies.
  • Deploy 5,000 ETH into Lido and Rocket Pool for diversified staking yield.
  • Allocate remaining funds to high-liquidity AMM pools (Uniswap V3 / Curve).

Quantitative modeling suggests an expected APY of 4.2% with a maximum drawdown of 1.5% under volatile market conditions. For full technical details, please refer to the documentation in the Protocol Wiki.
