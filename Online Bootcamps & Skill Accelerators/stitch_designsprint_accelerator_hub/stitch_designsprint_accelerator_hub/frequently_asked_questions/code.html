Admissions

What is the selection criteria? expand_more
We look for high-end design talent with a demonstrated ability to blend aesthetic rigor with strategic thinking. Candidates are evaluated on their portfolio’s originality, technical execution, and potential for growth within an avant-garde environment.
Do you require a formal degree? expand_more
No. DesignSprint values output over credentials. While many of our cohorts come from elite institutions, we prioritize the "eye" and the "hand"—your visual sensibility and your technical craft.

Curriculum

What software do we focus on? expand_more
We are platform agnostic but workflow intensive. Expect deep dives into Figma for UI, Cinema 4D for spatial thinking, and Framer for high-fidelity interactive prototyping.
Is the program remote-first? expand_more
The core curriculum is delivered via immersive digital sessions, but we maintain physical "Studios" in London, New York, and Berlin for monthly intensives and networking.

Mentorship

Unfiltered Access

Our mentors are active Design Directors and Founders at the world's most innovative agencies. You won't just get feedback; you'll get their intuition.

How often are 1-on-1 sessions? expand_more
Weekly. Each designer is paired with a Lead Mentor for the duration of the sprint to ensure consistent narrative development of their work.
