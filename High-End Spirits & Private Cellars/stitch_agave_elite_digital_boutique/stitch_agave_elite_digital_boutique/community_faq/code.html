FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How is 'Elite' agave different from commercial?

expand_more

Commercial production relies on industrial autoclaves and diffusers that strip the agave of its soul. Our 'Elite' classification requires traditional stone pit roasting (Horno), tahona wheel crushing, and wild yeast fermentation. This architectural approach to flavor ensures the terroir is preserved in every drop.

What defines a 'Masterclass' session?

expand_more

Our Masterclasses are live, sensory-rich digital experiences led by Master Mezcaleros. We ship a curated tasting kit of four 50ml expressions to your door, allowing for a synchronized exploration of distillation chemistry and heritage.

Shipping & Global Logistics

expand_more

We utilize high-precision courier services to ensure artisanal spirits reach you in pristine condition. Due to the rare nature of our collection, we currently offer bespoke shipping to 14 countries including the US, UK, and Mexico.
