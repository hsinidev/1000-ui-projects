Common Enquiries

Provenance & Authenticity

expand_more

Every bottle in our collection undergoes a rigorous multi-stage verification process. We partner with world-renowned master distillers and independent forensic experts to validate physical integrity, liquid chemistry, and historical records. Our certificates of authenticity are legally binding and permanent.

Secure Storage Solutions

expand_less

Assets are stored in our bonded warehouse facilities located in temperature and humidity-controlled vaults. These environments are monitored 24/7 and insured by top-tier global underwriters. For physical redemption, we offer temperature-controlled global logistics services.

Blockchain Verification

expand_more

We utilize private-public hybrid ledger technology to create a digital twin of your asset. This NFT serves as a programmable deed of ownership, recording every transfer, appraisal, and movement of the bottle, ensuring a transparent and immutable chain of custody for future generations.

Investment Valuation

expand_more

Our data analytics platform provides real-time market insights across major auction houses and private sales. We provide quarterly valuation reports for all managed portfolios, helping you make informed decisions about holding or listing your assets.
