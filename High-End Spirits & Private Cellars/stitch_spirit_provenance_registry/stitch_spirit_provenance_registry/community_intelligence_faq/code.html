HOW IS BLOCKCHAIN VERIFICATION EXECUTED?

Each asset in the Spirit-Archive is assigned a non-fungible cryptographic twin. Upon physical inspection by our custodians, a hash is generated encompassing the liquid volume, glass refraction index, and unique serial engraving. This data is permanently written to the ledger, ensuring absolute provenance.

WHAT DEFINES 'LEGAL SOVEREIGNTY' IN THE ARCHIVE?

Collectors maintain direct ownership through private keys. The Spirit-Archive acts only as a zero-knowledge custodian. Legal sovereignty implies that the digital deed is recognized in secondary markets as the sole valid claim to the physical asset stored in our hardened vaults.

IF THE PHYSICAL SEAL IS BREACHED, WHAT HAPPENS?

Our "Security Seals" utilize active fiber-optic monitoring. Any interruption in connectivity immediately marks the asset status as 'Compromised' on the public ledger. The digital record is locked, and a forensic audit is initiated by the Vault Custodian team.
