Finally uncorked the 2018 Château Pontet-Canet. The tannins are remarkably silk-like for its youth. Decanted for 3 hours; dark fruit profile is dominant but the secondary notes of graphite and cedar are already showing through beautifully. Anyone else notice the slight salinity on the finish?
