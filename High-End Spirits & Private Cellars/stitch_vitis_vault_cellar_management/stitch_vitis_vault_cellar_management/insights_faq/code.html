How do I calibrate my smart sensors?

expand_less

Calibration should be performed quarterly to ensure ±0.5°C accuracy. Within the app, navigate to Settings > Hardware > Sensor Sync. Ensure your mobile device is within 3 meters of the main hub. Follow the physical reset sequence detailed in your vault's manual before initiating the digital sync.
