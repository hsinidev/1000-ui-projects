Estate FAQ

The estate is open for professional tours and tastings by appointment only. Monday – Friday: 09:00 - 17:00. Weekend visits are reserved for trade partners.

Our terroir is defined by a 3-meter deep layer of Kimmeridgian limestone mixed with portlandian clay. This creates a unique mineral profile that provides high acidity and structural longevity to our white varietals.

  • 45% Limestone fragments
  • 30% Clay-marl base
  • 25% Sand and silty topsoil

Details on international shipping, temperature-controlled logistics, and distribution partners are available in our trade portal.

We have been certified Ecocert since 2012 and biodynamic by Demeter since 2018.
