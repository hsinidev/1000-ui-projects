Technical Operations FAQ

At 30,000 meters, atmospheric pressure is approximately 1% of sea level. Standard latex meteorological balloons will expand to roughly 3-4x their initial launch diameter. Monitoring internal pressure vs. ambient delta is critical to avoid premature burst.
Internal payload temperatures can drop to -60°C. We utilize passive Phase Change Materials (PCM) combined with active heating resistors triggered when core telemetry falls below -10°C.
Latency typically ranges from 120ms to 450ms depending on ground station proximity and satellite relay hops. The Mission Control UI uses local prediction models to smooth telemetry visualization.
