The Sovereign Equity Restructuring: A Landmark Resolution in Cross-Border Liquidity

Defending a global investment consortium against unprecedented jurisdictional challenges and regulatory shifts in the European financial sector.

The Challenge

In early 2023, our clients faced a multi-layered legal crisis involving over $4.2 billion in distressed assets. The complexity lay not only in the scale of the debt but in a conflicting set of regulatory frameworks across four different European jurisdictions. A failure to reach a consensus threatened the total dissolution of the client's regional operations.
