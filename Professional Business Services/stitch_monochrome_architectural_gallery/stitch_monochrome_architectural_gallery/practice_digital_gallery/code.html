PRACTICE

PHILOSOPHY

Rational organization of space through mathematical rigor.

We believe that architecture is not merely the construction of shelter, but the manifestation of structural logic. Every line drawn in our studio is a calculated response to the environment, human movement, and the inherent properties of matter.

EVOLUTION

Founded in 2008.

Digital Gallery was established in Berlin by E. Koenig as a research-led architectural laboratory. Since its inception, the firm has sought to bridge the gap between digital fabrication and traditional craft.

Over the last decade, we have grown from a boutique design studio into an international practice, delivering projects that challenge the conventional boundaries of material and form.
