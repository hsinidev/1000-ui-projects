ARCHIVAL METHODOLOGY

Our archive is organized through a rationalized categorization system that prioritizes structural logic over stylistic trends. Each entry is documented through three primary lenses: Material Integrity, Spatial Efficiency, and Environmental Integration. This data-driven approach ensures that our history remains a functional tool for future inquiries rather than a static gallery of past works.
